# SOP-3083: Quality Certification and Standards Compliance

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for achieving, maintaining, and leveraging quality certifications and standards compliance to demonstrate quality commitment, meet customer requirements, and enhance competitive positioning in the Florida roofing market.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality certification processes, standards compliance, audit preparation, and certification maintenance across all Florida First Roofing operations and quality management activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Certification**: Formal recognition that the organization meets specific quality standards
- **Standards Compliance**: Adherence to established quality, safety, and industry standards
- **Certification Body**: Independent organization that provides certification services
- **Accreditation**: Formal recognition of a certification body's competence
- **Surveillance Audit**: Regular audit to verify continued compliance
- **Nonconformity**: Failure to meet specified requirements or standards
- **Certification Scope**: Specific activities and processes covered by certification

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Certification program leadership, compliance oversight, audit coordination
- **Certification Coordinator**: Day-to-day compliance management, audit preparation, documentation
- **Process Owners**: Process compliance maintenance, improvement implementation
- **Management Team**: Strategic certification decisions, resource allocation, policy support
- **All Employees**: Standards compliance, quality culture, certification support

## 5. Prerequisites
- Quality management system established and operational
- Standards and certification requirements identified
- Compliance assessment and gap analysis completed
- Resources allocated for certification pursuit
- Employee training on standards requirements completed
