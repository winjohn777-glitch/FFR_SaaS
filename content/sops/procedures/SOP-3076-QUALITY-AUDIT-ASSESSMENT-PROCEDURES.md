# SOP-3076: Quality Audit and Assessment Procedures

## 1. Purpose
Establish systematic procedures for conducting internal quality audits and assessments to verify compliance with quality standards, identify improvement opportunities, and ensure continuous enhancement of quality management systems and processes.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all audit personnel, quality managers, and department heads involved in planning, conducting, and following up on internal quality audits across all operations, processes, and quality management activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Audit**: Systematic examination of quality activities and results to verify compliance
- **Audit Criteria**: Standards, procedures, or requirements against which audit evidence is evaluated
- **Audit Evidence**: Records, statements, or other information relevant to audit criteria
- **Audit Finding**: Result of evaluation of collected audit evidence against audit criteria
- **Corrective Action**: Action taken to eliminate the cause of detected nonconformance

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Audit program oversight, auditor qualification, audit planning
- **Lead Auditor**: Audit planning, team leadership, report preparation, follow-up coordination
- **Quality Auditor**: Audit execution, evidence collection, finding documentation
- **Auditee Department**: Audit cooperation, information provision, corrective action implementation
- **Management Representative**: Audit program review, resource allocation, system improvement

## 5. Prerequisites
- Quality management system fully documented and implemented
- Audit procedures and checklists developed and approved
- Auditor training and qualification completed
- Audit schedule developed and communicated
- Documentation and reporting systems established

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Audit Materials:
- Audit procedures and checklists
- Quality standards and reference documents
- Audit forms and documentation templates
- Sampling plans and statistical tools
- Communication and coordination materials

### Documentation Tools:
- Digital cameras and recording equipment
- Mobile documentation devices (tablets, smartphones)
- Measurement and verification tools
- Evidence collection and organization materials
- Reporting and presentation software

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Audit Program Planning
1. **Annual Audit Schedule Development**
   - Risk-based audit frequency determination
   - Resource allocation and auditor assignment
   - Coordination with operational schedules
   - Customer and regulatory requirement integration
   - Communication to all affected departments

2. **Audit Scope and Objectives Definition**
   - Specific processes and areas to be audited
   - Audit criteria and reference standards
   - Audit objectives and expected outcomes
   - Resource requirements and constraints
   - Success criteria and measurement methods

### 7.2 Audit Preparation and Planning
1. **Pre-Audit Activities**
   - Audit team selection and notification
   - Background information review and analysis
   - Previous audit results and corrective action review
   - Audit plan development and approval
   - Logistics coordination and scheduling

2. **Audit Checklist Development**
   - Process-specific audit questions and criteria
   - Sampling plans and selection criteria
   - Evidence collection requirements
   - Time allocation and schedule planning
   - Communication protocols and procedures

### 7.3 Audit Execution
1. **Opening Meeting**
   - Audit scope and objectives confirmation
   - Audit schedule and logistics review
   - Roles and responsibilities clarification
   - Communication protocols establishment
   - Questions and concerns addressing

2. **Evidence Collection and Evaluation**
   - Document review and analysis
   - Process observation and verification
   - Personnel interviews and discussions
   - Record examination and sampling
   - Performance measurement and analysis

3. **Audit Finding Development**
   - Evidence evaluation against criteria
   - Nonconformance identification and documentation
   - Opportunity for improvement identification
   - Good practice recognition and documentation
   - Finding classification and prioritization

### 7.4 Florida-Specific Audit Considerations
1. **Climate Compliance Verification**
   - Hurricane resistance requirement compliance
   - UV protection and weathering resistance verification
   - Moisture management and mold prevention assessment
   - Thermal performance and energy efficiency compliance
   - Long-term durability requirement verification

2. **Regulatory Compliance Assessment**
   - Florida Building Code compliance verification
   - Environmental regulation compliance assessment
   - Safety regulation compliance verification
   - Permit and inspection requirement compliance
   - Local ordinance and requirement compliance

### 7.5 Audit Finding Documentation
1. **Nonconformance Documentation**
   - Clear description of nonconformance condition
   - Reference to specific requirement or standard
   - Objective evidence supporting the finding
   - Risk assessment and impact evaluation
   - Classification by severity and priority

2. **Improvement Opportunity Documentation**
   - Description of improvement potential
   - Benefits and expected outcomes
   - Resource requirements and implementation considerations
   - Timeline and milestone recommendations
   - Success measurement criteria

### 7.6 Closing Meeting and Reporting
1. **Closing Meeting Conduct**
   - Audit findings presentation and discussion
   - Auditee feedback and clarification
   - Corrective action timeline discussion
   - Next steps and follow-up planning
   - Final questions and concerns addressing

2. **Audit Report Preparation**
   - Comprehensive audit summary and results
   - Detailed finding documentation and evidence
   - Corrective action requirements and timelines
   - Improvement recommendations and suggestions
   - Overall system effectiveness assessment

### 7.7 Corrective Action Follow-up
1. **Corrective Action Plan Review**
   - Root cause analysis adequacy assessment
   - Corrective action appropriateness and effectiveness
   - Implementation timeline and milestone review
   - Resource allocation and responsibility assignment
   - Success measurement criteria verification

2. **Implementation Verification**
   - Corrective action implementation monitoring
   - Effectiveness verification and assessment
   - Additional action requirement identification
   - Closure verification and documentation
   - Preventive action implementation verification

### 7.8 Special Audit Types
1. **Customer Complaint Audits**
   - Rapid response to customer quality concerns
   - Focused investigation of specific issues
   - Root cause analysis and corrective action
   - Customer communication and satisfaction
   - Prevention of similar issues

2. **Supplier Quality Audits**
   - Supplier quality system assessment
   - Process capability and control verification
   - Product quality and conformance assessment
   - Improvement opportunity identification
   - Supplier development planning

### 7.9 Management Review and System Improvement
1. **Audit Program Effectiveness Review**
   - Audit program performance assessment
   - Auditor competency and development needs
   - Audit frequency and scope adequacy
   - Resource allocation and efficiency
   - Customer and stakeholder satisfaction

2. **Quality System Improvement**
   - Trend analysis and pattern identification
   - System-wide improvement opportunities
   - Policy and procedure updates
   - Training and competency development
   - Technology and tool enhancement

### 7.10 External Audit Coordination
1. **Customer Audit Support**
   - Customer audit preparation and coordination
   - Documentation and evidence preparation
   - Personnel availability and training
   - Facility preparation and logistics
   - Follow-up and relationship management

2. **Regulatory Audit Compliance**
   - Regulatory audit preparation and response
   - Compliance verification and documentation
   - Issue resolution and corrective action
   - Ongoing compliance monitoring
   - Relationship management with regulators

## 8. Quality Standards
- Audit coverage: 100% of quality system processes audited annually
- Audit completion: All scheduled audits completed within planned timeframe
- Finding resolution: All audit findings addressed within specified timelines
- Auditor competency: All auditors qualified and trained per requirements
- Report quality: Comprehensive and actionable audit reports
