# SOP-3080: Customer Quality Feedback and Improvement

## 1. Purpose
Establish systematic procedures for collecting, analyzing, and responding to customer quality feedback to drive continuous improvement, enhance customer satisfaction, and maintain long-term customer relationships through superior quality performance.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all customer-facing personnel, quality managers, and project teams involved in collecting customer feedback, analyzing quality issues, and implementing improvements based on customer input.

## 3. Definitions
- **Customer Feedback**: Information provided by customers about quality performance and satisfaction
- **Voice of Customer (VOC)**: Systematic capture and analysis of customer requirements and expectations
- **Customer Quality Loop**: Closed-loop process from feedback collection to improvement implementation
- **Quality Satisfaction Score**: Quantitative measure of customer satisfaction with quality performance
- **Customer Quality Journey**: Customer's experience with quality throughout the service relationship

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Customer Service Manager**: Feedback collection coordination, response management, improvement planning
- **Quality Manager**: Feedback analysis, quality improvement prioritization, system enhancement
- **Project Manager**: Project-specific feedback collection, issue resolution, customer communication
- **Sales Manager**: Customer relationship management, feedback integration, expectation alignment
- **Senior Management**: Strategic improvement direction, resource allocation, policy development

## 5. Prerequisites
- Customer feedback collection systems established
- Analysis tools and methods defined
- Response procedures and timelines established
- Improvement implementation processes ready
- Customer communication protocols developed

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Customer Feedback Collection Framework
1. **Multi-Channel Feedback Collection**
   - **Surveys**: Structured questionnaires at project milestones
   - **Interviews**: Direct customer conversations and discussions
   - **Digital Platforms**: Online portals and mobile applications
   - **Social Media**: Monitoring and engagement on social platforms
   - **Third-Party Reviews**: Monitoring external review platforms

2. **Feedback Collection Timing**
   - **Pre-Project**: Initial expectations and requirements
   - **Mid-Project**: Progress satisfaction and issue identification
   - **Post-Completion**: Overall satisfaction and improvement suggestions
   - **Ongoing Service**: Regular satisfaction monitoring
   - **Issue Resolution**: Feedback on problem resolution effectiveness

### 7.2 Structured Customer Surveys
1. **Project Completion Survey**
   - Overall quality satisfaction rating
   - Specific quality aspect evaluation
   - Communication effectiveness assessment
   - Timeline and schedule satisfaction
   - Recommendation likelihood scoring

2. **Quality-Specific Questions**
   - Material quality and performance satisfaction
   - Installation workmanship evaluation
   - System performance and functionality
   - Durability and longevity expectations
   - Warranty and service satisfaction

### 7.3 Florida Climate Customer Feedback
1. **Performance in Florida Conditions**
   - Hurricane resistance and storm performance
   - Heat and UV resistance satisfaction
   - Moisture management effectiveness
   - Energy efficiency performance
   - Long-term durability in climate conditions

2. **Seasonal Performance Feedback**
   - Summer heat performance evaluation
   - Hurricane season preparation and response
   - Winter weather performance assessment
   - Year-round comfort and efficiency
   - Maintenance ease in climate conditions

### 7.4 Real-Time Feedback Collection
1. **Mobile and Digital Feedback**
   - SMS and text message surveys
   - QR code feedback access
   - Mobile application ratings
   - Email feedback requests
   - Social media engagement

2. **Immediate Issue Response**
   - Real-time complaint capture
   - Immediate acknowledgment and response
   - Rapid issue investigation and resolution
   - Follow-up satisfaction verification
   - Continuous improvement integration

### 7.5 Feedback Analysis and Categorization
1. **Quantitative Analysis**
   - Statistical analysis of satisfaction scores
   - Trend analysis and performance tracking
   - Correlation analysis between factors
   - Benchmark comparison and goal assessment
   - Performance gap identification

2. **Qualitative Analysis**
   - Content analysis of open-ended responses
   - Theme identification and categorization
   - Sentiment analysis and tone assessment
   - Root cause identification from comments
   - Improvement opportunity extraction

### 7.6 Customer Quality Journey Mapping
1. **Touchpoint Quality Assessment**
   - Initial contact and consultation quality
   - Planning and design phase satisfaction
   - Installation process quality experience
   - Completion and handover satisfaction
   - Ongoing service and support quality

2. **Pain Point Identification**
   - Quality issue occurrence patterns
   - Communication breakdown points
   - Expectation misalignment areas
   - Process friction and inefficiency
   - Service gap identification

1. **Individual Customer Response**

## 8. Quality Standards
- Response time: Acknowledge all feedback within 24 hours
- Resolution time: Resolve quality issues within committed timeframes
- Follow-up completion: 100% follow-up on all significant feedback
- Improvement implementation: Document and communicate all improvements
- Customer satisfaction: Continuous improvement in satisfaction scores
