# SOP-3100: Advanced Quality Analytics and Reporting

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for advanced quality data analytics, intelligent reporting, and actionable insights to drive data-driven decision making, performance optimization, and strategic quality management in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality data analysis, report generation, performance monitoring, and strategic quality decision making across all Florida First Roofing operations and quality management activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Analytics**: Statistical analysis and interpretation of quality data for insights
- **Predictive Analytics**: Use of data to predict future quality outcomes and trends
- **Business Intelligence**: Information and insights derived from quality data analysis
- **Data Visualization**: Graphical representation of quality data and analytics
- **Performance Dashboard**: Real-time display of key quality metrics and indicators
- **Advanced Reporting**: Sophisticated reports with analysis, insights, and recommendations
- **Quality Intelligence**: Strategic insights from comprehensive quality data analysis

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Analytics strategy development, insight interpretation, decision making
- **Data Analyst**: Advanced analysis, model development, insight generation
- **Reporting Specialist**: Report design, automation, distribution, user support
- **Business Intelligence Manager**: BI strategy, tool selection, infrastructure management
- **Management Team**: Strategic decisions based on analytics, resource allocation

## 5. Prerequisites
- Quality data collection systems operational
- Analytics tools and platforms implemented
- Data integration and warehousing established
- Reporting infrastructure and templates developed
- User training on analytics tools completed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Analytics Tools:
- Statistical analysis software (R, Python, SAS)
- Business intelligence platforms (Power BI, Tableau, Qlik)
- Data warehousing and ETL tools
- Machine learning and AI platforms
- Cloud-based analytics services

### Reporting Systems:
- Automated reporting platforms
- Dashboard and visualization tools
- Mobile reporting applications
- Web-based reporting portals
- Document generation and distribution systems

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Quality Analytics Framework
1. **Analytics Strategy Development**
   - Business objective alignment and prioritization
   - Key question identification and analysis planning
   - Data source identification and integration
   - Analytics methodology and tool selection
   - Success measurement and value demonstration

2. **Analytics Maturity Development**
   - **Descriptive Analytics**: What happened in quality performance
   - **Diagnostic Analytics**: Why quality events occurred
   - **Predictive Analytics**: What will likely happen to quality
   - **Prescriptive Analytics**: What should be done to optimize quality
   - **Cognitive Analytics**: AI-powered insights and recommendations
