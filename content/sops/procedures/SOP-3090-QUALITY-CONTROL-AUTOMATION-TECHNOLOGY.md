# SOP-3090: Quality Control Automation and Technology

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for implementing and managing automated quality control systems and advanced technologies to enhance accuracy, efficiency, consistency, and real-time monitoring of quality processes in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality control automation, technology implementation, system operation, and data management across all Florida First Roofing quality control and monitoring activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Automation**: Use of technology to perform quality control tasks without human intervention
- **Automated Testing**: Technology-enabled testing and measurement systems
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: Continuous automated monitoring and alerting systems
- **Digital Quality Control**: Integration of digital technologies in quality control processes
- **Smart Sensors**: Intelligent monitoring devices with communication capabilities
- **Automated Reporting**: System-generated quality reports and documentation
- **Predictive Quality**: Use of data analytics to predict quality outcomes

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Automation strategy development, technology oversight, performance monitoring
- **Technology Coordinator**: System implementation, integration, maintenance, user support
- **Automation Specialist**: Automated system operation, optimization, troubleshooting
- **Quality Technician**: System operation, data collection, analysis, reporting
- **IT Manager**: Infrastructure support, security, integration, backup systems

## 5. Prerequisites
- Automation strategy and roadmap developed
- Technology infrastructure and connectivity established
- Budget and resources allocated for automation initiatives
- Staff training on automated systems completed
- Quality standards and acceptance criteria defined
