# SOP-2140: Subcontractor Coordination and Management

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for subcontractor selection, coordination, and management to ensure quality work delivery, safety compliance, schedule adherence, and seamless integration with Florida First Roofing operations while maintaining customer satisfaction.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all project managers, field supervisors, and procurement personnel involved in subcontractor selection, management, and coordination for specialized roofing work, emergency services, and capacity supplementation.

## 3. Definitions
- **Qualified Subcontractor**: Contractor meeting all licensing, insurance, and quality requirements
- **Scope of Work**: Detailed description of subcontractor responsibilities and deliverables
- **Performance Standards**: Quality, safety, and schedule requirements for subcontractor work
- **Integration Management**: Coordination of subcontractor work with overall project execution
- **Compliance Verification**: Ongoing monitoring of subcontractor regulatory and standard compliance

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Project Manager**: Subcontractor selection, contract management, performance oversight
- **Field Supervisor**: Daily coordination, quality control, safety compliance monitoring
- **Procurement Manager**: Vendor qualification, contract negotiation, payment processing
- **Safety Coordinator**: Subcontractor safety compliance, training verification, incident management
- **Quality Manager**: Work quality verification, standard compliance, customer satisfaction

## 5. Prerequisites
- Subcontractor qualification and vetting completed
- Detailed scope of work and performance standards defined
- Insurance and licensing verification current
- Safety training and compliance requirements established
- Integration with project schedule completed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Management Materials:

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Subcontractor Qualification and Selection
1. **Initial Qualification Requirements**
   - Verify Florida contractor license and specialization
   - Confirm general liability and workers' compensation insurance
   - Check references from recent similar projects
   - Verify financial stability and bonding capacity
   - Assess technical capabilities and equipment

2. **Performance Evaluation**
   - Review past project performance and quality
   - Assess safety record and compliance history
   - Evaluate customer satisfaction and references
   - Check Better Business Bureau and complaint records
   - Verify ongoing training and certification programs

3. **Bid Evaluation Process**
   - Request detailed proposals with scope breakdown
   - Compare pricing against market standards
   - Evaluate proposed schedules and resource allocation
   - Assess value-added services and capabilities
   - Select based on best value, not lowest price

### 7.2 Contract Development and Management
1. **Scope of Work Definition**
   - Define specific work requirements and deliverables
   - Establish quality standards and acceptance criteria
   - Specify materials and equipment requirements
   - Set schedule milestones and completion dates
   - Include integration requirements with other trades

2. **Contract Terms and Conditions**
   - Establish payment terms and schedule
   - Define change order procedures and pricing
   - Include safety requirements and compliance standards
   - Specify insurance and bonding requirements
   - Set penalties for schedule delays or quality issues

### 7.3 Safety Integration and Compliance
1. **Safety Requirements Integration**
   - Require attendance at project safety meetings
   - Verify subcontractor safety training and certifications
   - Establish site-specific safety procedures
   - Require compliance with Florida First Roofing safety standards
   - Implement joint safety inspections and monitoring

2. **Insurance and Risk Management**
   - Verify coverage limits meet project requirements
   - Ensure Florida First Roofing is named as additional insured
   - Monitor certificate renewals and coverage continuity
   - Document any incidents or claims immediately
   - Coordinate with insurance providers for claims management

### 7.4 Florida-Specific Coordination Requirements
1. **Hurricane and Storm Preparedness**
   - Include storm response procedures in contracts
   - Establish material and equipment securing requirements
   - Plan for rapid mobilization after storm events
   - Coordinate emergency response and repair capabilities
   - Document storm preparedness and response procedures

2. **Local Code and Permit Compliance**
   - Verify knowledge of Florida Building Code requirements
   - Ensure compliance with local permit requirements
   - Coordinate inspections with local building officials
   - Maintain current knowledge of code updates
   - Document compliance throughout project execution

3. **Environmental Considerations**
   - Ensure compliance with environmental regulations
   - Coordinate stormwater management requirements
   - Plan for proper disposal of construction waste
   - Implement noise control measures for residential areas
   - Monitor and document environmental compliance

### 7.5 Work Coordination and Integration
1. **Schedule Coordination**
   - Integrate subcontractor schedules with overall project timeline
   - Coordinate material deliveries and staging
   - Plan for equipment sharing and site access
   - Establish communication protocols for schedule changes
   - Monitor progress against established milestones

2. **Quality Integration**
   - Establish quality control checkpoints and inspections
   - Coordinate with Florida First Roofing quality standards
   - Implement joint quality inspections and testing
   - Document quality compliance throughout work execution
   - Address quality issues immediately and systematically

### 7.6 Communication and Reporting
1. **Regular Communication Protocols**
   - Establish daily communication and coordination meetings
   - Implement progress reporting requirements
   - Use standardized communication channels and systems
   - Document all communications and decisions
   - Escalate issues to appropriate management levels

2. **Progress Monitoring and Reporting**
   - Track progress against established milestones
   - Monitor quality and safety performance continuously
   - Document issues and resolutions promptly
   - Provide regular updates to project stakeholders
   - Maintain comprehensive project documentation

### 7.7 Performance Management
1. **Ongoing Performance Evaluation**
   - Monitor quality, schedule, and safety performance
   - Conduct regular performance reviews and feedback
   - Address performance issues promptly and systematically
   - Document performance trends and improvements
   - Recognize outstanding performance and achievements

2. **Issue Resolution Process**
   - Establish clear procedures for issue identification
   - Implement rapid response for critical issues
   - Document root causes and corrective actions
   - Monitor implementation of corrective measures
   - Prevent recurrence through process improvements

### 7.8 Payment and Financial Management
1. **Payment Processing**
   - Verify work completion against payment schedule
   - Conduct quality inspections before payment authorization
   - Process payments according to contract terms
   - Document all payment authorizations and approvals
   - Maintain accurate financial records and reporting

2. **Change Order Management**
   - Establish clear procedures for scope changes
   - Require written authorization for all changes
   - Negotiate fair pricing for additional work
   - Document impacts on schedule and other trades
   - Update contracts and documentation promptly

### 7.9 Customer Interface Management
1. **Customer Communication**
   - Brief customers on subcontractor roles and responsibilities
   - Coordinate customer interactions and communications
   - Address customer concerns about subcontractor work
   - Maintain Florida First Roofing as primary customer contact
   - Ensure consistent customer service standards

2. **Quality Assurance for Customer**
   - Verify all work meets customer expectations
   - Conduct final inspections with customer participation
   - Address any customer concerns immediately
   - Ensure customer satisfaction before project completion
   - Provide warranty and service information to customer

### 7.10 Project Completion and Evaluation
1. **Final Performance Evaluation**
   - Conduct comprehensive evaluation of subcontractor performance
   - Document lessons learned and improvement opportunities
   - Update subcontractor qualification status
   - Provide feedback for future project improvements
   - Recognize outstanding performance and partnerships

2. **Documentation and Records Management**
   - Compile complete project documentation
   - Maintain records for warranty and legal requirements
   - Update subcontractor database with performance information
   - Archive project files according to company standards
   - Prepare final project reports and analysis

## 8. Quality Standards
- Subcontractor qualification: 100% compliance with qualification requirements
- Work quality: All work must meet Florida First Roofing quality standards
- Safety performance: Zero preventable safety incidents
- Schedule adherence: Complete work within established timelines
- Customer satisfaction: Maintain high customer satisfaction ratings
