# SOP-0020: Roof Replacement Estimate and Assessment

## Purpose
Standardize the roof replacement estimate process to ensure accuracy, completeness, and consistency across all assessments.

## Scope
Applies to all certified roof inspectors, estimators, and sales personnel conducting roof replacement evaluations.

## Pre-Assessment Preparation

### Property Research (30 minutes before arrival)
- Review county property records and building permits
- Check satellite imagery for roof configuration
- Research neighborhood and property history
- Verify property access and safety considerations
- Review previous service history if available

### Equipment and Documentation Preparation
- Calibrated measuring tools and laser meters
- Digital camera with timestamp capability
- Tablet/laptop with estimation software
- Safety equipment (harnesses, hard hats, non-slip footwear)
- Sample materials and color charts
- Insurance and contractor licensing documentation

## On-Site Assessment Process

### Initial Property Evaluation (15 minutes)
1. **Exterior Walkthrough**
   - Overall property condition assessment
   - Access route identification
   - Safety hazard identification
   - Surrounding property protection needs

2. **Customer Consultation**
   - Review customer concerns and objectives
   - Discuss insurance claim status
   - Explain assessment process and timeline
   - Address initial questions and concerns

### Detailed Roof Inspection (45-90 minutes)

#### Structural Assessment
- **Load-bearing capacity evaluation**
  - Rafter and truss condition inspection
  - Decking integrity assessment
  - Support beam evaluation
  - Foundation settlement impact analysis

- **Drainage system evaluation**
  - Gutter condition and capacity
  - Downspout placement and function
  - Roof pitch and water flow analysis
  - Ponding water identification

#### Material Condition Analysis
- **Shingle/roofing material assessment**
  - Age and wear pattern documentation
  - Granule loss and UV damage evaluation
  - Mechanical damage identification
  - Missing or loose material documentation

- **Underlayment and barrier evaluation**
  - Existing underlayment condition
  - Moisture barrier integrity
  - Ice and water shield requirements
  - Ventilation system assessment

#### Photographic Documentation
- **Comprehensive photo sequence:**
  - Four-corner overview shots
  - Close-up damage documentation
  - Interior ceiling inspection (if accessible)
  - Attic space evaluation
  - Surrounding property conditions

### Measurement and Calculation

#### Roof Area Calculation
- **Primary roof surfaces**
  - Main roof sections with pitch calculations
  - Dormer and addition measurements
  - Complex geometry documentation
  - Total square footage calculation

- **Linear measurements**
  - Ridge line measurements
  - Eave and rake edge calculations
  - Valley and hip measurements
  - Flashing and trim requirements

#### Material Quantity Calculations
- **Roofing materials**
  - Primary roofing material (with 10% waste factor)
  - Underlayment requirements
  - Starter strip and ridge cap quantities
  - Flashing and trim materials

- **Accessory materials**
  - Fastener quantities by zone
  - Ventilation component requirements
  - Gutter and downspout measurements
  - Disposal and cleanup materials

### Cost Estimation Process

#### Material Costs
- **Current pricing verification**
  - Supplier pricing confirmation (within 48 hours)
  - Quality tier selection based on customer needs
  - Warranty considerations in material selection
  - Availability and lead time factors

- **Labor calculations**
  - Base installation labor by complexity
  - Removal and disposal labor
  - Specialty work requirements
  - Weather and season adjustments

#### Additional Considerations
- **Permit and inspection fees**
  - County-specific permit requirements
  - Required inspection schedules
  - Engineering requirements if applicable
  - HOA approval processes

- **Insurance and protection costs**
  - Liability insurance allocation
  - Property protection measures
  - Temporary weather protection
  - Cleanup and restoration services

### Estimate Presentation

#### Written Estimate Components
1. **Executive Summary**
   - Project scope overview
   - Total investment range
   - Timeline and milestones
   - Warranty information

2. **Detailed Breakdown**
   - Material specifications and quantities
   - Labor components and timing
   - Permit and fee itemization
   - Optional upgrades and alternatives

3. **Terms and Conditions**
   - Payment schedule and methods
   - Change order procedures
   - Weather contingency plans
   - Completion guarantees

#### Customer Presentation Process
- **In-person review (45 minutes)**
  - Detailed walkthrough of assessment findings
  - Explanation of recommended solutions
  - Discussion of material options and upgrades
  - Timeline and logistics review

- **Question and objection handling**
  - Technical clarification provision
  - Alternative solution presentation
  - Financing option discussion
  - Insurance claim coordination explanation

### Follow-up and Documentation

#### Immediate Follow-up (Same day)
- Email estimate delivery within 2 hours
- Text confirmation of estimate receipt
- Schedule follow-up consultation within 24-48 hours
- Provide reference and portfolio materials

#### Documentation Requirements
- **Complete assessment file creation**
  - Photo documentation with timestamps
  - Measurement records and calculations
  - Material specifications and sources
  - Customer communication log

- **Quality assurance review**
  - Senior estimator review for projects over $15,000
  - Accuracy verification of measurements and calculations
  - Pricing validation against current market rates
  - Customer satisfaction survey scheduling

### Special Circumstances

#### Insurance Claim Projects
- **Adjuster coordination**
  - Schedule joint inspection when possible
  - Provide detailed damage documentation
  - Submit scope of work for approval
  - Handle supplemental claim processes

#### Emergency Replacements
- **Expedited process (2-hour completion)**
  - Focus on essential measurements and safety
  - Temporary protection priority
  - Simplified material selection
  - Fast-track permit applications

#### Historical or Specialty Properties
- **Enhanced documentation requirements**
  - Architectural compliance verification
  - Specialty material sourcing
  - Extended timeline considerations
  - Additional permit and approval processes

### Quality Standards and Metrics

#### Accuracy Requirements
- Measurement accuracy: ±2% tolerance
- Cost estimation accuracy: ±5% tolerance
- Timeline estimation accuracy: ±10% tolerance
- Material quantity accuracy: ±3% tolerance

#### Performance Metrics
- Assessment completion time: 90 minutes maximum
- Estimate delivery time: 2 hours maximum
- Customer satisfaction score: 4.5/5 minimum
- Estimate conversion rate: Target varies by season

#### Continuous Improvement
- Monthly performance review
- Customer feedback analysis
- Cost accuracy trending
- Process efficiency optimization
