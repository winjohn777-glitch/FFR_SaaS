# SOP-3086: Quality Vendor and Partner Management

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for managing quality relationships with vendors, suppliers, subcontractors, and partners to ensure consistent quality standards, performance excellence, and mutual success in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in vendor selection, partner management, supplier quality assurance, and relationship development across all Florida First Roofing vendor and partner interactions.

## 3. Definitions
- **Vendor**: External organization providing materials, equipment, or services
- **Strategic Partner**: Long-term collaborative relationship for mutual benefit
- **Supplier Quality Management**: Systematic approach to ensure supplier quality performance
- **Vendor Assessment**: Evaluation of vendor capabilities and performance
- **Partnership Agreement**: Formal agreement defining partnership terms and expectations
- **Vendor Scorecard**: Performance measurement and tracking tool
- **Supplier Development**: Activities to improve supplier capabilities and performance

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Procurement Manager**: Vendor selection, contract management, relationship oversight
- **Quality Manager**: Quality standards development, assessment, performance monitoring
- **Project Manager**: Vendor coordination, performance evaluation, issue resolution
- **Technical Manager**: Technical specification development, capability assessment
- **Senior Management**: Strategic partnership decisions, policy development, relationship governance

## 5. Prerequisites
- Vendor management strategy and framework established
- Quality standards and requirements defined
- Assessment criteria and methods developed
- Contract templates and agreements prepared
- Performance monitoring systems operational

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Vendor Management Strategy
1. **Strategic Approach**
   - Partnership-focused relationship development
   - Quality-driven vendor selection and management
   - Long-term collaboration and mutual success
   - Performance-based relationship management
   - Continuous improvement and development

2. **Vendor Categorization**
   - **Strategic Partners**: Critical long-term relationships
   - **Preferred Vendors**: High-performing regular suppliers
   - **Qualified Vendors**: Approved vendors meeting standards
   - **Potential Vendors**: Candidates under evaluation
   - **Restricted Vendors**: Vendors with performance issues

### 7.2 Florida Roofing Vendor Requirements
1. **Climate-Specific Requirements**
   - Hurricane-resistant material capabilities
   - UV-resistant product offerings
   - Moisture management expertise
   - Thermal expansion accommodation
   - Corrosion-resistant solutions

2. **Regional Compliance Requirements**
   - Florida building code knowledge and compliance
   - Local licensing and certification requirements
   - Insurance and bonding requirements
   - Emergency response capabilities
   - Hurricane season preparation and support

### 7.3 Vendor Identification and Sourcing

   - **Financial Stability**: Financial health, creditworthiness

2. **Assessment Methods**

   - On-site audits and facility visits
   - Reference interviews and verification
   - Sample testing and evaluation

## 8. Quality Standards
- Vendor approval: 100% of vendors meet quality approval criteria
- Performance monitoring: Monthly performance review and scorecard updates
- Issue resolution: 95% of issues resolved within agreed timeframes
- Vendor satisfaction: 85% minimum vendor satisfaction with relationship
- Quality compliance: 100% vendor compliance with quality standards
