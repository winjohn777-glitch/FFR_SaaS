# SOP-2150: Project Completion and Customer Handover

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for project completion and customer handover to ensure all work is completed to satisfaction, all documentation is provided, customer expectations are exceeded, and long-term customer relationships are established for future business development.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all project managers, field supervisors, customer service representatives, and quality assurance personnel involved in finalizing roofing projects and transitioning customers to ongoing service relationships.

## 3. Definitions
- **Project Completion**: Final verification that all contracted work has been completed satisfactorily
- **Customer Handover**: Formal transfer of completed project to customer with documentation
- **Final Walkthrough**: Comprehensive inspection with customer to verify satisfaction
- **Warranty Package**: Complete documentation of warranties and service agreements
- **Customer Transition**: Movement from construction to service relationship

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Project Manager**: Overall completion oversight, customer satisfaction, documentation coordination
- **Quality Assurance Manager**: Final quality verification, inspection coordination, standard compliance
- **Customer Service Manager**: Customer communication, satisfaction verification, relationship transition
- **Field Supervisor**: Work completion verification, final cleanup, customer walkthrough
- **Service Manager**: Warranty setup, service program enrollment, ongoing relationship establishment

## 5. Prerequisites
- All construction work completed and internally inspected
- Final cleanup and site restoration completed
- All permits and inspections obtained and approved
- Warranty documentation prepared and ready
- Customer communication scheduled and confirmed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Completion Documentation:
- Final inspection checklists and forms
- Warranty documentation packages
- Maintenance guides and instructions
- Service program information
- Customer satisfaction surveys

### Presentation Materials:
- Project completion portfolio
- Before and after photography
- Quality verification documentation
- Maintenance and care instructions
- Emergency contact information

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Pre-Completion Verification
1. **Internal Quality Review**
   - Conduct comprehensive internal inspection
   - Verify all work completed per contract specifications
   - Check all systems for proper operation
   - Review all quality control documentation
   - Address any deficiencies before customer walkthrough

2. **Documentation Preparation**
   - Compile all warranty documentation
   - Prepare maintenance guides and instructions
   - Organize permit and inspection records
   - Prepare project completion portfolio
   - Assemble emergency contact information

### 7.2 Final Inspection and Testing
1. **Comprehensive System Testing**
   - Test all roof drainage systems for proper operation
   - Verify integrity of all waterproofing systems
   - Check all flashings and penetration seals
   - Test equipment attachments and supports
   - Document all testing results

2. **Performance Verification**
   - Verify energy performance meets specifications
   - Check all installed accessories and components
   - Test emergency systems and access features
   - Verify compliance with all applicable codes
   - Document performance compliance

### 7.3 Customer Walkthrough Process
1. **Walkthrough Preparation**
   - Schedule convenient time for customer
   - Prepare comprehensive inspection checklist
   - Gather all relevant documentation
   - Plan systematic walkthrough route
   - Coordinate with key project personnel

2. **Detailed Customer Inspection**
   - Conduct systematic inspection with customer
   - Explain all work completed and systems installed
   - Demonstrate proper operation of all components
   - Address any customer questions or concerns
   - Document customer feedback and satisfaction

3. **Issue Resolution**
   - Identify any customer concerns immediately
   - Develop action plans for any deficiencies
   - Schedule completion of any required work
   - Follow up to ensure customer satisfaction
   - Document all issue resolutions

### 7.4 Documentation Handover
1. **Warranty Package Presentation**
   - Explain all warranty terms and coverage
   - Provide manufacturer warranty documentation
   - Present workmanship warranty information
   - Explain warranty claim procedures
   - Register warranties as required

2. **Maintenance Documentation**
   - Provide comprehensive maintenance guides
   - Explain routine maintenance requirements
   - Schedule first maintenance inspection
   - Provide emergency contact information
   - Demonstrate basic inspection techniques

### 7.5 Florida-Specific Completion Requirements
1. **Hurricane Preparedness Information**
   - Provide hurricane preparation guidelines
   - Explain emergency response procedures
   - Identify potential vulnerabilities
   - Provide emergency repair contact information
   - Document storm response capabilities

2. **Climate Care Instructions**
   - Explain UV protection and maintenance
   - Provide guidelines for dealing with heavy rains
   - Discuss ventilation and moisture control
   - Explain seasonal maintenance requirements
   - Provide heat management recommendations

3. **Code Compliance Documentation**
   - Provide copies of all permits and approvals
   - Document code compliance verification
   - Explain ongoing compliance requirements
   - Provide contact information for inspectors
   - Document energy efficiency compliance

### 7.6 Service Program Enrollment
1. **Service Program Presentation**
   - Explain available service program options
   - Discuss benefits of ongoing maintenance
   - Provide service program pricing and terms
   - Schedule initial service visit
   - Enroll customer in selected programs

2. **Relationship Transition**
   - Introduce service team and contacts
   - Establish ongoing communication preferences
   - Set up automatic service reminders
   - Provide service portal access information
   - Plan for regular relationship check-ins

### 7.7 Customer Satisfaction and Feedback
1. **Satisfaction Survey**
   - Conduct comprehensive satisfaction survey
   - Address any areas of concern immediately
   - Document customer feedback and suggestions
   - Follow up on any improvement opportunities
   - Request testimonials and references

2. **Feedback Integration**
   - Analyze customer feedback for improvement opportunities
   - Share feedback with project team
   - Update procedures based on customer input
   - Recognize outstanding team performance
   - Plan for process improvements

### 7.8 Administrative Completion
1. **Financial Completion**
   - Process final billing and payment
   - Close out all project accounts
   - Document final project costs
   - Process any warranty retentions
   - Complete financial project documentation

2. **Legal and Regulatory Completion**
   - File completion documentation with authorities
   - Release any bonds or guarantees
   - Complete lien waivers and releases
   - Archive legal documentation
   - Update compliance records

### 7.9 Project Documentation and Archive
1. **Comprehensive Documentation Package**
   - Compile complete project file
   - Include all permits, inspections, and approvals
   - Document all changes and modifications
   - Include warranty and service information
   - Prepare project completion report

2. **Documentation Archive**
   - Archive project files per company standards
   - Ensure easy retrieval for warranty service
   - Maintain customer access to documentation
   - Create backup copies of critical documents
   - Update customer database with project information

### 7.10 Future Business Development
1. **Referral Program Presentation**
   - Explain customer referral programs and benefits
   - Provide referral materials and information
   - Request permission for case study development
   - Schedule follow-up relationship meetings
   - Plan for future business opportunities

2. **Long-term Relationship Planning**
   - Establish regular communication schedule
   - Plan for future maintenance and service needs
   - Identify potential future projects
   - Maintain ongoing customer satisfaction monitoring
   - Document relationship development opportunities

## 8. Quality Standards
- Completion accuracy: 100% of contracted work completed satisfactorily
- Customer satisfaction: Minimum 4.8/5 rating at completion
- Documentation completeness: All required documentation provided
- Issue resolution: All customer concerns addressed within 24 hours
- Warranty compliance: 100% compliance with warranty requirements
