# SOP-4073: Customer Communication Automation

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for automated customer communication systems to enhance efficiency, consistency, personalization, and timeliness of customer interactions while maintaining personal touch and relationship quality in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in customer communication, marketing automation, customer service, and communication system management across all Florida First Roofing customer interaction channels.

## 3. Definitions
- **Communication Automation**: Technology-enabled automated customer messaging and interaction
- **Marketing Automation**: Automated marketing campaigns and customer engagement
- **Triggered Communication**: Automated messages based on customer actions or events
- **Drip Campaign**: Series of automated messages sent over time
- **Personalization**: Customization of messages based on customer data and preferences
- **Multi-Channel Communication**: Coordinated messaging across multiple communication channels
- **Customer Journey Automation**: Automated communication based on customer lifecycle stage

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Marketing Manager**: Automation strategy, campaign development, performance monitoring
- **Customer Service Manager**: Service automation, response management, customer satisfaction
- **Communications Coordinator**: Content creation, message scheduling, channel management
- **IT Manager**: System integration, technical support, automation platform maintenance
- **CRM Administrator**: Data integration, customer segmentation, automation rules

## 5. Prerequisites
- Marketing automation platform implemented and configured
- Customer data and segmentation completed
- Communication templates and content developed
- Integration with CRM and other systems established
- Staff training on automation tools completed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Automation Platforms:
- Marketing automation software (HubSpot, Mailchimp, Pardot)

### Integration Tools:
