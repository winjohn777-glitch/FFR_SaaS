# SOP-0030: Customer Communication Standards

## Purpose
Establish consistent, professional, and effective communication standards throughout the customer relationship lifecycle.

## Scope
Applies to all personnel with direct or indirect customer contact including sales, service, administrative, and field teams.

## Communication Principles

### Core Values
- **Transparency**: Honest, clear information sharing
- **Timeliness**: Prompt response and proactive updates
- **Professionalism**: Courteous, knowledgeable interaction
- **Empathy**: Understanding and addressing customer concerns
- **Accountability**: Taking ownership of issues and solutions

### Response Time Standards
- **Phone calls**: Answer within 3 rings, return calls within 2 hours
- **Emails**: Acknowledge within 1 hour, respond within 4 hours
- **Text messages**: Respond within 30 minutes during business hours
- **Emergency communications**: Immediate response required

## Initial Customer Contact

### First Impression Standards
- **Professional greeting**: Company name, personal name, offer to help
- **Active listening**: Allow customer to fully explain their situation
- **Information gathering**: Use standardized intake forms
- **Expectation setting**: Clear timelines and next steps
- **Follow-up confirmation**: Summarize discussion and agreements

### Information Collection Protocol
- **Customer identification**: Name, address, contact preferences
- **Property details**: Type, age, access considerations
- **Service needs**: Specific issues, urgency level, budget range
- **Decision makers**: Who needs to be involved in decisions
- **Scheduling preferences**: Availability, timing constraints

## Ongoing Project Communication

### Project Milestone Updates
- **Pre-work confirmation**: 24-48 hours before start date
- **Daily progress updates**: End-of-day status for active projects
- **Milestone completion**: Immediate notification with photos
- **Issue identification**: Immediate communication with solution options
- **Project completion**: Final walkthrough scheduling within 24 hours

### Communication Channels
- **Primary method**: Customer's preferred communication channel
- **Backup methods**: Alternative channels for urgent communications
- **Documentation**: All communications logged in CRM system
- **File sharing**: Secure photo and document sharing platforms

## Problem Resolution Communication

### Issue Escalation Process
1. **Initial response**: Acknowledge issue within 30 minutes
2. **Investigation**: Gather facts and assess scope within 2 hours
3. **Solution development**: Create action plan within 4 hours
4. **Customer consultation**: Present options and get approval
5. **Resolution implementation**: Execute approved solution
6. **Follow-up verification**: Confirm satisfaction within 24 hours

### Difficult Conversation Management
- **Stay calm and professional**: Maintain composure regardless of customer emotion
- **Listen actively**: Allow customer to fully express concerns
- **Empathize**: Acknowledge frustration and validate concerns
- **Focus on solutions**: Redirect conversation toward resolution
- **Document thoroughly**: Record all details for future reference

## Digital Communication Standards

### Email Communication
- **Subject lines**: Clear, descriptive, include project number
- **Professional formatting**: Company signature, contact information
- **Attachment guidelines**: Photos, documents, estimates clearly labeled
- **Response acknowledgment**: Confirm receipt of important emails
- **Grammar and spelling**: Professional proofreading required

### Text Messaging Protocol
- **Business hours only**: 7 AM - 7 PM unless emergency
- **Professional tone**: Avoid casual language or abbreviations
- **Clear identification**: Include company name and sender name
- **Photo sharing**: High-quality, relevant images only
- **Opt-out respect**: Honor customer communication preferences

### Social Media and Online Reviews
- **Response timing**: Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
- **Professional tone**: Courteous regardless of review sentiment
- **Privacy protection**: Never share customer-specific information
- **Issue resolution**: Direct complex issues to private communication
- **Follow-up**: Encourage updated reviews after issue resolution

## Cultural and Language Considerations

### Multilingual Communication
- **Spanish language support**: Bilingual staff available for all interactions
- **Written translations**: Key documents available in Spanish
- **Interpreter services**: Professional interpreters for complex discussions
- **Cultural sensitivity**: Respect for cultural preferences and customs

### Accessibility Accommodations
- **Hearing impaired**: TTY services and written communication options
- **Visual impaired**: Large print documents and verbal descriptions
- **Mobility limitations**: Flexible meeting locations and times
- **Technology barriers**: Multiple communication channel options

## Customer Education and Information Sharing

### Roofing Knowledge Sharing
- **Process explanation**: Clear description of work procedures
- **Material education**: Benefits and limitations of different options
- **Maintenance guidance**: Proper care and inspection recommendations
- **Warranty information**: Coverage details and claim procedures

### Project Timeline Communication
- **Realistic scheduling**: Account for weather and permit delays
- **Milestone mapping**: Clear project phases and completion dates
- **Contingency planning**: Communication about potential delays
- **Progress tracking**: Regular updates on project advancement

## Documentation and Quality Assurance

### Communication Logging
- **CRM system entries**: All customer interactions documented
- **Timestamp recording**: Date, time, and duration of communications
- **Issue tracking**: Problems and resolutions recorded
- **Satisfaction surveys**: Regular feedback collection and analysis

### Quality Monitoring
- **Call recording**: Random call reviews for quality assurance
- **Email monitoring**: Sample review of written communications
- **Customer feedback**: Regular satisfaction surveys and interviews
- **Team coaching**: Training based on quality review findings

### Performance Metrics
- **Response time compliance**: Target 95% within standards
- **Customer satisfaction scores**: Target 4.5/5 average rating
- **Issue resolution time**: Target 90% resolved within 24 hours
- **Communication clarity**: Target 95% customer understanding confirmation

## Training and Development

### Initial Communication Training
- **Customer service fundamentals**: 16-hour initial training
- **Company communication standards**: 4-hour company-specific training
- **Technology platforms**: CRM and communication tool training
- **Role-playing exercises**: Difficult scenario practice sessions

### Ongoing Development
- **Monthly team meetings**: Communication best practice sharing
- **Quarterly skills assessment**: Individual coaching and development
- **Annual training updates**: Industry trends and technology updates
- **Cross-training**: Exposure to different communication scenarios

## Special Situation Protocols

### Insurance Claim Communications
- **Adjuster coordination**: Professional liaison and documentation
- **Customer advocacy**: Clear explanation of claim process and rights
- **Documentation support**: Comprehensive damage and repair documentation
- **Timeline coordination**: Managing expectations around insurance timelines

### Emergency Situation Communication
- **Immediate notification**: All relevant parties informed within 15 minutes
- **Calm reassurance**: Professional composure during crisis situations
- **Clear instructions**: Step-by-step guidance for customer safety
- **Frequent updates**: Progress reports every 30 minutes during emergencies

### Legal and Compliance Situations
- **Privacy protection**: Strict confidentiality maintenance
- **Documentation requirements**: Detailed records for legal purposes
- **Professional boundaries**: Appropriate limitation of discussions
- **Escalation procedures**: Immediate supervisor and legal team notification
