# SOP-2099: Roof Warranty and Service Programs

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for managing roof warranty programs, service agreements, and ongoing customer relationships to ensure warranty compliance, customer satisfaction, and long-term business development through superior service delivery.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all service managers, warranty coordinators, field technicians, and customer service representatives involved in warranty administration, service program delivery, and ongoing customer relationship management.

## 3. Definitions
- **Material Warranty**: Manufacturer's guarantee of material performance and quality
- **Workmanship Warranty**: Company guarantee of installation quality and methods
- **Extended Service Agreement**: Optional ongoing maintenance and service coverage
- **Warranty Registration**: Process of registering warranties with manufacturers
- **Service Program**: Structured ongoing maintenance and inspection programs

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Warranty Manager**: Overall warranty program administration and compliance
- **Service Coordinator**: Service program delivery, scheduling, customer communication
- **Field Service Technician**: Warranty inspections, service delivery, repair work
- **Customer Service Representative**: Customer communication, scheduling, issue resolution
- **Quality Assurance Manager**: Warranty compliance verification, service quality oversight

## 5. Prerequisites
- Completed roof installation with all documentation
- Warranty registration with manufacturers completed
- Customer service program selection and enrollment
- Service team training and certification current
- Customer contact information and preferences updated

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Service Materials:
- Warranty-compliant repair materials
- Manufacturer-approved replacement components
- Service maintenance supplies
- Documentation and inspection forms
- Customer communication materials

### Service Equipment:
- Inspection tools and testing equipment
- Basic repair tools and equipment
- Documentation equipment (cameras, tablets)
- Safety equipment for service work
- Transportation and logistics equipment

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Warranty Registration and Documentation
1. **Initial Warranty Setup**
   - Register all material warranties with manufacturers
   - Document workmanship warranty terms and coverage
   - Create customer warranty file with all documentation
   - Provide warranty documentation to customer
   - Enter warranty information in management system

2. **Warranty Compliance Verification**
   - Verify installation meets all warranty requirements
   - Document compliance with manufacturer specifications
   - Complete required manufacturer inspections
   - Obtain warranty certificates and documentation
   - File warranty compliance documentation

### 7.2 Service Program Options and Enrollment
1. **Service Program Design**
   - **Basic Warranty Service**: Warranty repairs and emergency response
   - **Standard Maintenance Program**: Annual inspections and basic maintenance
   - **Comprehensive Care Program**: Bi-annual service with priority response
   - **Premium Service Agreement**: Quarterly service with extended warranties

2. **Customer Enrollment Process**
   - Explain service program options and benefits
   - Assess customer needs and recommend appropriate program
   - Complete service agreement documentation
   - Schedule initial service and inspection
   - Set up billing and payment processing

### 7.3 Routine Service Delivery
1. **Scheduled Maintenance Visits**
   - Contact customer to schedule service appointments
   - Conduct comprehensive roof inspections
   - Perform preventive maintenance activities
   - Document all service activities and findings
   - Provide customer with service report and recommendations

2. **Service Documentation**
   - Complete detailed service checklists
   - Take photos of roof conditions and any issues
   - Document maintenance activities performed
   - Note any warranty or service issues identified
   - Update customer service history records

### 7.4 Warranty Claim Processing
1. **Claim Initiation**
   - Receive and document customer warranty claims
   - Assess claim validity and warranty coverage
   - Schedule inspection to evaluate claim
   - Document claim details and customer concerns
   - Initiate claim processing workflow

2. **Claim Investigation**
   - Conduct thorough inspection of claimed defect
   - Determine if issue is covered under warranty
   - Identify root cause of problem
   - Document findings with photos and measurements
   - Prepare claim evaluation report

3. **Claim Resolution**
   - Coordinate repairs with manufacturer if material defect
   - Perform workmanship warranty repairs promptly
   - Use warranty-compliant materials and methods
   - Document repair work and completion
   - Follow up with customer satisfaction verification

### 7.5 Florida Climate Warranty Considerations
1. **Hurricane and Storm Damage**
   - Assess storm damage versus normal wear
   - Coordinate with insurance adjusters as needed
   - Distinguish between warranty and insurance claims
   - Document storm-related damage thoroughly
   - Provide emergency temporary repairs when covered

2. **UV and Heat Damage Assessment**
   - Evaluate UV degradation versus normal aging
   - Assess thermal cycling effects on roof systems
   - Document climate-related wear patterns
   - Determine warranty coverage for climate effects
   - Recommend preventive measures for UV protection

3. **Moisture and Humidity Issues**
   - Assess moisture-related problems and coverage
   - Evaluate condensation versus leak issues
   - Check ventilation system adequacy
   - Document moisture problems and sources
   - Recommend solutions for humidity control

### 7.6 Customer Communication and Satisfaction
1. **Proactive Communication**
   - Send warranty renewal and service reminders
   - Provide seasonal maintenance recommendations
   - Communicate about new services and programs
   - Share energy efficiency and performance tips
   - Maintain regular customer contact schedule

2. **Issue Resolution Process**
   - Respond to customer concerns within 24 hours
   - Investigate issues thoroughly and professionally
   - Communicate findings and resolution plans clearly
   - Follow up to ensure customer satisfaction
   - Document all customer interactions

### 7.7 Service Program Management
1. **Program Performance Monitoring**
   - Track service delivery metrics and performance
   - Monitor customer satisfaction and feedback
   - Assess warranty claim trends and patterns
   - Review service program profitability
   - Adjust programs based on performance data

2. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Analyze service delivery effectiveness
   - Identify opportunities for program enhancement
   - Update service procedures based on experience
   - Train service team on new techniques and requirements
   - Implement customer feedback improvements

### 7.8 Technology Integration
1. **Service Management Systems**
   - Use integrated software for service scheduling
   - Maintain comprehensive customer databases
   - Track warranty and service history electronically
   - Automate service reminders and communications
   - Generate service reports and analytics

2. **Mobile Technology**
   - Use mobile devices for field service documentation
   - Implement mobile inspection and reporting apps
   - Provide real-time customer updates
   - Use GPS tracking for service efficiency
   - Enable electronic signatures and approvals

## 8. Quality Standards
- Warranty compliance: 100% compliance with manufacturer requirements
- Service delivery: All scheduled services completed on time
- Customer satisfaction: Minimum 4.5/5 rating for service programs
- Response time: 24-hour response to warranty claims
- Documentation: Complete and accurate service records
