# SOP-3130: Quality Control Data Management

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for collection, storage, analysis, protection, and utilization of quality control data to ensure data integrity, accessibility, compliance, and strategic value in Florida roofing quality management operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality data collection, management, analysis, reporting, and protection across all Florida First Roofing quality control and data management activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Data**: Information collected during quality control and management activities
- **Data Integrity**: Accuracy, completeness, and reliability of data throughout its lifecycle
- **Data Governance**: Framework for managing data availability, usability, integrity, and security
- **Data Analytics**: Process of examining data to extract insights and support decisions
- **Data Retention**: Policies and procedures for data storage duration and disposal
- **Data Security**: Protection of data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure
- **Master Data**: Core data entities critical to quality operations

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Data Manager**: Data governance, architecture, security, compliance oversight
- **Quality Manager**: Quality data requirements, analysis, reporting, decision support
- **Database Administrator**: Database design, maintenance, backup, performance optimization
- **Data Analyst**: Data analysis, reporting, visualization, insight generation
- **IT Manager**: Infrastructure, security, integration, technical support

## 5. Prerequisites
- Data management strategy and governance framework established
- Data storage and management systems implemented
- Data security and access control systems operational
- Data backup and recovery procedures established
- Personnel training on data management completed
