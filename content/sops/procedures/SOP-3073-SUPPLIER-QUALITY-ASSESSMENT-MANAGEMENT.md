# SOP-3073: Supplier Quality Assessment and Management

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for supplier quality assessment, qualification, and ongoing management to ensure consistent delivery of high-quality materials and services while maintaining cost-effectiveness and reliability in Florida's demanding roofing environment.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all procurement personnel, quality managers, and project managers involved in supplier selection, evaluation, and ongoing relationship management for materials, equipment, and services used in roofing operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Qualified Supplier**: Vendor meeting all quality, delivery, and performance requirements
- **Supplier Audit**: Systematic evaluation of supplier quality systems and capabilities
- **Performance Scorecard**: Standardized measurement of supplier performance metrics
- **Supplier Development**: Activities to improve supplier capabilities and performance
- **Supply Chain Quality**: Quality management throughout the entire supply chain

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Procurement Manager**: Supplier selection, contract negotiation, relationship management
- **Quality Manager**: Quality assessment, audit coordination, performance evaluation
- **Supplier Quality Engineer**: Technical evaluation, capability assessment, improvement planning
- **Project Manager**: Supplier performance feedback, quality requirements communication
- **Receiving Inspector**: Material quality verification, nonconformance reporting

## 5. Prerequisites
- Supplier quality standards and requirements defined
- Evaluation criteria and scoring systems established
- Audit procedures and checklists developed
- Performance measurement systems operational
- Supplier database and tracking systems ready

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Assessment Materials:

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Supplier Qualification Framework
1. **Initial Qualification Requirements**
   - Business license and registration verification
   - Financial stability and credit worthiness assessment
   - Insurance coverage and bonding capacity verification
   - Technical capability and equipment evaluation
   - Quality system and process assessment

2. **Quality System Evaluation**
   - Quality management system documentation review
   - Process control and improvement capabilities
   - Inspection and testing procedures and equipment
   - Corrective and preventive action systems
   - Training and competency management programs

### 7.2 Supplier Assessment Process
1. **Documentation Review**
   - Quality manual and procedure documentation
   - Certifications and accreditations (ISO, industry standards)
   - Material specifications and test certificates
   - Previous customer references and testimonials
   - Compliance with regulatory requirements

2. **Technical Capability Assessment**
   - Manufacturing processes and quality control
   - Testing and inspection capabilities
   - Research and development capabilities
   - Continuous improvement programs
   - Innovation and technology advancement

### 7.3 Supplier Audit Procedures
1. **Audit Planning and Preparation**
   - Audit scope and objectives definition
   - Audit team selection and preparation
   - Audit schedule and logistics coordination
   - Pre-audit documentation review
   - Customer notification and preparation

2. **On-Site Audit Execution**
   - Opening meeting and scope confirmation
   - Systematic evaluation of quality systems
   - Process observation and verification
   - Document and record review
   - Interview with key personnel

3. **Audit Reporting and Follow-up**
   - Audit findings documentation and analysis
   - Corrective action plan development
   - Implementation timeline and milestones
   - Follow-up verification and assessment
   - Continuous improvement recommendations

### 7.4 Florida-Specific Supplier Requirements
1. **Hurricane Resistance Capabilities**
   - Impact testing and certification capabilities
   - Wind uplift testing and verification
   - Hurricane-rated product development
   - Emergency response and supply capabilities
   - Storm damage assessment and recovery support

2. **Climate Performance Requirements**
   - UV resistance testing and certification
   - Heat aging and thermal cycling capabilities
   - Moisture resistance and mold prevention
   - Corrosion resistance in salt air environments
   - Long-term durability in extreme conditions

3. **Local Market Knowledge**
   - Understanding of Florida Building Code requirements
   - Local permit and inspection process familiarity
   - Regional market conditions and pricing
   - Local distribution and logistics capabilities
   - Emergency response and storm recovery experience

### 7.5 Performance Measurement and Monitoring
1. **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
   - **Quality Performance**: Defect rates, returns, nonconformances
   - **Delivery Performance**: On-time delivery, lead times, flexibility
   - **Service Performance**: Responsiveness, communication, support
   - **Cost Performance**: Pricing competitiveness, cost reductions, value
   - **Innovation Performance**: New product development, improvements

2. **Performance Scorecard System**
   - Monthly performance measurement and scoring
   - Trend analysis and performance tracking
   - Benchmark comparison and industry standards
   - Performance improvement goals and targets
   - Recognition and reward programs for excellence

### 7.6 Supplier Development Programs
1. **Capability Development**
   - Training and education programs
   - Technical assistance and support
   - Quality system improvement initiatives
   - Process optimization and efficiency improvement
   - Technology transfer and innovation sharing

2. **Relationship Building**
   - Regular business reviews and planning
   - Joint improvement projects and initiatives
   - Communication and collaboration enhancement
   - Long-term partnership development
   - Strategic alliance and integration opportunities

### 7.7 Risk Management and Mitigation
1. **Supply Chain Risk Assessment**
   - Financial stability and business continuity risk
   - Geographic and natural disaster risk exposure
   - Single source and dependency risk analysis
   - Quality and performance risk evaluation
   - Regulatory and compliance risk assessment

2. **Risk Mitigation Strategies**
   - Multiple supplier qualification and backup plans
   - Emergency response and contingency planning
   - Supply chain diversification and flexibility
   - Insurance and financial protection measures
   - Regular risk monitoring and reassessment

### 7.8 Quality Agreement Management
1. **Quality Agreement Development**
   - Quality requirements and specifications definition
   - Inspection and testing requirements
   - Nonconformance handling and resolution procedures
   - Continuous improvement expectations and commitments
   - Performance measurement and reporting requirements

2. **Agreement Implementation and Monitoring**
   - Implementation planning and coordination
   - Regular performance review and assessment
   - Agreement updates and modifications
   - Compliance monitoring and verification
   - Dispute resolution and problem solving

### 7.9 Supplier Communication and Collaboration
1. **Regular Communication Protocols**
   - Monthly performance reviews and feedback
   - Quarterly business planning meetings
   - Annual strategic planning and goal setting
   - Emergency communication and response procedures
   - Continuous feedback and improvement suggestions

2. **Collaboration Platforms**
   - Supplier portal and information sharing
   - Joint quality improvement projects
   - Technology sharing and development
   - Training and education collaboration
   - Industry best practice sharing

### 7.10 Supplier Exit and Transition Management
1. **Performance-Based Exit Criteria**
   - Persistent quality performance failures
   - Delivery and service reliability issues
   - Financial instability or business failure
   - Regulatory compliance failures
   - Strategic business changes and realignment

2. **Transition Management Process**
   - Alternative supplier identification and qualification
   - Transition planning and timeline development
   - Customer communication and expectation management
   - Quality continuity during transition
   - Relationship closure and final assessment

## 8. Quality Standards
- Supplier qualification: 100% compliance with qualification requirements
- Performance measurement: Monthly scorecards for all active suppliers
- Quality compliance: Zero tolerance for non-conforming materials
- Audit completion: Annual audits for critical suppliers
- Supplier development: Continuous improvement in supplier performance

## 9. Safety Requirements
