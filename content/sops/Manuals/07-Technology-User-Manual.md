## 1. INTRODUCTION AND OVERVIEW

### Purpose and Scope
This Technology User Manual provides comprehensive guidance for all employees on the proper use, management, and maintenance of technology systems at Florida First Roofing LLC. This manual covers system management procedures, Learning Management System (LMS) integration, performance measurement tools, and compliance requirements.

### Technology Infrastructure Overview
Our technology ecosystem includes:
- **Document Management Systems** (SharePoint, Network Drives, Cloud Storage)
- **Learning Management System** (Training and Certification Platform)
- **Performance Dashboard Systems** (KPI Monitoring and Analytics)
- **Customer Relationship Management** (CRM) System
- **Enterprise Resource Planning** (ERP) System
- **Communication and Collaboration Tools**
- **Data Backup and Recovery Systems**
- **Security and Access Control Systems**

### User Responsibilities
All employees are responsible for:
- Following established technology policies and procedures
- Maintaining security of login credentials and access
- Reporting technical issues and security concerns promptly
- Completing required technology training and certifications
- Using technology resources efficiently and appropriately
- Adhering to data protection and privacy requirements

## 7. USER ACCESS AND SECURITY

### 7.1 Account Management
**Password Requirements:**
- **Complexity:** Minimum 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols
- **Uniqueness:** Unique passwords for each system and application
- **Rotation:** Regular password changes as required by policy
- **Protection:** Never share passwords or write them down insecurely
- **Multi-Factor Authentication:** Use MFA where available and required

**Account Security:**
1. **Login Procedures:** Follow secure login practices and protocols
2. **Session Management:** Properly log out and lock workstations when away
3. **Suspicious Activity:** Report unusual account activity immediately
4. **Access Reviews:** Participate in periodic access reviews and updates
5. **Account Changes:** Report role changes that affect system access needs

### 7.2 Data Protection and Privacy
**Privacy Requirements:**
- **Customer Data:** Protect customer information according to privacy policies
- **Employee Data:** Maintain confidentiality of personnel information
- **Financial Data:** Secure financial and accounting information
- **Intellectual Property:** Protect company proprietary information and trade secrets
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Follow industry-specific data protection requirements

**Protection Measures:**
1. **Access Control:** Only access data required for job functions
2. **Data Sharing:** Follow approved procedures for sharing sensitive information
3. **External Communications:** Use secure methods for external data transmission
4. **Device Security:** Secure all devices containing company data
5. **Incident Response:** Follow breach notification and response procedures
