## 3. LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (LMS) INTEGRATION {#lms-integration}

### 3.1 LMS Platform Overview
**Reference:** SOP-0041 - LMS Platform Setup and Configuration

#### System Capabilities
The Learning Management System (LMS) provides a comprehensive digital learning environment that supports all aspects of training delivery, tracking, and management for Florida First Roofing LLC.

**Core Features:**
- **Course Catalog**: Centralized library of all available training courses
- **Learning Paths**: Structured learning sequences tailored to specific job roles
- **Progress Tracking**: Real-time monitoring of individual and team training progress
- **Assessment Tools**: Integrated testing and competency evaluation capabilities
- **Certification Management**: Automated tracking of certifications and renewal requirements
- **Reporting Analytics**: Comprehensive reporting on training effectiveness and compliance
- **Mobile Access**: Training accessibility from any device, anywhere

#### User Access and Navigation
**Getting Started:**
1. **Login Process**: Access the LMS through the company portal or direct URL
2. **Dashboard Overview**: Personal learning dashboard displaying assigned courses and progress
