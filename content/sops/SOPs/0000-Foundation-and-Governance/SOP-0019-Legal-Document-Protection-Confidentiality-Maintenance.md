## BLOCK 3: DEFINITIONS - Key Terms and Concepts

### 3.1 Legal Privilege Terms
**Attorney-Client Privilege:** Legal protection for confidential communications between attorney and client
**Work Product Doctrine:** Protection for materials prepared in anticipation of litigation
**Joint Defense Privilege:** Shared privilege protection among parties with common legal interests
**Confidentiality Agreement:** Legal contract restricting disclosure of sensitive information

### 3.2 Protection Classification Levels
**Level 1 - Attorney-Client Privileged:** Highest protection requiring legal counsel approval for access
**Level 2 - Work Product Protected:** Legal work requiring litigation team access controls
**Level 3 - Confidential Business Information:** Proprietary information requiring business justification
**Level 4 - Sensitive Personal Information:** Customer/employee data requiring privacy compliance

## BLOCK 6: STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURES

### 6.1 Document Classification and Protection
**Step 1: Privilege and Confidentiality Assessment**
- Review document content for privilege and confidentiality indicators
- Consult with legal counsel for privilege determination when uncertain
- Apply appropriate classification markings and access restrictions
- Document rationale for classification decisions and approvals
- Implement technical controls for protection level requirements

**Step 2: Access Control and Monitoring**
- Configure role-based access controls for classified documents
- Implement approval workflows for privileged material access
- Monitor and log all access attempts and document activities
- Generate regular access reports for legal counsel review
- Investigate and respond to unauthorized access attempts immediately

### 6.2 Legal Hold and Litigation Support
**Step 1: Legal Hold Implementation**
- Receive and analyze legal hold notices from counsel
- Identify and preserve all relevant documents and communications
- Suspend normal retention schedules for hold-covered materials
- Notify all relevant personnel of legal hold obligations
- Monitor compliance and report hold status to legal counsel

**Step 2: Discovery Preparation and Production**
- Coordinate with legal counsel for discovery scope determination
- Conduct privilege review for all documents subject to production
- Prepare privilege logs and confidentiality designations
- Implement secure production procedures and tracking
- Maintain chain of custody and authentication documentation
