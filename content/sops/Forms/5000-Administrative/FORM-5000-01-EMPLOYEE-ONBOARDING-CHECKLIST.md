## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

**Purpose:** This comprehensive checklist ensures all new employees receive complete onboarding, orientation, training, and integration into the company culture and operations.

**Who Completes This Form:** HR Manager, Supervisor, and New Employee (collaborative completion)
**When to Complete:** Begin on employee's first day, complete within first 30 days
**Distribution:** HR Department, Employee File, Supervisor, Safety Manager
**Digital System:** Upload to HR management system and employee portal immediately
