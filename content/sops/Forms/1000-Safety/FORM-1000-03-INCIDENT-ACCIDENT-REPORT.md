## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

**CRITICAL:** This form must be completed immediately following any workplace incident, accident, near-miss, or safety-related occurrence. Report all incidents regardless of severity.

**Who Completes This Form:** Site Supervisor, Safety Manager, or Witness
**When to Complete:** Immediately after incident (within 1 hour maximum)
**Distribution:** Safety Manager (immediate), HR Department, Insurance Company (if applicable)
**Digital System:** Upload to incident management system within 2 hours

**EMERGENCY:** For serious injuries or fatalities, call 911 immediately, then notify Safety Manager at (407) 123-4567 ext. 105
