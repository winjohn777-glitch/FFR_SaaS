## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

**Purpose:** This comprehensive checklist ensures systematic preparation for hurricane events to protect personnel, equipment, facilities, and maintain business continuity during and after severe weather events.

**Who Completes This Form:** Emergency Response Manager and Operations Manager (collaborative completion)
**When to Complete:** 72-48 hours before projected hurricane arrival
**Distribution:** All Department Managers, Safety Manager, Operations Staff
**Digital System:** Upload to emergency management system and distribute via emergency communication channels

**CRITICAL:** This checklist is activated when a Hurricane Watch or Warning is issued for Central Florida
