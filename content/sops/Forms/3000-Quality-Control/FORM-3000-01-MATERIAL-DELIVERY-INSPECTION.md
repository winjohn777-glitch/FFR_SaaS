## INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

**Purpose:** This form must be completed for all material deliveries to ensure quality, accuracy, and compliance with project specifications before materials are accepted and stored.

**Who Completes This Form:** Site Supervisor, Project Manager, or designated Quality Inspector
**When to Complete:** Upon delivery of all materials before unloading and acceptance
**Distribution:** Original to Project File, Copy to Quality Control Manager, Copy to Accounting
**Digital System:** Upload to inventory management system within 4 hours of completion
