# SOP-8010: Contractor License Maintenance and Renewal

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for maintaining and renewing all contractor licenses required for Florida First Roofing LLC operations, ensuring continuous legal authorization to operate, regulatory compliance, and protection of business interests throughout Florida.

### Scope
- State contractor license maintenance and renewal procedures
- Local and municipal license tracking and compliance
- Continuing education requirement fulfillment and documentation
- Insurance and bonding requirement maintenance
- License status monitoring and proactive renewal management
- Regulatory compliance verification and audit preparation

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 License Status Monitoring
```
Monitoring Schedule and Requirements:
□ Monthly license status verification through DBPR database
□ Quarterly renewal deadline review and notification
□ Semi-annual continuing education credit assessment
□ Annual comprehensive compliance audit and verification
□ Real-time alert monitoring for regulatory changes

Key Information Tracking:
- License number and classification details
- Expiration dates and renewal deadlines
- Continuing education credit status and requirements
- Insurance policy status and coverage verification
- Disciplinary action or violation history monitoring
```

### 5.2 Continuing Education Management
```
Education Requirement Tracking:
- Certified Roofing Contractor: 14 hours every 2 years
- Business and Finance: 1 hour minimum required
- Worker's Compensation: 1 hour minimum required
- Advanced technical and safety training credits
- Professional development and industry update training

Education Planning Process:
1. Credit requirement assessment and planning
2. Course selection and registration coordination
3. Training completion and certificate collection
4. Credit reporting and verification with DBPR
5. Record maintenance and audit preparation
```

### 5.3 License Renewal Process
```
Renewal Timeline (60 days before expiration):
Day 1-30: Initial renewal preparation and documentation
Day 31-45: Application submission and fee payment
Day 46-60: Verification and follow-up coordination
Post-renewal: Record updating and confirmation

Required Documentation:
□ Completed renewal application and forms
□ Continuing education certificates and credit verification
□ Current insurance policy certificates and coverage proof
□ Financial responsibility and bonding documentation
□ Any required exam results or professional development
```

### 5.4 Local License Coordination
```
Municipal Requirements:
- Business tax receipt renewal and payment
- Local contractor registration and certification
- Building department coordination and approval
- Occupational license maintenance and compliance
- Special permit privileges and authorization

Tracking and Management:
- Local license inventory and status monitoring
- Renewal deadline tracking and coordination
- Fee payment and documentation management
- Municipal relationship maintenance and coordination
- Compliance verification and audit preparation
```
