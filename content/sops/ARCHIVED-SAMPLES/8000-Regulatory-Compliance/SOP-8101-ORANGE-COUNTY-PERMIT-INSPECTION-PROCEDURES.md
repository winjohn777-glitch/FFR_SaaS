## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes specific protocols for permit applications, inspection procedures, and regulatory compliance for all roofing and construction projects within Orange County, Florida, ensuring adherence to local building codes and municipal requirements.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all projects, personnel, subcontractors, and operations conducted within Orange County boundaries, including Orlando metropolitan area, unincorporated county areas, and all municipal jurisdictions within Orange County.

## Definitions

- **Orange County Building Services**: Local building department responsible for permit issuance and inspections
- **Municipal Jurisdiction**: Cities within Orange County with separate building departments
- **Orange County Growth Management**: Department overseeing development and land use compliance
- **Orange County Utilities**: Department managing water, wastewater, and solid waste services
- **Tourist Development Zone**: Areas with special requirements due to tourism industry impact

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC maintains comprehensive compliance with Orange County building codes, municipal regulations, environmental standards, and growth management requirements while providing superior roofing services throughout the county.

### 8.2 Permit Application and Processing

#### 8.2.1 Orange County Permit Types and Requirements
1. **Residential Roofing Permits**
   - **Single-Family Residential**
     - Standard roofing permit: $85 base fee + $3 per $1,000 valuation
     - Expedited review available: Additional $150 fee
     - Online application system: ePermits Orange County
     - Same-day issuance for standard re-roofing projects

   - **Multi-Family and Townhome Projects**
     - Commercial permit classification for buildings over 3 units
     - Fire department review for buildings over 2 stories
     - Accessibility compliance verification
     - Parking and site impact assessment

2. **Commercial and Industrial Permits**
   - **Plan Review Requirements**
     - Structural engineer seal required for buildings over 5,000 sq ft
     - Fire marshal review and approval
     - Site plan coordination with existing approvals
     - Environmental impact assessment for large projects

   - **Special Use and Tourist Area Projects**
     - Tourism development zone compliance review
     - Traffic impact assessment for major projects
     - Aesthetic compatibility review in scenic corridors
     - Hospitality industry coordination requirements

#### 8.2.2 Application Submission and Processing
1. **Required Documentation**
   - **Standard Permit Package**
     - Completed Orange County permit application
     - Property survey and site plan
     - Roofing plans and specifications
     - Structural calculations (when required)
     - Material specifications and testing reports

   - **Supplemental Requirements**
     - Flood zone compliance documentation
     - Environmental impact assessment (large projects)
     - Historic preservation review (designated areas)
     - Tree preservation plan (when applicable)

2. **Processing Timeline and Procedures**
   - **Standard Review Process**
     - Initial submittal review: 1-2 business days
     - Plan review completion: 3-5 business days for residential, 7-10 for commercial
     - Permit issuance: Same day upon approval
     - Electronic permit delivery via email

   - **Expedited Review Options**
     - Fast-track residential permits: 24-hour review
     - Priority commercial review: 3-5 business days
     - Emergency repair permits: Same-day issuance
     - After-hours emergency permit authorization

### 8.3 Inspection Procedures and Requirements

#### 8.3.1 Inspection Scheduling and Coordination
1. **Orange County Inspection System**
   - **Online Scheduling Platform**
     - Orange County ePermits inspection portal
     - 24/7 online scheduling availability
     - Mobile app for field scheduling and updates
     - Automated reminder and confirmation system

   - **Inspection Request Requirements**
     - Minimum 24-hour advance notice for routine inspections
     - Same-day inspection available for emergency repairs
     - Weather contingency rescheduling protocol
     - Inspector direct contact for coordination

2. **Inspection Types and Timing**
   - **Residential Re-roofing Inspections**
     - Deck inspection (if deck replacement required)
     - Rough inspection (flashing, penetrations, and ventilation)
     - Final inspection (completion and material verification)

   - **Commercial Roofing Inspections**
     - Foundation/structural inspection (new construction)
     - Rough roofing inspection (deck, flashing, and penetrations)
     - Insulation and membrane inspection
     - Final inspection with warranty documentation

#### 8.3.2 Inspector Requirements and Standards
1. **Inspection Preparation Protocol**
   - **Site Safety and Access**
     - OSHA-compliant safety equipment and procedures
     - Clear access routes to all roof areas
     - Proper ladder or lift access for inspector
     - Site organization and cleanliness standards

   - **Documentation and Reference Materials**
     - Approved permit and plans on-site
     - Material certification and compliance documentation
     - Installation instructions and manufacturer specifications
     - Inspection checklist and previous inspection records

2. **Common Inspection Focus Areas**
   - **Structural Compliance**: Deck condition, fastener patterns, load path continuity
   - **Weatherproofing**: Flashing installation, sealant application, penetration sealing
   - **Code Compliance**: Material specifications, installation methods, safety features
   - **Energy Efficiency**: Insulation requirements, ventilation standards, cool roof compliance
