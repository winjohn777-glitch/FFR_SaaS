## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes specific protocols for compliance with Brevard County building codes, permit requirements, inspection procedures, and regulatory standards for all roofing and construction projects within Brevard County, Florida.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all projects, personnel, subcontractors, and operations conducted within Brevard County boundaries, including but not limited to residential and commercial roofing installations, repairs, and emergency services.

## Definitions

- **Brevard County Building Code**: Local building code based on Florida Building Code with county-specific amendments
- **High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)**: Coastal areas subject to enhanced wind load requirements
- **Coastal Construction Control Line (CCCL)**: Regulated boundary requiring additional permitting
- **Space Coast Environmental Standards**: Local environmental protection requirements
- **Historic District Regulations**: Special requirements for properties in designated historic areas

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC maintains full compliance with all Brevard County building codes, environmental regulations, historic preservation requirements, and coastal construction standards while delivering exceptional roofing services.

## Regulatory Framework

### 8.1 Brevard County Specific Requirements

#### 8.1.1 Building Code Adoptions and Amendments
1. **Florida Building Code - Brevard County Amendments**
   - Local amendment adoption date: January 1, 2024
   - Wind load requirements: Enhanced for coastal zones
   - Energy efficiency standards: IECC 2021 with local modifications
   - Accessibility requirements: ADA compliance plus local accessibility standards
   - Green building incentives: LEED and Florida Green Building certification programs

2. **Coastal Construction Requirements**
   - **Coastal High Hazard Area (CHHA) Compliance**
     - Enhanced foundation and structural requirements
     - Flood-resistant construction standards
     - Breakaway wall construction specifications
     - Utility elevation and protection requirements
   - **Coastal Construction Control Line (CCCL) Permits**
     - DEP coastal construction permit coordination
     - Environmental impact assessment requirements
     - Sea turtle nesting season construction restrictions
     - Dune and vegetation protection compliance

#### 8.1.2 Permit Requirements and Procedures
1. **Residential Roofing Permits**
   - **Minor Repairs (under $1,000)**
     - Homeowner permit option available
     - 24-hour online permit issuance
     - Same-day inspection scheduling
     - Streamlined approval process

   - **Major Repairs and Replacements**
     - Professional contractor permit required
     - Structural analysis requirement for homes built before 1994
     - Energy compliance documentation (2024 IECC standards)
     - Wind mitigation upgrade incentive programs

2. **Commercial Roofing Permits**
   - **Plan Review Requirements**
     - Structural engineer seal required for buildings over 3,000 sq ft
     - Fire department accessibility review
     - Stormwater management plan approval
     - Environmental impact assessment for projects over 10,000 sq ft

   - **Special Use Permits**
     - Airport overlay zone compliance (near Melbourne International Airport)
     - Kennedy Space Center operational coordination
     - Military installation buffer zone requirements
     - Historic district commission approval (when applicable)

### 8.2 Inspection and Approval Process

#### 8.2.1 Inspection Scheduling and Procedures
1. **Standard Inspection Timeline**
   - **Permit Issuance**: 1-3 business days for standard projects
   - **First Inspection Request**: Minimum 24-hour advance notice
   - **Inspection Scheduling**: Online system preferred, phone backup available
   - **Inspection Completion**: Same-day approval for compliant work
   - **Re-inspection Requirements**: 48-hour minimum between inspections

2. **Required Inspections by Project Type**
   - **Residential Re-roofing**
     - Deck inspection (if replacing decking)
     - Rough inspection (flashing and penetrations)
     - Final inspection (completion and cleanup)

   - **Commercial Roofing**
     - Foundation/structural inspection (if applicable)
     - Rough roofing inspection
     - Insulation and membrane installation
     - Final inspection with warranty documentation

#### 8.2.2 Inspector Requirements and Expectations
1. **Preparation for Inspections**
   - **Site Accessibility**
     - Clear access to all roof areas
     - Proper safety equipment and fall protection in place
     - Ladder access or lift availability for inspector
     - Work area lighting for interior inspections

   - **Documentation Requirements**
     - Approved plans and permit documents on-site
     - Material certification and testing reports
     - Manufacturer installation instructions
     - Wind mitigation upgrade documentation (if applicable)

2. **Common Inspection Points**
   - **Structural Integrity**: Deck condition, support adequacy, fastener patterns
   - **Flashing and Penetrations**: Proper installation, waterproofing, code compliance
   - **Ventilation Systems**: Intake and exhaust balance, energy code compliance
   - **Fire Safety**: Access routes, emergency egress, fire-resistant materials

### 8.3 Environmental and Coastal Compliance

#### 8.3.1 Environmental Protection Standards
1. **Sea Turtle Protection (May 1 - October 31)**
   - **Lighting Restrictions**
     - No nighttime exterior work during nesting season
     - Shielded lighting requirements for extended projects
     - Window tinting and light reduction measures
     - Temporary lighting plan approval for emergency repairs

   - **Beach Access and Protection**
     - Dune vegetation protection protocols
     - Beach access route designation and protection
     - Sand displacement and erosion prevention
     - Waste and debris management during coastal projects

2. **Manatee Protection Zones**
   - **Waterway Access Restrictions**
     - No motor vessel use in designated manatee zones
     - Alternative material delivery methods for waterfront properties
     - Noise reduction requirements near sensitive habitats
     - Coordination with Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission

#### 8.3.2 Stormwater Management Compliance
1. **Brevard County Stormwater Ordinance**
   - **Project Size Thresholds**
     - Projects over 1 acre require stormwater permit
     - Drainage plan approval for significant roof area changes
     - Retention pond and filtration system maintenance
     - Low Impact Development (LID) technique implementation

2. **Water Quality Protection**
   - **Indian River Lagoon Protection**
     - Fertilizer and chemical runoff prevention
     - Erosion and sediment control during construction
     - Material storage and containment requirements
     - Spill prevention and response protocols

### 8.4 Historic District and Preservation Requirements

#### 8.4.1 Historic District Compliance
1. **Brevard County Historic Preservation Office Coordination**
   - **Historic District Identification**
     - Cocoa Village Historic District
     - Eau Gallie Arts District
     - Melbourne Downtown Historic District
     - Titusville Downtown Historic District

   - **Certificate of Appropriateness (COA) Requirements**
     - Design review for visible roof changes
     - Material compatibility and authenticity requirements
     - Color and texture approval processes
     - Architectural style preservation standards

2. **National Register Properties**
   - **Section 106 Review Process**
     - Federal project coordination requirements
     - State Historic Preservation Office consultation
     - Archaeological survey requirements (when applicable)
     - Mitigation measures for adverse effects

#### 8.4.2 Preservation Standards and Guidelines
1. **Secretary of Interior Standards Compliance**
   - **Material Selection Criteria**
     - Period-appropriate material selection when possible
     - Compatible replacement material approval
     - Reversibility of modifications preference
     - Documentation of changes and modifications

   - **Installation Technique Requirements**
     - Traditional fastening methods preference
     - Minimal structural alteration requirements
     - Preservation of character-defining features
     - Professional historic preservation consultation

### 8.5 Special Zoning and Overlay Districts

#### 8.5.1 Airport Overlay Zone Compliance
1. **Melbourne International Airport Influence**
   - **Height Restrictions**
     - FAA notification requirements for structures over 200 feet
     - Approach zone height limitations
     - Crane and equipment height coordination
     - Temporary structure notification protocols

   - **Noise Compatibility Standards**
     - Noise reduction construction requirements
     - Insulation and sound attenuation measures
     - HVAC system noise control requirements
     - Window and door specification standards

2. **Patrick Space Force Base Coordination**
   - **Mission Compatibility Review**
     - Security clearance requirements for certain areas
     - Launch operation schedule coordination
     - Communication system interference prevention
     - Emergency evacuation plan integration

#### 8.5.2 Floodplain and Coastal High Hazard Area Requirements
1. **FEMA Flood Zone Compliance**
   - **Elevation Requirements**
     - Base Flood Elevation (BFE) compliance verification
     - Lowest floor elevation certification
     - Flood-resistant material requirements below BFE
     - Mechanical and electrical equipment elevation

   - **Floodproofing Standards**
     - Wet floodproofing vs. dry floodproofing determination
     - Hydrostatic pressure relief requirements
     - Flood damage resistant material selection
     - Emergency flood response plan development

2. **Coastal Construction Standards**
   - **Design Wind Speed Requirements**
     - Ultimate design wind speed: 180 mph (coastal areas)
     - Component and cladding design requirements
     - Impact resistance standards for openings
     - Continuous load path verification

## Implementation Procedures

### 8.6 Project Planning and Permit Application

#### 8.6.1 Pre-Application Research and Planning
1. **Property Research and Analysis**
   - **Zoning and Overlay District Verification**
     - Online zoning map consultation
     - Property deed and survey review
     - Easement and restriction identification
     - Homeowners association requirement review

   - **Environmental Constraint Assessment**
     - Wetland and conservation area identification
     - Sea turtle nesting zone verification
     - Manatee protection zone mapping
     - Historic district and landmark status confirmation

2. **Code Compliance Planning**
   - **Building Code Analysis**
     - Current code edition verification
     - Local amendment identification and application
     - Structural requirement assessment
     - Energy efficiency standard compliance planning

   - **Permit Requirement Determination**
     - Permit type and scope identification
     - Required inspections and timeline planning
     - Professional seal requirements assessment
     - Special approval processes identification

#### 8.6.2 Permit Application and Submission
1. **Application Preparation**
   - **Required Documentation Assembly**
     - Completed permit application forms
     - Property survey and site plan
     - Architectural drawings and specifications
     - Structural calculations (when required)
     - Environmental impact documentation

   - **Professional Seal Requirements**
     - Registered engineer seal for structural work
     - Architect seal for design elements
     - Surveyor seal for property boundary verification
     - Environmental consultant reports (when applicable)

2. **Submission and Review Process**
   - **Online Submission System Utilization**
     - Brevard County ePermit portal access
     - Digital document upload and management
     - Application status tracking and monitoring
     - Payment processing and fee calculation

   - **Plan Review Coordination**
     - Building department review timeline tracking
     - Review comment response and revision submission
     - Expedited review option evaluation
     - Approval notification and permit issuance

### 8.7 Construction and Inspection Management

#### 8.7.1 Construction Phase Compliance
1. **Permit Display and Documentation**
   - **On-Site Requirements**
     - Permit placard display in visible location
     - Approved plans availability for inspection
     - Inspection record maintenance and display
     - Contact information posting for project management

   - **Compliance Monitoring**
     - Daily code compliance verification
     - Material specification adherence checking
     - Installation method standard compliance
     - Safety protocol implementation and maintenance

2. **Environmental Protection During Construction**
   - **Sea Turtle Season Compliance (May 1 - October 31)**
     - Lighting restriction implementation
     - Noise control during sensitive periods
     - Beach and dune protection measures
     - Waste and debris immediate removal

   - **Stormwater Protection**
     - Erosion and sediment control installation
     - Material containment and spill prevention
     - Daily cleanup and site maintenance
     - Weather condition monitoring and response

#### 8.7.2 Inspection Coordination and Management
1. **Inspection Scheduling and Preparation**
   - **Advanced Scheduling Protocol**
     - 24-hour minimum advance notice
     - Online scheduling system utilization
     - Weather contingency planning
     - Inspector contact and coordination

   - **Site Preparation Standards**
     - Safe access route establishment
     - Work area organization and cleanliness
     - Tool and equipment staging
     - Documentation and reference material availability

2. **Inspection Process Management**
   - **Inspector Assistance and Cooperation**
     - Knowledgeable representative availability
     - Question answering and clarification provision
     - Problem identification and correction discussion
     - Follow-up action planning and scheduling

   - **Correction and Re-inspection Process**
     - Deficiency correction prioritization
     - Quality control verification before re-inspection
     - Documentation of corrections and improvements
     - Re-inspection scheduling and coordination
