# SOP-8215: Duval County Roofing Operations

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for conducting roofing operations in Duval County, Florida, ensuring compliance with consolidated government requirements, major port operations, military installation coordination, beach communities, and protocols for Florida First Roofing LLC's certified roofing services in Northeast Florida's largest city.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel conducting roofing operations, inspections, repairs, and installations within Duval County boundaries, including Jacksonville (consolidated city-county), Jacksonville Beach, Neptune Beach, Atlantic Beach, Baldwin, and areas around Naval Air Station Jacksonville, JAXPORT, and Jacksonville International Airport.

## 3. Definitions
- **City of Jacksonville Building Inspection Division**: Consolidated city-county authority
- **JAXPORT**: Major cargo port and cruise terminal operations
- **Naval Air Station Jacksonville**: Major military installation
- **Jacksonville International Airport (JAX)**: Regional aviation hub
- **First Coast**: Northeast Florida regional designation

## 4. Responsibilities
- **County Operations Manager**: Overall compliance with consolidated government regulations
- **Port Operations Coordinator**: JAXPORT and marine industry coordination
- **Military Installation Liaison**: Naval Air Station coordination
- **Beach Communities Manager**: Coastal area and beach town coordination
- **Airport Operations Specialist**: Aviation facility coordination

## 5. Prerequisites
- Active contractor license with City of Jacksonville
- Military installation security clearances
- Port facility security clearances (TWIC card)
- Airport area security clearances
- Beach community coordination experience
