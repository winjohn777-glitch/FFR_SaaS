## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for compliance with Volusia County coastal construction requirements, environmental protection standards, and specialized building codes for projects within coastal and flood-prone areas of Volusia County, Florida.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all roofing and construction projects within Volusia County coastal zones, including Daytona Beach, New Smyrna Beach, Ormond Beach, and unincorporated coastal areas subject to special environmental and construction regulations.

## Definitions

- **Coastal Construction Control Line (CCCL)**: State-regulated boundary requiring special permits for construction
- **Volusia County Beach Management**: Department overseeing coastal construction and environmental protection
- **Dune Protection Zone**: Environmentally sensitive areas requiring special construction protocols
- **Sea Turtle Conservation Area**: Protected nesting areas with seasonal construction restrictions
- **Volusia Forever Environmental Lands**: Preserved areas with construction limitations and special requirements

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC ensures full compliance with all Volusia County coastal construction requirements, environmental protection standards, and sea turtle conservation measures while delivering exceptional roofing services in environmentally sensitive coastal areas.

## Regulatory Framework

### 8.1 Volusia County Coastal Zone Requirements

#### 8.1.1 Coastal Construction Control Line (CCCL) Compliance
1. **CCCL Permit Requirements**
   - **Florida Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) Coordination**
     - Major structure permits for new construction seaward of CCCL
     - Minor structure permits for additions and modifications
     - Emergency authorization for storm damage repairs
     - Exemption determination for routine maintenance

   - **Volusia County CCCL Review Process**
     - Local consistency review with county comprehensive plan
     - Environmental impact assessment and mitigation
     - Public notice and comment period coordination
     - Construction monitoring and compliance verification

2. **Coastal Construction Standards**
   - **Enhanced Structural Requirements**
     - Design wind speed: 180 mph for coastal high hazard areas
     - Flood-resistant construction below Base Flood Elevation (BFE)
     - Pile foundation and elevated construction standards
     - Breakaway wall and flood opening requirements

   - **Environmental Protection Measures**
     - Native vegetation preservation and restoration
     - Dune stabilization and protection protocols
     - Drainage and stormwater management requirements
     - Light pollution reduction for sea turtle protection

#### 8.1.2 Municipal Coastal Jurisdiction Coordination
1. **City of Daytona Beach Requirements**
   - **Beachfront Overlay District Standards**
     - Height and density restrictions for coastal properties
     - Architectural design guidelines for beachfront development
     - Public beach access and parking requirements
     - Tourism accommodation and development standards

   - **Motorsports Tourism Zone Considerations**
     - Race event schedule coordination for construction timing
     - Enhanced structural standards for high-traffic tourism areas
     - Parking and accessibility requirements during events
     - Economic impact consideration for major projects

2. **New Smyrna Beach and Ormond Beach Requirements**
   - **Historic District and Preservation Areas**
     - Design review for properties in historic districts
     - Material authenticity and architectural compatibility
     - Community character preservation requirements
     - Public involvement and notification procedures

   - **Environmental Sensitivity Zones**
     - Enhanced environmental protection in sensitive areas
     - Wildlife habitat preservation and restoration requirements
     - Water quality protection and stormwater management
     - Public access and recreation facility coordination

### 8.2 Environmental Protection and Conservation Requirements

#### 8.2.1 Sea Turtle Conservation Compliance
1. **Nesting Season Restrictions (May 1 - October 31)**
   - **Lighting Ordinance Compliance**
     - Turtle-friendly lighting installation requirements
     - Window tinting and light shielding for beachfront properties
     - Temporary lighting restrictions during construction
     - Emergency lighting exemption procedures for safety

   - **Construction Activity Limitations**
     - No nighttime construction activities during nesting season
     - Beach and dune access route designation and protection
     - Noise reduction requirements during sensitive periods
     - Immediate waste and debris removal requirements

2. **Habitat Protection and Restoration**
   - **Native Vegetation Preservation**
     - Sea oat and native dune vegetation protection
     - Invasive species removal and control requirements
     - Restoration planting requirements for disturbed areas
     - Long-term maintenance and monitoring obligations

   - **Beach and Dune Stabilization**
     - Erosion control and sediment management
     - Sand displacement prevention and restoration
     - Walkway and access structure design standards
     - Natural shoreline process protection and enhancement

#### 8.2.2 Water Quality and Stormwater Management
1. **Indian River Lagoon and Atlantic Ocean Protection**
   - **Water Quality Standards Compliance**
     - Nutrient pollution prevention and control
     - Sediment and erosion control during construction
     - Chemical and material containment protocols
     - Spill prevention and emergency response procedures

   - **Stormwater Management Requirements**
     - Low Impact Development (LID) technique implementation
     - Green infrastructure and natural treatment systems
     - Retention and detention facility design and maintenance
     - Water reuse and conservation system integration

2. **Volusia Forever Environmental Lands Coordination**
   - **Conservation Area Buffer Requirements**
     - Setback and buffer zone maintenance
     - Wildlife corridor protection and enhancement
     - Habitat connectivity preservation
     - Conservation easement compliance and monitoring

   - **Ecosystem Impact Minimization**
     - Wetland protection and avoidance measures
     - Wildlife habitat preservation during construction
     - Native species protection and enhancement
     - Restoration and mitigation requirement compliance

### 8.3 Flood Zone and Hurricane Preparedness Requirements

#### 8.3.1 FEMA Flood Zone Compliance
1. **Coastal High Hazard Area (V Zone) Requirements**
   - **Elevated Construction Standards**
     - Pile foundation and elevated structure requirements
     - Flood-resistant materials below Base Flood Elevation (BFE)
     - Breakaway wall construction and flood opening specifications
     - Mechanical and electrical equipment elevation requirements

   - **Wave Action and Storm Surge Protection**
     - Wave runup and overtopping calculation requirements
     - Enhanced fastening and connection standards
     - Impact-resistant design for debris and wave forces
     - Emergency access and evacuation route maintenance

2. **Special Flood Hazard Area (AE Zone) Compliance**
   - **Floodproofing and Elevation Requirements**
     - Lowest floor elevation above BFE requirements
     - Flood-resistant construction material specifications
     - Hydrostatic pressure relief and flood opening design
     - Certification and documentation requirements

   - **Insurance and Documentation Compliance**
     - Elevation certificate preparation and submission
     - FEMA compliance documentation and record keeping
     - Insurance requirement communication and verification
     - Post-construction inspection and certification

#### 8.3.2 Hurricane and Storm Preparedness
1. **Enhanced Wind Resistance Standards**
   - **Ultimate Design Wind Speed Compliance**
     - 180 mph design wind speed for coastal areas
     - Component and cladding design and testing requirements
     - Continuous load path verification and documentation
     - Impact resistance standards for roof systems and openings

   - **Storm Damage Prevention and Mitigation**
     - Wind-borne debris protection systems
     - Emergency repair access and planning
     - Post-storm damage assessment and reporting protocols
     - Insurance claim documentation and support procedures

2. **Emergency Response and Recovery Planning**
   - **Evacuation Zone Compliance**
     - Construction timing coordination with evacuation procedures
     - Emergency access route protection and maintenance
     - Temporary housing and shelter coordination
     - Post-storm recovery assistance and support

   - **Community Resilience and Preparedness**
     - Public education and awareness program participation
     - Emergency communication and notification system integration
     - Resource sharing and mutual aid coordination
     - Long-term recovery and rebuilding support
