# SOP-8020: Consumer Protection Law Compliance

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for ensuring compliance with Florida consumer protection laws, implementing fair business practices, and maintaining ethical customer relationships while protecting both consumer rights and company interests in all roofing contractor operations.

### Scope
- Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act compliance
- Home improvement contractor consumer protection requirements
- Contract and warranty disclosure obligations
- Lien law compliance and customer notification procedures
- Dispute resolution and customer service standards
- Regulatory reporting and record-keeping requirements

## 2. Regulatory Framework

### Florida Deceptive and Unfair Trade Practices Act (FDUTPA)
- Chapter 501, Part II, Florida Statutes
- Prohibition of deceptive and unfair business practices
- Consumer right to legal remedies and attorney fees
- Civil penalty and enforcement provisions

### Home Improvement Finance Act
- Chapter 520, Florida Statutes
- Home improvement contract requirements and disclosures
- Financing and payment protection provisions
- Cancellation rights and refund procedures

### Construction Lien Law
- Chapter 713, Florida Statutes
- Lien rights and notice requirements
- Payment protection and dispute resolution
- Property owner notification and protection

### Professional Licensing Standards
- Chapter 489, Florida Statutes: Contracting regulations
- Professional conduct and ethical practice requirements
- Consumer complaint and disciplinary procedures
- Quality standards and warranty obligations

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 Contract Compliance and Disclosure
```
Required Contract Elements:
□ Complete contractor identification and licensing information
□ Detailed scope of work and material specifications
□ Total cost breakdown and payment schedule
□ Start and completion date projections
□ Warranty and guarantee terms and conditions
□ Customer cancellation rights and procedures
□ Lien law notice and customer protection information

Disclosure Requirements:
- Three-day cancellation right for door-to-door sales
- Lien law notice and payment protection information
- Warranty terms and limitation disclosures
- Insurance coverage and protection information
- Dispute resolution procedures and customer rights
```

### 5.2 Fair Business Practice Standards
```
Prohibited Practices:
- Deceptive advertising or misrepresentation
- High-pressure sales tactics or coercion
- Unreasonable pricing or cost inflation
- Failure to honor written agreements or warranties
- Inadequate disclosure of material information

Required Practices:
- Honest and accurate advertising and marketing
- Fair and competitive pricing and cost estimates
- Complete and transparent contract terms
- Prompt and professional service delivery
- Responsive customer service and communication
```

### 5.3 Customer Service and Dispute Resolution
```
Customer Service Standards:
- Prompt response to inquiries and concerns (24-48 hours)
- Professional and respectful customer interaction
- Clear communication and regular progress updates
- Quality work performance and cleanup procedures
- Warranty service and follow-up support

Dispute Resolution Process:
1. Initial customer concern reception and documentation
2. Internal investigation and fact-finding coordination
3. Resolution development and customer communication
4. Implementation and follow-up verification
5. Documentation and improvement planning
```

### 5.4 Warranty and Guarantee Administration
```
Warranty Requirements:
- Written warranty terms and coverage disclosure
- Reasonable warranty period and scope definition
- Clear claim procedure and customer rights
- Prompt response and resolution timeline
- Quality workmanship and material guarantee

Administration Process:
- Warranty registration and customer education
- Claim reception and evaluation procedures
- Resolution coordination and implementation
- Customer satisfaction verification and follow-up
- Record maintenance and performance tracking
```
