## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for compliance with Miami-Dade County High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ) requirements, including enhanced building codes, product approval processes, and specialized construction standards for roofing projects in hurricane-prone areas.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all roofing projects within Miami-Dade County boundaries, particularly those in the HVHZ designation, including residential, commercial, and institutional buildings subject to enhanced hurricane protection requirements.

## Definitions

- **High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)**: Miami-Dade and Broward County areas subject to enhanced building code requirements
- **Miami-Dade County Product Control Division**: Department responsible for testing and approving building products for HVHZ use
- **Notice of Acceptance (NOA)**: Required approval document for all building products used in HVHZ
- **Florida Building Code - High Velocity Hurricane Zone**: Enhanced building code requirements for hurricane protection
- **Design Pressure (DP)**: Wind pressure resistance rating required for building components

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC ensures full compliance with all Miami-Dade County HVHZ requirements, product approval processes, and enhanced construction standards while delivering superior hurricane-resistant roofing systems that protect life and property.
