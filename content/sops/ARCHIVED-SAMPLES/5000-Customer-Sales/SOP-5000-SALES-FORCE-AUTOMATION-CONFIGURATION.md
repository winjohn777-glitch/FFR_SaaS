## BLOCK 3: DEFINITIONS

**Sales Force Automation:** The integrated technology platform managing all sales activities from lead generation through contract completion including opportunity tracking, proposal creation, customer communication, and performance analytics.

**Sales Pipeline:** The systematic progression of sales opportunities through defined stages from initial contact to closed sale, providing visibility into sales activity, forecasting, and performance measurement.

**Lead Scoring:** The automated evaluation and ranking of sales prospects based on demographics, behavior, and engagement criteria to prioritize sales efforts and optimize conversion rates.

## BLOCK 5: PROCEDURE

### 5.1 Lead Management and Qualification

**Lead Capture and Entry:**
1. Capture leads from multiple sources including website, referrals, and marketing
2. Enter complete lead information into CRM system
3. Assign lead scores based on qualification criteria
4. Route leads to appropriate sales representatives
5. Set up automated follow-up sequences and reminders

**Lead Qualification Process:**
1. Contact leads within established timeframes (24-48 hours)
2. Conduct initial qualification using BANT criteria (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
3. Document qualification results and lead scoring updates
4. Convert qualified leads to sales opportunities
5. Schedule follow-up activities and customer meetings

### 5.2 Opportunity Pipeline Management

**Opportunity Creation and Tracking:**
1. Create opportunity records for qualified leads
2. Define opportunity value, timeline, and probability
3. Assign opportunities to appropriate sales stages
4. Configure automated stage progression and requirements
5. Set up activity reminders and follow-up tasks

**Pipeline Stage Management:**
- **Stage 1:** Initial Contact and Qualification (10% probability)
- **Stage 2:** Needs Assessment and Solution Design (25% probability)
- **Stage 3:** Proposal Presentation and Negotiation (50% probability)
- **Stage 4:** Contract Review and Approval (75% probability)
- **Stage 5:** Closed Won or Lost (100% or 0% probability)

### 5.3 Proposal and Quote Generation

**Automated Proposal Creation:**
1. Generate proposals using predefined templates and pricing
2. Include customer-specific requirements and specifications
3. Calculate pricing based on materials, labor, and margin requirements
4. Attach supporting documents and reference materials
5. Configure electronic delivery and tracking capabilities

**Quote Management and Tracking:**
1. Track proposal delivery and customer engagement
2. Monitor quote expiration dates and renewal requirements
3. Process quote revisions and updates efficiently
4. Track conversion rates and proposal effectiveness
5. Generate reports on proposal activity and success rates

### 5.4 Sales Performance and Analytics

**Activity Tracking and Reporting:**
1. Track all sales activities including calls, meetings, and proposals
2. Monitor sales representative performance against targets
3. Analyze conversion rates and sales cycle timeframes
4. Identify coaching opportunities and performance gaps
5. Generate executive dashboards and performance reports

**Forecasting and Planning:**
1. Create sales forecasts based on pipeline probability
2. Analyze historical trends and seasonal patterns
3. Set performance targets and goal tracking
4. Monitor territory performance and opportunity distribution
5. Plan resource allocation and sales strategy optimization
