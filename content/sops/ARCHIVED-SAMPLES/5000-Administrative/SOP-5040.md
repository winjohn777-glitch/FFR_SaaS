# SOP-5040: IT System Management and Security

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for managing information technology systems, ensuring data security, maintaining system performance, and supporting business operations through reliable and secure technology infrastructure for Florida First Roofing LLC.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all IT systems, networks, devices, software applications, and data management across all company operations, including office systems, field technology, customer data, and third-party integrations.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **IT Manager**: Oversee all technology systems and security protocols
- **Network Administrator**: Manage network infrastructure and connectivity
- **Database Administrator**: Maintain data systems and backup procedures
- **Security Coordinator**: Implement cybersecurity measures and monitoring
- **Help Desk Support**: Provide user support and troubleshooting assistance
- **Department Managers**: Ensure compliance with IT policies and procedures

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Infrastructure Systems:
- Network equipment including servers, routers, switches, and wireless systems
- Backup and disaster recovery systems with offsite storage capabilities
- Security systems including firewalls, antivirus, and intrusion detection
- Cloud services and hybrid infrastructure management platforms
- Mobile device management and field technology support systems

### Software and Applications:
- Business management software including CRM, accounting, and project management
- Security software including endpoint protection and vulnerability scanning
- Backup and recovery software with automated scheduling capabilities
- Remote access and VPN solutions for secure connectivity
- Monitoring and performance management tools for system optimization

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Ensure all electrical installations meet safety codes and standards
- Implement proper grounding and surge protection for equipment
- Maintain safe working conditions in server rooms and equipment areas
- Provide proper training on electrical safety for IT personnel
- Regular inspection of equipment and infrastructure for safety hazards

## 6. Pre-Operation Checklist
- [ ] Verify all systems are operational and performing within parameters
- [ ] Confirm backup systems completed successfully and data integrity verified
- [ ] Review security monitoring alerts and address any identified issues
- [ ] Update software patches and security updates across all systems
- [ ] Test disaster recovery procedures and communication systems
- [ ] Verify user access controls and permission settings
- [ ] Monitor network performance and capacity utilization

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: System Infrastructure Management
1. **Network Infrastructure Maintenance**:
   - **Server Management**: Monitor server performance, capacity, and availability
   - **Network Connectivity**: Maintain reliable internet and internal network connections
   - **Wireless Systems**: Ensure secure and reliable wireless access throughout facilities
   - **Equipment Lifecycle**: Plan and execute hardware refresh and upgrade cycles
   - **Vendor Management**: Coordinate with technology vendors and service providers

2. **Performance Monitoring and Optimization**:
   - Daily monitoring of system performance metrics and availability
   - Weekly analysis of network traffic and capacity utilization
   - Monthly review of system logs and error reports
   - Quarterly performance optimization and capacity planning
   - Annual infrastructure assessment and upgrade planning

### Phase 2: Data Security and Protection
1. **Cybersecurity Implementation**:
   - **Firewall Management**: Configure and maintain network security boundaries
   - **Antivirus Protection**: Deploy and update endpoint protection across all devices
   - **Access Controls**: Implement role-based access controls and authentication
   - **Encryption**: Encrypt sensitive data in transit and at rest
   - **Security Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for threats and unauthorized access

2. **Data Backup and Recovery**:
   - **Automated Backups**: Daily automated backups of all critical business data
   - **Offsite Storage**: Secure offsite backup storage with encryption and access controls
   - **Recovery Testing**: Monthly recovery testing to verify backup integrity
   - **Disaster Recovery Planning**: Comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity procedures
   - **Version Control**: Maintain multiple backup versions for point-in-time recovery

### Phase 3: User Support and Training
1. **Help Desk and Technical Support**:
   - **Ticket Management**: Structured help desk system for user support requests
   - **Response Time Standards**: Defined response and resolution time targets
   - **Remote Support**: Secure remote access capabilities for efficient problem resolution
   - **Documentation**: Maintain knowledge base and troubleshooting guides
   - **Escalation Procedures**: Clear escalation paths for complex technical issues

2. **User Training and Education**:
   - **Security Awareness**: Regular training on cybersecurity best practices
   - **Software Training**: Training on business applications and system updates
   - **Policy Compliance**: Education on IT policies and acceptable use requirements
   - **Mobile Device Security**: Training on secure use of mobile devices and apps
   - **Incident Reporting**: Training on how to report security incidents and technical issues

### Phase 4: System Integration and Development
1. **Business Application Management**:
   - **CRM System**: Maintain customer relationship management system performance
   - **Accounting Software**: Ensure financial system reliability and data integrity
   - **Project Management**: Support project tracking and collaboration tools
   - **Field Technology**: Manage mobile apps and field data collection systems
   - **Third-Party Integrations**: Coordinate with external systems and data exchanges

2. **Technology Planning and Innovation**:
   - **Technology Roadmap**: Develop strategic technology plans aligned with business goals
   - **Vendor Evaluation**: Assess new technologies and vendor solutions
   - **Cost-Benefit Analysis**: Evaluate technology investments and return on investment
   - **Implementation Planning**: Plan and execute technology upgrades and implementations
   - **Change Management**: Manage technology changes with minimal business disruption

## 8. Quality Control Standards
- System uptime: 99.5% availability during business hours
- Backup success rate: 100% of critical data backed up daily
- Security incident response: 95% of incidents resolved within 4 hours
- User satisfaction: 4.5/5 rating for IT support and system performance
- Data recovery: 100% data recovery capability within established timeframes

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Network connectivity problems**: Check network equipment and ISP connections
- **Slow system performance**: Monitor resource utilization and optimize configurations
- **Security threats**: Implement immediate containment and follow incident response procedures
- **Data corruption**: Restore from backup and investigate root cause
- **User access issues**: Verify permissions and reset authentication credentials

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Document all issues and maintain incident tracking for pattern analysis
2. Implement immediate fixes while planning long-term solutions
3. Escalate complex issues to vendors or external specialists when needed
4. Communicate system issues and resolution status to affected users
5. Review incidents to identify process improvements and prevention strategies
