# SOP-5003: Performance Management and Evaluation Procedures

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive performance management and evaluation procedures to ensure consistent employee assessment, promote continuous improvement, support career development, and align individual performance with Florida First Roofing LLC organizational objectives and strategic goals.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all employees across all departments and levels, including full-time, part-time, and temporary personnel, as well as all supervisors, managers, and executives responsible for conducting performance evaluations and managing employee development.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Human Resources Manager**: Oversee performance management system and ensure compliance with evaluation procedures
- **Department Managers**: Conduct regular performance evaluations and provide ongoing feedback and coaching
- **Direct Supervisors**: Monitor daily performance and provide immediate feedback and support
- **Executive Team**: Review management performance and approve significant performance-related decisions
- **Employees**: Participate actively in performance discussions and take ownership of professional development
- **Performance Review Committee**: Evaluate complex performance issues and recommend corrective actions

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Performance Management Systems:
- Performance evaluation software and digital documentation platforms
- Goal-setting and tracking tools with measurable objective management
- 360-degree feedback collection systems and multi-source evaluation tools
- Performance improvement plan templates and monitoring systems
- Compensation and advancement tracking databases

### Assessment Tools:
- Standardized evaluation forms customized for different role categories
- Competency assessment frameworks and skill evaluation matrices
- Customer feedback collection and analysis systems
- Productivity measurement tools and quality control metrics
- Professional development planning and tracking resources

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Ensure performance evaluations include safety performance and compliance metrics
- Document safety training completion and incident involvement in performance records
- Address safety violations immediately through performance improvement planning
- Maintain confidentiality of performance-related safety discussions and documentation
- Integrate safety performance with overall employee evaluation and advancement decisions

## 6. Pre-Operation Checklist
- [ ] Review employee performance data and documentation from previous evaluation period
- [ ] Prepare performance evaluation forms and assessment materials
- [ ] Schedule evaluation meetings with adequate time allocation
- [ ] Gather input from customers, colleagues, and other stakeholders
- [ ] Review employee goals and objectives from previous evaluation period
- [ ] Prepare performance improvement or development recommendations
- [ ] Ensure compliance with company policies and legal requirements

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Continuous Performance Monitoring and Documentation (Ongoing)
1. **Daily Performance Observation and Feedback**:
   - **Regular Check-ins**: Weekly one-on-one meetings between supervisors and direct reports
   - **Project Performance Tracking**: Monitor progress on assignments and quality of work completion
   - **Customer Interaction Assessment**: Evaluate customer service skills and professional relationships
   - **Safety Performance Monitoring**: Document safety compliance and incident involvement
   - **Team Collaboration Evaluation**: Assess teamwork skills and contribution to group objectives

2. **Goal Setting and Progress Tracking**:
   - **SMART Goals Development**: Create Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound objectives
   - **Quarterly Progress Reviews**: Assess advancement toward annual goals and adjust as needed
   - **Performance Metric Tracking**: Monitor key performance indicators relevant to each role
   - **Professional Development Monitoring**: Track training completion and skill development progress
   - **Career Advancement Planning**: Discuss advancement opportunities and preparation requirements

3. **Documentation and Record Keeping**:
   - Maintain detailed performance logs with specific examples and measurable outcomes
   - Document both positive achievements and areas requiring improvement
   - Record customer feedback and commendations or complaints
   - Track training completion, certifications, and professional development activities
   - Maintain confidential files with appropriate access controls and security measures

### Phase 2: Formal Performance Evaluation Process (Annual/Bi-Annual)
1. **Evaluation Preparation and Data Collection**:
   - **360-Degree Feedback Collection**: Gather input from supervisors, peers, subordinates, and customers
   - **Self-Assessment Completion**: Employee evaluation of own performance and goal achievement
   - **Performance Data Analysis**: Review productivity metrics, quality scores, and objective measurements
   - **Customer Satisfaction Review**: Analyze customer feedback and service quality assessments
   - **Professional Development Assessment**: Evaluate growth in skills and knowledge over evaluation period

2. **Formal Evaluation Meeting and Discussion**:
   - **Performance Review Session**: Structured discussion of achievements, challenges, and opportunities
   - **Goal Achievement Assessment**: Review progress on previous year's objectives and outcomes
   - **Strength Recognition**: Acknowledge and celebrate significant accomplishments and contributions
   - **Improvement Area Identification**: Discuss areas for development and enhancement opportunities
   - **Future Goal Setting**: Establish objectives for upcoming evaluation period with clear expectations

3. **Evaluation Documentation and Scoring**:
   - **Performance Rating Assignment**: Use standardized rating scales with specific criteria and examples
   - **Written Evaluation Completion**: Detailed narrative assessment of performance in all key areas
   - **Employee Response Documentation**: Record employee agreement or disagreement with evaluation
   - **Development Plan Creation**: Outline specific steps for improvement and advancement
   - **Compensation Review**: Assess salary, benefits, and incentive adjustments based on performance

### Phase 3: Performance Improvement and Development Planning
1. **High Performance Recognition and Advancement**:
   - **Achievement Recognition**: Public acknowledgment and reward for exceptional performance
   - **Career Advancement Opportunities**: Promotion considerations and expanded responsibilities
   - **Leadership Development**: Management training and succession planning for high performers
   - **Mentoring Assignments**: Opportunities to guide and develop junior team members
   - **Special Project Leadership**: Assignment to high-visibility and challenging initiatives

2. **Performance Improvement Planning**:
   - **Improvement Plan Development**: Specific, measurable steps for addressing performance deficiencies
   - **Timeline Establishment**: Clear deadlines for improvement and progress checkpoints
   - **Resource Allocation**: Training, coaching, and support resources to facilitate improvement
   - **Monitoring and Follow-up**: Regular review sessions to assess progress and provide guidance
   - **Consequence Communication**: Clear understanding of outcomes if improvement is not achieved

3. **Professional Development and Career Planning**:
   - **Skills Gap Analysis**: Identify training needs and development opportunities
   - **Career Path Discussion**: Explore advancement opportunities and preparation requirements
   - **External Education Support**: Tuition assistance and professional certification funding
   - **Cross-Training Opportunities**: Exposure to different departments and skill development
   - **Industry Involvement**: Professional association participation and networking opportunities

### Phase 4: Follow-up and Continuous Improvement
1. **Implementation Monitoring and Support**:
   - **Monthly Progress Reviews**: Regular check-ins on goal progress and development activities
   - **Resource Provision**: Ensure access to training, tools, and support needed for improvement
   - **Obstacle Removal**: Address barriers to performance improvement and goal achievement
   - **Coaching and Mentoring**: Provide ongoing guidance and skill development support
   - **Recognition and Encouragement**: Acknowledge improvement progress and maintain motivation

2. **System Evaluation and Enhancement**:
   - **Process Effectiveness Assessment**: Evaluate performance management system effectiveness and fairness
   - **Employee Feedback Collection**: Gather input on evaluation process and improvement suggestions
   - **Manager Training and Development**: Enhance supervisor skills in performance management and feedback
   - **Technology and Tool Updates**: Improve systems and processes for better efficiency and accuracy
   - **Legal Compliance Review**: Ensure all procedures meet employment law and regulatory requirements

## 8. Quality Control Standards
- Evaluation completion rate: 100% of employees receive timely performance evaluations
- Evaluation quality: 95% of evaluations include specific examples and measurable outcomes
- Employee satisfaction: 4.0/5 rating for fairness and usefulness of performance evaluation process
- Goal achievement: 80% of employees meet or exceed annual performance objectives
- Performance improvement: 85% success rate for employees completing performance improvement plans

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Inconsistent evaluation standards**: Provide manager training and standardized evaluation criteria
- **Employee disagreement with evaluation**: Implement appeal process and neutral review options
- **Lack of specific feedback**: Train managers in documentation and specific example provision
- **Goal setting difficulties**: Provide training in SMART goal development and tracking methods
- **Performance improvement resistance**: Enhance communication and support resources

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Address evaluation disputes through formal appeal process and neutral review
2. Provide additional manager training on fair and effective performance evaluation techniques
3. Implement calibration sessions to ensure consistent evaluation standards across departments
4. Enhance documentation requirements and provide examples of effective performance feedback
5. Seek legal counsel for complex performance management and employment law issues
