## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for implementing crisis communication plans during emergency situations to ensure coordinated, accurate, and timely information dissemination to all stakeholders while maintaining Florida First Roofing LLC's reputation and operational continuity.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All management personnel
- Emergency response team members
- Communications team
- Customer service representatives
- All employees during crisis situations
- External stakeholders and partners

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Crisis Communication Plan**: Structured framework for managing information flow during emergency situations
**Stakeholder**: Any party affected by or interested in company operations during crisis
**Crisis Coordinator**: Designated individual responsible for overseeing communication activities
**Information Hub**: Central location for gathering, verifying, and distributing crisis information
**Message Template**: Pre-approved communication formats for various crisis scenarios

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Chief Operating Officer
- Activate crisis communication plan
- Authorize major communication decisions
- Coordinate with senior leadership
- Oversee plan implementation effectiveness

### 4.2 Crisis Communications Coordinator
- Execute daily communication activities
- Manage information verification processes
- Coordinate with all communication channels
- Monitor communication effectiveness

### 4.3 Department Managers
- Provide accurate departmental information
- Communicate with direct reports
- Implement department-specific protocols
- Report communication challenges

### 4.4 All Employees
- Follow established communication protocols
- Report to designated supervisors
- Maintain confidentiality requirements
- Support crisis response efforts

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Crisis Communication Plan Activation

#### 5.1.1 Immediate Actions (0-30 minutes)
1. **Crisis Assessment**
   - Evaluate situation severity and scope
   - Determine stakeholder impact levels
   - Identify required communication channels
   - Assess timeline requirements

2. **Team Assembly**
   - Contact crisis communication team members
   - Establish communication command center
   - Verify contact information accuracy
   - Test communication systems functionality

3. **Initial Notifications**
   - Notify senior leadership immediately
   - Contact key department heads
   - Alert emergency response coordinators
   - Inform legal and compliance teams

#### 5.1.2 Communication Infrastructure Setup (30-60 minutes)
1. **Command Center Establishment**
   - Set up primary communication hub
   - Activate backup communication systems
   - Test all technology platforms
   - Establish information verification protocols

2. **Contact List Verification**
   - Confirm employee contact information
   - Verify customer communication channels
   - Update vendor and partner contacts
   - Test emergency notification systems

### 5.2 Information Management and Verification

#### 5.2.1 Information Gathering
1. **Source Identification**
   - Establish primary information sources
   - Verify source credibility and accuracy
   - Document information timestamps
   - Track information updates and changes

2. **Fact Verification Process**
   - Cross-reference multiple sources
   - Validate technical information accuracy
   - Confirm timeline and scope details
   - Document verification procedures

#### 5.2.2 Message Development
1. **Content Creation**
   - Use approved message templates
   - Adapt content for specific audiences
   - Include essential information elements
   - Maintain consistent messaging tone

2. **Approval Process**
   - Submit messages for legal review
   - Obtain management approval
   - Verify technical accuracy
   - Confirm regulatory compliance

### 5.3 Multi-Channel Communication Implementation

#### 5.3.1 Internal Communications
1. **Employee Notifications**
   - Send immediate safety alerts
   - Provide operational updates
   - Share resource availability information
   - Communicate timeline expectations

2. **Management Updates**
   - Conduct emergency leadership meetings
   - Share situation assessments
   - Coordinate department responses
   - Plan resource allocation

#### 5.3.2 External Communications
1. **Customer Communications**
   - Send immediate safety notifications
   - Provide service impact updates
   - Share alternative contact methods
   - Communicate resolution timelines

2. **Stakeholder Updates**
   - Notify vendors and suppliers
   - Update insurance providers
   - Inform regulatory authorities
   - Contact business partners

### 5.4 Florida-Specific Crisis Communication Considerations

#### 5.4.1 Hurricane Season Protocols
1. **Pre-Storm Communications**
   - Issue 72-hour advance warnings
   - Provide preparation instructions
   - Share evacuation procedures
   - Communicate business continuity plans

2. **Storm Event Communications**
   - Maintain hourly status updates
   - Provide safety condition reports
   - Share emergency contact information
   - Coordinate with local authorities

#### 5.4.2 Regulatory Compliance
1. **State Requirements**
   - Follow Florida Emergency Management protocols
   - Comply with public safety notifications
   - Coordinate with county emergency services
   - Meet insurance reporting requirements
