## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for employee communication and support during crisis situations to ensure workforce safety, maintain morale, provide necessary assistance, and preserve organizational cohesion throughout emergency events.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All Florida First Roofing LLC employees
- Human Resources personnel
- Management and supervisory staff
- Emergency response coordinators
- Employee assistance program providers
- Family members and emergency contacts

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Employee Crisis Communication**: Systematic approach to informing and supporting employees during emergencies
**Employee Assistance Program (EAP)**: Support services for employees facing personal or work-related challenges
**Crisis Support Services**: Emergency assistance and resources for affected employees
**Employee Welfare Check**: Process for verifying employee safety and well-being during crisis
**Emergency Employee Support**: Immediate assistance provided to employees during crisis situations

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Human Resources Manager
- Oversee employee communication and support programs
- Coordinate with emergency response team
- Authorize employee assistance expenditures
- Monitor employee welfare and support effectiveness

### 4.2 Supervisors and Managers
- Maintain direct contact with assigned employees
- Provide immediate support and assistance
- Report employee status and needs
- Implement departmental support procedures

### 4.3 Emergency Response Coordinator
- Activate employee support protocols
- Coordinate with external assistance providers
- Monitor overall employee welfare status
- Ensure integration with crisis response efforts

### 4.4 Employees
- Follow emergency communication procedures
- Report personal status and needs
- Support fellow employees when possible
- Participate in welfare check procedures
