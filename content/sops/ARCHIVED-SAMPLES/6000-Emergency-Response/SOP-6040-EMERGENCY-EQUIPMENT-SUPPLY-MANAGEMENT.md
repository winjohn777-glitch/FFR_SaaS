## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for managing emergency equipment and supplies to ensure Florida First Roofing LLC maintains adequate resources for crisis response, employee safety, business continuity, and rapid recovery during emergency situations.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Operations management personnel
- Warehouse and inventory staff
- Field supervisors and crew leaders
- Safety coordinators
- Procurement and purchasing staff
- Emergency response team members

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Emergency Equipment**: Tools and machinery required for crisis response and safety
**Emergency Supplies**: Materials and consumables needed during emergency situations
**Critical Inventory**: Items essential for employee safety and business continuity
**Emergency Cache**: Pre-positioned emergency supplies at strategic locations
**Rapid Deployment Kit**: Portable emergency response equipment package

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Operations Manager
- Oversee emergency equipment and supply management
- Authorize emergency procurement activities
- Coordinate with department heads on resource needs
- Monitor inventory levels and readiness status

### 4.2 Warehouse Manager
- Maintain emergency inventory tracking systems
- Conduct regular equipment inspections and maintenance
- Coordinate emergency supply distribution
- Manage vendor relationships for emergency procurement

### 4.3 Safety Coordinator
- Specify safety equipment requirements
- Inspect emergency safety equipment regularly
- Train personnel on emergency equipment use
- Maintain safety equipment certification records

### 4.4 Field Supervisors
- Monitor field emergency equipment readiness
- Report equipment needs and deficiencies
- Implement emergency equipment deployment
- Ensure crew access to emergency supplies
