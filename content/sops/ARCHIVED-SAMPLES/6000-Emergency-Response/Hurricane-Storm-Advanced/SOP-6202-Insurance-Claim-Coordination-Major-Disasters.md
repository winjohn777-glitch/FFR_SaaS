# SOP-6202: Insurance Claim Coordination During Major Disasters

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive insurance claim coordination procedures for Florida First Roofing LLC during major disaster events to ensure efficient claims processing, maximize coverage benefits for customers, coordinate with multiple insurance carriers and adjusters, and expedite repair authorization during peak claim periods.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all insurance claim activities during major disaster declarations, including coordination with multiple insurance carriers, public adjusters, independent adjusters, and emergency management agencies for expedited claims processing and repair authorization following hurricanes, tornadoes, and other catastrophic weather events.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Insurance Coordination Manager**: Oversee all disaster-related insurance claim activities and carrier relationships
- **Senior Claims Specialists**: Manage complex claims and coordinate with adjusters and carriers
- **Documentation Specialists**: Ensure comprehensive damage documentation and claims package preparation
- **Customer Advocacy Representatives**: Assist customers with claims process and coverage interpretation
- **Legal Counsel**: Provide guidance on coverage disputes and claim resolution procedures
- **Public Adjuster Liaisons**: Coordinate with retained public adjusters and manage joint assessments

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Immediate Claim Initiation (0-24 Hours Post-Storm)
1. **Emergency Claim Filing**:
   - File initial claims notices with all relevant insurance carriers
   - Coordinate with customers to ensure proper claim initiation procedures
   - Document emergency contact with carriers and claim number assignment
   - Establish priority scheduling for adjuster inspections based on damage severity
   - Coordinate with emergency management for area access and safety clearance

2. **Initial Damage Documentation**:
   - Capture comprehensive photographic evidence of all storm damage
   - Document pre-loss conditions using available historical records and photos
   - Prepare preliminary damage assessments for emergency repair authorization
   - Coordinate with customers on immediate needs and temporary living arrangements
   - Submit initial damage reports to carriers for emergency repair pre-authorization

### Phase 2: Comprehensive Claims Documentation (24-72 Hours)
1. **Detailed Damage Assessment and Documentation**:
   - **Comprehensive Property Evaluation**: Document all visible and hidden damage using advanced assessment techniques
   - **Technical Damage Analysis**: Provide detailed technical evaluation of structural damage and repair requirements
   - **Causation Documentation**: Establish clear linkage between storm event and property damage
   - **Code Compliance Assessment**: Evaluate current code requirements and upgrade necessities
   - **Material and Labor Cost Analysis**: Prepare detailed estimates using current market pricing

2. **Insurance Carrier-Specific Documentation**:
   - Prepare carrier-specific claim documentation packages per individual requirements
   - Complete standardized damage assessment forms for each insurance company
   - Coordinate with adjusters on specific documentation needs and preferences
   - Provide technical expertise and professional opinions on damage scope and repair requirements
   - Submit preliminary estimates for emergency repair authorization and coverage review

### Phase 3: Adjuster Coordination and Inspection Management (48-120 Hours)
1. **Adjuster Scheduling and Coordination**:
   - Coordinate inspection schedules with multiple adjusters and carriers
   - Provide technical support and expertise during adjuster property inspections
   - Document adjuster findings and provide additional technical clarification when needed
   - Coordinate joint inspections when multiple coverage types are involved (wind, flood, etc.)
   - Manage scheduling conflicts and priority adjustments based on damage severity

2. **Claims Advocacy and Technical Support**:
   - Provide professional expertise on construction standards and repair requirements
   - Advocate for proper damage recognition and coverage interpretation
   - Coordinate with public adjusters when retained by property owners
   - Provide technical education on roofing systems and damage patterns
   - Document any coverage disputes or disagreements for resolution procedures

### Phase 4: Coverage Analysis and Repair Authorization (3-10 Days)
1. **Coverage Evaluation and Maximization**:
   - Analyze policy coverage limits and coordinate claims across multiple policies when applicable
   - Identify all covered damage components and advocate for comprehensive coverage
   - Coordinate wind and flood claims when multiple perils are involved
   - Evaluate code upgrade coverage and additional living expense benefits
   - Work with customers and public adjusters to maximize policy benefits

2. **Repair Authorization and Scope Management**:
   - Negotiate repair scopes and pricing with insurance adjusters
   - Secure emergency repair authorizations for immediate property protection
   - Coordinate permanent repair authorizations and payment schedules
   - Manage supplemental claims for additional damage discovered during repairs
   - Establish quality control measures for insurance-approved repair specifications

### Phase 5: Claims Resolution and Repair Coordination (7-30 Days)
1. **Payment Coordination and Management**:
   - Coordinate insurance payment schedules and milestone releases
   - Manage joint check procedures and endorsement requirements
   - Coordinate with mortgage companies for payment release procedures
   - Track payment status and coordinate with carriers on any delays
   - Manage customer communication regarding payment timelines and procedures

2. **Quality Assurance and Claims Closure**:
   - Ensure all repair work meets insurance approval specifications
   - Coordinate final inspections with adjusters and quality control verification
   - Document repair completion and warranty information for insurance records
   - Process any remaining supplemental claims for additional discovered damage
   - Provide final documentation and photos for claims closure and customer records

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Adjuster scheduling conflicts**: Implement flexible scheduling and priority management systems
- **Coverage disputes**: Engage legal counsel and public adjuster coordination procedures
- **Payment delays**: Activate carrier escalation procedures and customer communication protocols
- **Documentation requirements**: Maintain comprehensive backup documentation and alternative formats
- **Communication failures**: Implement redundant communication systems and emergency protocols

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Escalate issues to Insurance Coordination Manager immediately
2. Engage legal counsel for coverage interpretation and dispute resolution
3. Coordinate with public adjusters and customer advocates as needed
4. Document all issues and implement process improvements
5. Maintain customer communication and manage expectations throughout resolution
