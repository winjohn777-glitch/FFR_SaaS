# SOP-3050: Customer Satisfaction Measurement and Analysis

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### 1.1 Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for measuring, analyzing, and improving customer satisfaction across all Florida First Roofing LLC services. This procedure ensures systematic collection of customer feedback, continuous service improvement, and maintenance of high customer satisfaction standards.

### 1.2 Scope
This SOP applies to all Florida First Roofing LLC customer interactions including:
- Residential and commercial roofing projects
- Emergency repair and maintenance services
- Insurance claim support and documentation
- Warranty service and follow-up
- Customer service and communication touchpoints
- Sales and consultation processes

## 2. DEFINITIONS

- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)**: Measure of how well company services meet customer expectations
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Metric measuring customer likelihood to recommend services
- **Customer Effort Score (CES)**: Measure of effort required for customers to complete tasks
- **Touchpoint**: Any interaction between customer and company personnel or systems
- **Customer Journey**: Complete experience from initial contact through project completion
- **Service Recovery**: Actions taken to address customer complaints and restore satisfaction

## 3. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 3.4 All Employees

- Collect informal customer feedback during interactions
- Report customer concerns and satisfaction issues
- Participate in customer service training and improvement initiatives
- Contribute to positive customer experience delivery

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Customer Satisfaction Measurement Framework

#### 5.1.1 Measurement Methodology Selection
1. **Multi-Metric Approach**
   - Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT) - 1-10 scale rating
   - Net Promoter Score (NPS) - likelihood to recommend (0-10)
   - Customer Effort Score (CES) - ease of service experience
   - Qualitative feedback through open-ended questions

2. **Measurement Timing**
   - Post-project completion surveys (within 48 hours)
   - Mid-project check-ins for long-duration projects
   - Annual relationship surveys for ongoing customers
   - Service recovery follow-up after complaint resolution

3. **Survey Distribution Methods**
   - Email surveys with mobile-optimized design
   - Phone interviews for high-value customers
   - Text message surveys for quick feedback
   - Paper surveys for customers preferring traditional methods

#### 5.1.2 Customer Journey Mapping
1. **Touchpoint Identification**
   - Initial inquiry and consultation
   - Sales process and contract negotiation
   - Project planning and preparation
   - Installation and construction activities
   - Project completion and cleanup
   - Follow-up and warranty service

2. **Satisfaction Measurement Points**
   - Pre-project expectation setting assessment
   - During-project communication and progress updates
   - Post-project completion satisfaction survey
   - Long-term relationship and loyalty measurement

### 5.2 Data Collection Procedures

#### 5.2.1 Survey Design and Implementation
1. **Survey Content Development**
   - Standard questions for all service types
   - Project-specific questions based on service delivered
   - Demographic information for segmentation analysis
   - Open-ended questions for detailed feedback

2. **Question Design Principles**
   - Clear, unbiased language and terminology
   - Appropriate response scales and options
   - Logical flow and reasonable survey length
   - Mobile-friendly design and formatting

3. **Survey Distribution Process**
   - Automated survey sending within 48 hours of project completion
   - Personalized survey invitations with project details
   - Multi-channel distribution for maximum response rate
   - Follow-up reminders for non-respondents

#### 5.2.2 Alternative Feedback Collection
1. **Direct Customer Interviews**
   - Structured interviews with high-value customers
   - In-depth discussions about service experience
   - Feedback on specific service improvements
   - Relationship building and retention activities

2. **Online Review Monitoring**
   - Regular monitoring of Google, Yelp, and industry review sites
   - Response to online reviews and feedback
   - Integration of online feedback with internal satisfaction data
   - Reputation management and improvement activities

3. **Informal Feedback Collection**
   - Training staff to recognize and document customer feedback
   - Standardized forms for recording informal feedback
   - Regular team meetings to discuss customer feedback
   - Integration of informal feedback with formal measurement

### 5.3 Data Analysis and Interpretation

#### 5.3.1 Quantitative Analysis
1. **Statistical Analysis**
   - Calculate overall CSAT, NPS, and CES scores
   - Trend analysis over time periods
   - Segmentation analysis by customer type, project size, geography
   - Correlation analysis between different satisfaction metrics

2. **Benchmarking and Comparison**
   - Compare performance to industry benchmarks
   - Track improvement against previous periods
   - Identify best-performing teams and processes
   - Set targets and goals for satisfaction improvement

#### 5.3.2 Qualitative Analysis
1. **Content Analysis**
   - Categorize open-ended feedback by themes
   - Identify common issues and improvement opportunities
   - Analyze language and sentiment in customer comments
   - Extract actionable insights for service improvement

2. **Root Cause Analysis**
   - Investigate underlying causes of satisfaction issues
   - Map problems to specific processes or touchpoints
   - Identify systemic issues requiring organizational changes
   - Prioritize improvement opportunities by impact and effort

### 5.4 Customer Service Improvement Implementation

#### 5.4.1 Improvement Planning
1. **Priority Setting**
   - Rank improvement opportunities by customer impact
   - Consider implementation feasibility and resource requirements
   - Align improvements with business strategy and goals
   - Set specific, measurable improvement targets

2. **Action Plan Development**
   - Define specific actions to address satisfaction issues
   - Assign responsibility and timelines for implementation
   - Identify resource requirements and budget implications
   - Establish success metrics and monitoring procedures

#### 5.4.2 Implementation and Monitoring
1. **Change Management**
   - Communicate changes to all affected personnel
   - Provide training on new processes and procedures
   - Monitor implementation progress and address obstacles
   - Collect feedback on change effectiveness

2. **Performance Monitoring**
   - Track satisfaction metrics during and after implementation
   - Monitor process performance indicators
   - Collect staff feedback on change implementation
   - Adjust improvements based on results and feedback

### 5.5 Service Recovery Procedures

#### 5.5.1 Complaint Identification and Classification
1. **Complaint Intake Process**
   - Multiple channels for customer complaint submission
   - Standardized complaint documentation and tracking
   - Initial response within 24 hours of receipt
   - Escalation procedures for serious complaints

2. **Complaint Classification**
   - Severity levels based on impact and urgency
   - Category classification by type of issue
   - Root cause identification and documentation
   - Assignment to appropriate personnel for resolution

#### 5.5.2 Resolution and Follow-Up
1. **Resolution Process**
   - Investigation and fact-finding procedures
   - Solution development and customer communication
   - Implementation of corrective actions
   - Documentation of resolution and lessons learned

2. **Customer Follow-Up**
   - Satisfaction verification after resolution
   - Relationship restoration and retention activities
   - Process improvement based on complaint analysis
   - Prevention of similar issues in the future

## 7. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS

### 7.1 Satisfaction Targets
- Overall Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT): 9.0 or higher (10-point scale)
- Net Promoter Score (NPS): 70 or higher
- Customer Effort Score (CES): 2.0 or lower (7-point scale)
- Customer retention rate: 90% or higher for existing customers

### 7.2 Response and Resolution Standards
- Survey response rate: 40% or higher
- Initial complaint response: within 24 hours
- Complaint resolution: 95% within 7 business days
- Service recovery satisfaction: 85% or higher

### 7.3 Improvement Standards
- Quarterly satisfaction trend improvement
- 100% implementation of high-priority improvement actions
- Annual customer service training completion for all staff
- 95% accuracy in satisfaction data collection and analysis
