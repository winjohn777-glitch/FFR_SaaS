# SOP-3071: Quality Management System Implementation

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### Purpose
To establish comprehensive procedures for implementing and maintaining a Quality Management System (QMS) for Florida First Roofing LLC, ensuring consistent quality delivery, continuous improvement, and customer satisfaction while meeting all regulatory and industry standards specific to Florida's roofing industry.

### Scope
This SOP applies to all quality management activities including:
- QMS framework development and implementation
- Quality policy and objectives establishment
- Process documentation and control
- Quality performance measurement and analysis
- Continuous improvement initiatives
- Compliance management and auditing
- Training and competency development
- Customer satisfaction management

## 2. DEFINITIONS

**Quality Management System (QMS)**: A formal system that documents processes, procedures, and responsibilities for achieving quality policies and objectives.

**Quality Policy**: A formal statement by management of the organization's intentions and direction related to quality.

**Quality Objectives**: Specific measurable goals that support the quality policy.

**Process**: A set of interrelated activities that transform inputs into outputs.

**Procedure**: A specified way to carry out an activity or process.

**Quality Manual**: A document that describes the quality management system of an organization.

**Corrective Action**: Action to eliminate the cause of a detected nonconformity or other undesirable situation.

**Preventive Action**: Action to eliminate the cause of a potential nonconformity or other undesirable situation.
