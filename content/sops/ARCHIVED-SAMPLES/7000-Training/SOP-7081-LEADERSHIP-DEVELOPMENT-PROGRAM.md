## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for leadership development to identify, develop, and retain high-potential employees while building Florida First Roofing LLC's leadership pipeline, enhancing organizational capability, and ensuring sustainable business growth through effective leadership at all levels.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- High-potential employees identified for leadership development
- Current supervisors and managers
- Senior leadership team members
- Human Resources and training personnel
- Leadership mentors and coaches
- External leadership development providers

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Leadership Development**: Systematic process of enhancing leadership capabilities and competencies
**High-Potential Employee**: Individual demonstrating leadership potential and advancement readiness
**Leadership Competency**: Essential skills and behaviors required for effective leadership
**Leadership Pipeline**: Structured progression of leadership development and advancement
**360-Degree Feedback**: Comprehensive feedback from supervisors, peers, and direct reports

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Human Resources Manager
- Oversee leadership development program implementation
- Coordinate with senior leadership on program direction
- Monitor program effectiveness and participant progress
- Manage external development provider relationships

### 4.2 Senior Leadership Team
- Identify and nominate high-potential employees
- Serve as mentors and coaches
- Provide development opportunities and experiences
- Support program participants and initiatives

### 4.3 Program Participants
- Actively engage in development activities
- Complete assigned learning and development tasks
- Apply learned leadership skills in current roles
- Mentor and support other developing leaders

### 4.4 Direct Supervisors
- Support employee participation in development programs
- Provide on-the-job leadership opportunities
- Offer feedback and coaching support
- Monitor skill application and improvement

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Leadership Development Program Structure

#### 5.1.1 Program Levels and Tracks
1. **Emerging Leaders Program**
   - Target: High-potential individual contributors
   - Duration: 12 months
   - Focus: Basic leadership fundamentals
   - Outcome: Promotion readiness to supervisor roles

2. **Supervisor Development Program**
   - Target: New and experienced supervisors
   - Duration: 18 months
   - Focus: Team leadership and management skills
   - Outcome: Enhanced team leadership effectiveness

3. **Manager Development Program**
   - Target: Managers and senior supervisors
   - Duration: 24 months
   - Focus: Strategic thinking and organizational leadership
   - Outcome: Preparation for senior management roles

4. **Executive Leadership Program**
   - Target: Senior managers and executives
   - Duration: Ongoing
   - Focus: Strategic leadership and business transformation
   - Outcome: Organizational leadership excellence

#### 5.1.2 Core Leadership Competencies
1. **Self-Leadership**
   - Self-awareness and emotional intelligence
   - Personal vision and goal setting
   - Stress management and resilience
   - Continuous learning and adaptation
   - Ethics and integrity demonstration

2. **People Leadership**
   - Team building and development
   - Communication and influence
   - Conflict resolution and negotiation
   - Coaching and mentoring skills
   - Performance management excellence

3. **Business Leadership**
   - Strategic thinking and planning
   - Decision-making and problem-solving
   - Financial acumen and business understanding
   - Change management and innovation
   - Customer focus and market awareness

4. **Organizational Leadership**
   - Culture development and transformation
   - Cross-functional collaboration
   - Stakeholder relationship management
   - Crisis leadership and adaptability
   - Succession planning and talent development
