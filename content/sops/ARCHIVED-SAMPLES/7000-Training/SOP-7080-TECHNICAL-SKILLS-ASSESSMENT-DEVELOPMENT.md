## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for technical skills assessment and development to ensure Florida First Roofing LLC employees maintain and advance the technical competencies required for safe, efficient, and high-quality roofing operations while supporting career advancement and organizational excellence.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All technical employees and field personnel
- Training and development staff
- Supervisors and project managers
- Human Resources personnel
- Skills assessment evaluators
- External training providers and instructors

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Technical Skills Assessment**: Systematic evaluation of employee technical competencies and capabilities
**Competency Gap Analysis**: Identification of differences between current and required skill levels
**Skills Development Plan**: Structured approach for improving employee technical capabilities
**Performance-Based Assessment**: Evaluation method based on actual work performance and outcomes
**Continuous Learning**: Ongoing process of skill enhancement and knowledge acquisition

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Training Manager
- Oversee technical skills assessment and development programs
- Coordinate with department heads on skill requirements
- Develop assessment tools and evaluation criteria
- Monitor training effectiveness and outcomes

### 4.2 Department Supervisors
- Conduct regular employee skill assessments
- Identify training needs and development opportunities
- Support employee participation in training programs
- Monitor skill application and improvement

### 4.3 Skills Assessment Evaluators
- Conduct formal technical skill evaluations
- Document assessment results and recommendations
- Provide feedback to employees and supervisors
- Maintain assessment records and documentation

### 4.4 Employees
- Participate in skill assessment activities
- Engage actively in development programs
- Apply learned skills in work performance
- Pursue continuous learning opportunities

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Technical Skills Assessment Framework

#### 5.1.1 Competency Standards Development
1. **Job-Specific Skill Requirements**
   - Roofing installation techniques
   - Safety procedures and protocols
   - Equipment operation and maintenance
   - Quality control and inspection
   - Customer service and communication

2. **Proficiency Level Definitions**
   - **Novice**: Basic understanding, requires supervision
   - **Competent**: Independent performance, standard quality
   - **Proficient**: Consistent high performance, minimal supervision
   - **Expert**: Advanced skills, teaches and mentors others
   - **Master**: Industry-leading expertise, innovation capability

#### 5.1.2 Assessment Tool Development
1. **Performance-Based Assessments**
   - Hands-on skill demonstrations
   - Real work project evaluations
   - Simulated scenario assessments
   - Peer evaluation components
   - Self-assessment instruments

2. **Knowledge-Based Assessments**
