# SOP-7020: Roofing Installation Techniques Training

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for training employees in proper roofing installation techniques specific to Florida's climate, building codes, and industry best practices, ensuring high-quality installations, safety compliance, and customer satisfaction.

### Scope
- Roofing material installation technique training and certification
- Florida-specific installation requirements and climate considerations
- Quality control and inspection technique training
- Equipment operation and maintenance training
- Customer interaction and professionalism during installation
- Troubleshooting and problem-solving skill development

## 2. Regulatory Framework

### Florida Building Code Requirements
- Chapter 15: Roof Assemblies and Rooftop Structures
- Section 1507: Requirements for Roof Coverings
- Chapter 16: Structural Design for wind load compliance
- Section 1609: Wind Loads for hurricane-resistant installation

### International Residential Code (IRC) Adoption
- Chapter 9: Roof Assemblies for residential applications
- Section R905: Requirements for Roof Coverings
- Section R802: Wood Roof Framing requirements
- Section R903: Weather Protection standards

### National Roofing Contractors Association (NRCA) Standards
- Installation guidelines and best practices
- Quality control and inspection standards
- Material handling and storage requirements
- Safety procedures during installation activities

### Manufacturer Specifications
- Product-specific installation requirements and warranties
- Tool and equipment specifications and usage
- Environmental condition limitations and considerations
- Quality control and inspection protocols

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 Theoretical Foundation Training
```
Material Knowledge Curriculum:
□ Roofing material types and applications
□ Florida climate considerations and material selection
□ Building code requirements and compliance
□ Manufacturer specifications and warranty requirements
□ Installation tool and equipment requirements

Assessment Methods:
- Written examination covering material properties
- Code compliance quiz and scenario analysis
- Manufacturer specification interpretation test
- Climate consideration and application assessment
- Tool and equipment identification and usage test
```

### 5.2 Basic Installation Technique Training
```
Core Installation Skills:
- Measurement and layout techniques for accuracy
- Fastener selection and installation procedures
- Sealing and waterproofing application methods
- Flashing installation and integration techniques
- Quality control inspection and verification

Training Methods:
- Instructor demonstration and explanation
- Hands-on practice with supervision and feedback
- Peer learning and collaborative skill development
- Video instruction and technique review
- Real-world application and reinforcement
```

### 5.3 Advanced Installation Techniques
```
Specialized Applications:
- Complex roof geometry and design challenges
- Architectural feature integration and detailing
- Emergency repair and temporary protection
- Historic building and restoration techniques
- High-performance and energy-efficient systems

Florida-Specific Considerations:
- Hurricane-resistant installation techniques
- High wind and storm preparation methods
- Heat and UV resistance installation procedures
- Moisture management and ventilation integration
- Coastal environment and salt air considerations
```

### 5.4 Quality Control and Inspection Training
```
Inspection Techniques:
- Visual inspection methods and checklists
- Material defect identification and resolution
- Installation quality verification and standards
- Water resistance testing and validation
- Long-term durability assessment and monitoring

Documentation Requirements:
- Installation photo documentation and records
- Quality control checklist completion and verification
- Customer walkthrough and acceptance procedures
- Warranty registration and maintenance recommendations
- Follow-up inspection scheduling and tracking
```
