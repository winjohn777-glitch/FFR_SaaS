# SOP-1092: Safety Audit and Compliance Verification

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### Purpose
To establish comprehensive procedures for conducting safety audits and compliance verification activities within Florida First Roofing LLC operations, ensuring systematic evaluation of safety programs, regulatory compliance, and continuous improvement while addressing Florida's unique regulatory environment and operational challenges.

### Scope
This SOP applies to all safety audit activities including:
- Internal safety program audits
- Regulatory compliance audits
- Third-party safety audits
- Customer safety audits
- Vendor and subcontractor audits
- Self-assessment programs
- Certification body audits
- Insurance carrier audits

## 2. DEFINITIONS

**Safety Audit**: A systematic, independent examination of safety management systems, procedures, and practices to determine compliance and effectiveness.

**Compliance Verification**: The process of confirming adherence to regulatory requirements, standards, and company policies.

**Audit Finding**: An objective observation based on evidence that indicates nonconformance with requirements.

**Corrective Action**: Measures taken to eliminate the cause of detected nonconformities or deficiencies.

**Audit Trail**: Documentation that provides evidence of the sequence of activities and decisions during an audit.

**Continuous Improvement**: An ongoing effort to improve products, services, or processes.
