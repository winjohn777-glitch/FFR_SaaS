## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for the integration, deployment, and management of artificial intelligence technologies across all business operations to enhance efficiency, improve decision-making, and deliver superior customer experiences.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all AI-powered systems, machine learning applications, automated decision-making processes, and intelligent automation tools implemented across Florida First Roofing LLC operations.

## Definitions

- **Artificial Intelligence (AI)**: Computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence
- **Machine Learning (ML)**: AI subset that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: AI technology that enables computers to understand and process human language
- **Computer Vision**: AI technology that enables computers to interpret and analyze visual information
- **Predictive Analytics**: Use of data, algorithms, and ML techniques to predict future outcomes based on historical data

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC leverages artificial intelligence technologies to enhance operational efficiency, improve customer service, optimize resource allocation, and maintain competitive advantage while ensuring ethical AI use, data privacy, and human oversight.
