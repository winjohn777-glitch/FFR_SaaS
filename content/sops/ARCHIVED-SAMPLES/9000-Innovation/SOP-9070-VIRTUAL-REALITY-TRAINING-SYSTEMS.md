## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for implementing and managing virtual reality (VR) training systems to enhance employee safety training, technical skill development, and customer education through immersive and interactive learning experiences.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all VR training programs, equipment, content development, and learning management systems used for employee training, safety education, and customer engagement by Florida First Roofing LLC.

## Definitions

- **Virtual Reality (VR)**: Immersive computer-generated environment that simulates physical presence in real or imagined worlds
- **Augmented Reality (AR)**: Technology that overlays digital information and virtual objects onto the real world
- **Mixed Reality (MR)**: Technology that combines real and virtual worlds where physical and digital objects co-exist and interact
- **Learning Management System (LMS)**: Software application for administration, documentation, tracking, and delivery of educational content
- **Immersive Learning**: Educational approach that uses technology to create realistic simulations and interactive experiences

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC leverages virtual reality technology to provide safe, effective, and engaging training experiences that improve learning outcomes, reduce training costs, enhance safety performance, and deliver innovative customer education services.
