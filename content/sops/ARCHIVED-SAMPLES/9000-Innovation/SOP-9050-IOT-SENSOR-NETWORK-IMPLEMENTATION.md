## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for implementing and managing Internet of Things (IoT) sensor networks to monitor roof performance, environmental conditions, and building systems, enabling predictive maintenance and enhanced customer service.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all IoT sensor installations, network management, data collection systems, and monitoring platforms deployed across residential and commercial roofing projects by Florida First Roofing LLC.

## Definitions

- **Internet of Things (IoT)**: Network of interconnected devices that collect and exchange data through embedded sensors and connectivity
- **Sensor Network**: Collection of spatially distributed autonomous sensors that monitor environmental or physical conditions
- **Edge Computing**: Processing data near the source of data generation rather than in a centralized data center
- **Gateway Device**: Hardware that connects IoT sensors to the internet and cloud-based analytics platforms
- **Telemetry**: Automated collection and transmission of data from remote sensors and monitoring devices

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC leverages IoT sensor technology to provide proactive monitoring services, predictive maintenance capabilities, and data-driven insights that enhance roof performance, extend system lifespan, and deliver exceptional customer value.

## IoT Implementation Framework

### 9.1 IoT Architecture and System Design

#### 9.1.1 Sensor Network Architecture
1. **Hierarchical Network Design**
   - **Device Layer (Sensors and Actuators)**
     - Environmental monitoring sensors (temperature, humidity, wind)
     - Structural monitoring sensors (vibration, stress, movement)
     - Water detection and moisture sensors
     - Air quality and indoor environment sensors

   - **Edge Layer (Gateways and Local Processing)**
     - IoT gateway devices for data aggregation
     - Local data processing and filtering
     - Real-time decision making and alerts
     - Backup power and connectivity systems

   - **Cloud Layer (Data Platform and Analytics)**
     - Centralized data storage and management
     - Advanced analytics and machine learning
     - Dashboard and visualization platforms
     - Integration with business systems

2. **Communication Protocols and Connectivity**
   - **Wireless Communication Standards**
     - LoRaWAN for long-range, low-power applications
     - WiFi for high-bandwidth local connectivity
     - Cellular (4G/5G) for remote location connectivity
     - Bluetooth/Zigbee for short-range sensor networks

   - **Network Security and Encryption**
     - End-to-end encryption for data transmission
     - Device authentication and certificate management
     - Network segmentation and access control
     - Regular security updates and patch management

#### 9.1.2 Sensor Selection and Deployment Strategy
1. **Environmental Monitoring Sensors**
   - **Weather and Climate Sensors**
     - Temperature and humidity monitoring
     - Wind speed and direction measurement
     - Precipitation and moisture detection
     - Solar irradiance and UV exposure monitoring

   - **Structural Health Monitoring**
     - Accelerometers for vibration and movement detection
     - Strain gauges for structural stress monitoring
     - Tilt sensors for settling and displacement detection
     - Crack monitoring and expansion sensors

2. **Performance and Efficiency Sensors**
   - **Energy and Thermal Performance**
     - Thermal imaging and heat signature monitoring
     - Insulation effectiveness measurement
     - HVAC system efficiency monitoring
     - Solar panel performance optimization

   - **Water Management and Leak Detection**
     - Roof membrane integrity monitoring
     - Gutter and drainage system monitoring
     - Indoor leak detection and alerting
     - Water damage prevention and early warning
