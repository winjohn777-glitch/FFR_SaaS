# SOP-9010: Sustainable Roofing Material Selection and Implementation

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for evaluating, selecting, and implementing sustainable roofing materials and systems at Florida First Roofing LLC, ensuring environmental responsibility, energy efficiency, customer value, and regulatory compliance while maintaining quality standards and competitive pricing.

### Scope
- Sustainable material evaluation and certification criteria
- Environmental impact assessment and lifecycle analysis
- Energy efficiency measurement and performance verification
- Cost-benefit analysis and customer value proposition
- Installation and maintenance requirements for sustainable systems
- Marketing and customer education for sustainable roofing solutions

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 Sustainability Assessment Criteria
```
Environmental Impact Assessment:
□ Material lifecycle environmental impact analysis
□ Manufacturing process sustainability and efficiency
□ Transportation and carbon footprint evaluation
□ Installation impact and waste generation assessment
□ End-of-life disposal and recycling capability

Performance Criteria:
□ Energy efficiency and thermal performance
□ Durability and longevity assessment
□ Weather resistance and Florida climate suitability
□ Maintenance requirements and lifecycle costs
□ Warranty and manufacturer support availability
```

### 5.2 Material Certification and Verification
```
Required Certifications:
- ENERGY STAR qualification and rating verification
- Cool Roof Rating Council (CRRC) certification
- Green building standard compliance (LEED, NGBS)
- Manufacturer sustainability certification and documentation
- Third-party performance verification and testing

Verification Process:
1. Certification document collection and verification
2. Third-party testing result review and validation
3. Manufacturer sustainability practice assessment
4. Installation requirement and training verification
5. Warranty and support service evaluation
```

### 5.3 Customer Assessment and Recommendation
```
Customer Needs Analysis:
- Energy efficiency goal identification and quantification
- Budget and financing option assessment
- Aesthetic preference and architectural compatibility
- Maintenance capability and preference evaluation
- Long-term value and ROI expectation assessment

Recommendation Development:
- Customized sustainable solution design and specification
- Energy efficiency and cost savings projection
- Incentive and tax credit maximization strategy
- Installation timeline and process coordination
- Long-term maintenance and service planning
```

### 5.4 Implementation and Installation
```
Installation Requirements:
- Specialized training and certification for sustainable materials
- Modified installation techniques and quality standards
- Environmental protection during installation process
- Performance verification and testing procedures
- Customer education and maintenance training delivery

Quality Assurance:
- Installation quality verification and performance testing
- Energy efficiency measurement and validation
- Warranty registration and documentation completion
- Customer satisfaction and education verification
- Long-term performance monitoring setup
```
