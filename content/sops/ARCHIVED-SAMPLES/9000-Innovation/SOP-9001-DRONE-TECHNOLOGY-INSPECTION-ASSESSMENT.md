# SOP-9001: Drone Technology for Inspection and Assessment

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for the safe, legal, and effective use of drone technology for roofing inspection and assessment activities at Florida First Roofing LLC, ensuring regulatory compliance, safety standards, and enhanced service capabilities while maintaining professional insurance and liability protection.

### Scope
- Drone operation certification and regulatory compliance
- Pre-flight planning and safety assessment procedures
- Inspection methodology and data collection protocols
- Image and data analysis and documentation procedures
- Customer communication and service integration
- Equipment maintenance and calibration requirements

## 2. Regulatory Framework

### Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Requirements
- Part 107: Small Unmanned Aircraft Systems (sUAS) regulations
- Remote Pilot Certificate requirements and maintenance
- Airspace authorization and flight operation compliance
- Aircraft registration and identification requirements

### Florida State Aviation Regulations
- State drone operation laws and restrictions
- Privacy protection and property rights compliance
- Local municipality drone ordinances and restrictions
- Insurance and liability requirement compliance

### Professional Liability and Insurance
- Commercial drone operation insurance requirements
- Professional liability coverage for inspection services
- Property damage and third-party liability protection
- Equipment and data protection insurance coverage

### Data Protection and Privacy
- Customer data protection and confidentiality requirements
- Image and video privacy and consent procedures
- Data storage and security compliance standards
- Information sharing and disclosure protocols

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 Regulatory Compliance and Certification
```
FAA Part 107 Requirements:
□ Valid Remote Pilot Certificate maintenance
□ Biennial flight review and recurrent training
□ Aircraft registration and identification compliance
□ Airspace authorization and flight operation approval
□ Operational limitation compliance and documentation

Florida State Compliance:
□ State drone operation law adherence
□ Local municipality ordinance compliance
□ Privacy protection and property rights respect
□ Insurance and liability coverage verification
□ Professional licensing and certification maintenance
```

### 5.2 Equipment and Technology Standards
```
Drone Platform Requirements:
- Professional-grade camera and imaging capability
- GPS navigation and flight stabilization systems
- Real-time video transmission and monitoring
- Weather resistance and operational durability
- Regulatory compliance and safety features

Inspection Technology:
- High-resolution visible light imaging
- Thermal imaging capability for moisture detection
- 3D mapping and measurement capabilities
- Video recording and documentation features
- Data storage and transmission security
```

### 5.3 Flight Planning and Safety Assessment
```
Pre-Flight Planning Checklist:
□ Customer property survey and access coordination
□ Airspace analysis and authorization verification
□ Weather condition assessment and flight feasibility
□ Safety hazard identification and mitigation planning
□ Emergency procedure review and preparation

Safety Assessment:
- Obstacle identification and avoidance planning
- Personnel safety and exclusion zone establishment
- Equipment malfunction and emergency landing procedures
- Communication protocol and coordination procedures
- Insurance and liability verification and documentation
```

### 5.4 Inspection Methodology
```
Inspection Protocol:
1. Systematic roof coverage and documentation
2. Detailed damage and defect identification
3. Thermal imaging for moisture and insulation assessment
4. 3D mapping for measurement and planning
5. Video documentation for customer education

Data Collection Standards:
- High-resolution imagery with GPS coordination
- Multiple angle documentation for comprehensive coverage
- Thermal imaging overlay and analysis
- Measurement and scaling accuracy verification
- Metadata documentation and organization
```
