# SOP-0055: LMS Compliance and Audit Management

**FLORIDA FIRST ROOFING LLC**
**Standard Operating Procedure**

---

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

**Purpose:** This SOP establishes standardized procedures for managing compliance requirements, conducting audits, maintaining regulatory documentation, and ensuring adherence to all applicable standards within the Learning Management System (LMS) for Florida First Roofing LLC.

**Scope:** This procedure applies to all LMS compliance activities including regulatory requirement management, internal and external audit preparation, documentation maintenance, compliance reporting, and continuous compliance monitoring across all training programs and organizational levels.

---

## 2. REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

- OSHA 29 CFR 1926 Training Documentation and Audit Requirements
- Florida Building Code Continuing Education Compliance (Chapter 489)
- EPA RRP Training Record Maintenance (40 CFR 745)
- NRCA Training Standards and Audit Requirements
- Educational Privacy and Data Protection (FERPA, GDPR)
- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Compliance
- Quality Assurance Standards for Training Programs
- State and Federal Employment Training Regulations

---

## 3. DEFINITIONS

- **Compliance Framework:** Systematic approach to meeting regulatory and policy requirements
- **Audit Trail:** Complete chronological record of training activities and transactions
- **Documentation Control:** Systematic management of training records and evidence
- **Compliance Gap:** Difference between current state and required compliance standards
- **Remediation Plan:** Structured approach to address compliance deficiencies
- **Risk Assessment:** Evaluation of compliance-related risks and mitigation strategies

## 6. PROCEDURES

### A. Compliance Framework Development

1. **Regulatory Requirement Identification:**
   - Identify all applicable regulatory and compliance requirements
   - Map requirements to specific training programs and activities
   - Document compliance obligations and deadlines
   - Establish compliance monitoring and verification procedures
   - Create regulatory change monitoring and update processes

2. **Compliance Policy Development:**
   - Develop comprehensive compliance policies and procedures
   - Establish compliance standards and performance criteria
   - Define roles, responsibilities, and accountability measures
   - Create compliance training and awareness programs
   - Implement compliance monitoring and reporting systems

3. **Risk Assessment and Management:**
   - Conduct comprehensive compliance risk assessments
   - Identify potential compliance failures and consequences
   - Develop risk mitigation strategies and contingency plans
   - Establish risk monitoring and early warning systems
   - Create incident response and remediation procedures

### B. Compliance Monitoring and Verification

1. **Ongoing Compliance Monitoring:**
   - Implement continuous monitoring of compliance indicators
   - Track training completion and certification requirements
   - Monitor data security and privacy protection measures
   - Verify adherence to documentation and record keeping standards
   - Assess compliance with accessibility and accommodation requirements

2. **Compliance Verification Activities:**
   - Conduct regular compliance assessments and reviews
   - Verify training program adherence to regulatory standards
   - Review documentation and record keeping practices
   - Assess system security and data protection measures
   - Validate compliance reporting and audit trail integrity

3. **Performance Measurement:**
   - Establish compliance key performance indicators (KPIs)
   - Monitor compliance metrics and trending analysis
   - Benchmark compliance performance against standards
   - Generate compliance dashboards and status reports
   - Identify compliance improvement opportunities

### C. Audit Management

1. **Internal Audit Program:**
   - Develop comprehensive internal audit schedule and scope
   - Conduct regular internal compliance audits
   - Document audit findings and recommendations
   - Track corrective action implementation and effectiveness
   - Report audit results to management and stakeholders

2. **External Audit Preparation:**
   - Prepare for regulatory and third-party audits
   - Compile required documentation and evidence
   - Coordinate with auditors and inspection teams
   - Support audit activities and information requests
   - Manage audit findings and corrective action responses

3. **Audit Documentation Management:**
   - Maintain comprehensive audit trail documentation
   - Organize and index audit evidence and supporting materials
   - Ensure document accessibility and retrieval capabilities
   - Implement document security and confidentiality measures
   - Establish document retention and disposal procedures

### D. Documentation and Record Management

1. **Training Record Management:**
   - Maintain complete and accurate training records
   - Implement standardized documentation formats and procedures
   - Ensure record completeness and data integrity
   - Establish secure storage and backup procedures
   - Implement access controls and confidentiality measures

2. **Compliance Documentation Control:**
   - Develop standardized documentation templates and formats
   - Implement version control and change management procedures
   - Maintain current and historical compliance documentation
   - Ensure document accuracy and consistency
   - Establish document review and approval processes

3. **Data Retention and Disposal:**
   - Implement regulatory-compliant data retention schedules
   - Establish secure data disposal and destruction procedures
   - Maintain data retention logs and disposal documentation
   - Ensure compliance with privacy and confidentiality requirements
   - Coordinate with legal and regulatory requirements

### E. Continuous Improvement and Remediation

1. **Compliance Gap Analysis:**
   - Identify gaps between current state and compliance requirements
   - Assess root causes of compliance deficiencies
   - Prioritize improvement initiatives and resource allocation
   - Develop remediation plans and implementation timelines
   - Monitor improvement progress and effectiveness

2. **Corrective Action Management:**
   - Implement systematic corrective action procedures
   - Track corrective action implementation and completion
   - Verify corrective action effectiveness and sustainability
   - Document lessons learned and best practices
   - Prevent recurrence through process improvement

3. **Regulatory Change Management:**
   - Monitor regulatory and standard changes affecting compliance
   - Assess impact of changes on current compliance programs
   - Update policies, procedures, and training content
   - Communicate changes to affected personnel and stakeholders
   - Implement transition plans and timeline management
