# Brevard County Roofing Requirements and Variations

## County-Specific Information
- **County Seat**: Titusville
- **Building Department**: Brevard County Building & Code Enforcement
- **Primary Contact**: (321) 633-2069
- **Online Portal**: www.brevardfl.gov/BuildingDepartment
- **Hurricane Risk Level**: High (Atlantic Coast Exposure)

## Permit Requirements

### Residential Roofing Permits
- **New Construction**: Required for all new roof installations
- **Re-roofing**: Required for complete roof replacement over 100 sq ft
- **Repair Permits**: Required for repairs exceeding $1,000 or 25% of roof area
- **Emergency Permits**: Available for storm damage (72-hour temporary permits)

### Permit Application Process
- **Online Submission**: Available through county portal
- **Required Documents**:
  - Site plan with roof layout
  - Structural drawings (if structural modifications)
  - Product approval documentation
  - Contractor licensing verification
- **Processing Time**: 3-5 business days for standard permits
- **Fees**: $0.85 per $100 of project value (minimum $25)

### Special Permit Considerations
- **Historic Properties**: Additional review required for historic districts
- **Coastal Properties**: Enhanced wind load requirements (140+ mph)
- **Mobile Homes**: Special permitting process and requirements
- **Commercial Properties**: Separate commercial permit requirements

## Building Code Requirements

### Florida Building Code Adoption
- **Current Code**: 2020 Florida Building Code (7th Edition)
- **Local Amendments**: Brevard County specific amendments apply
- **High Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ)**: Applies to coastal areas only
- **Wind Load Requirements**: 140-150 mph design wind speeds

### Roofing Material Requirements
- **Shingles**: Must have Miami-Dade or Florida Product Approval
- **Metal Roofing**: NOA (Notice of Acceptance) required
- **Tile Roofing**: Impact resistance testing required
- **Underlayment**: Secondary water barrier required

### Installation Standards
- **Fastener Requirements**: Enhanced fastening for wind uplift
- **Ventilation**: 1 sq ft ventilation per 150 sq ft roof area
- **Flashing**: Corrosion-resistant materials mandatory
- **Gutters**: Impact-resistant materials recommended

## Inspection Requirements

### Mandatory Inspections
1. **Rough Inspection**: After decking repair/replacement before underlayment
2. **Final Inspection**: Upon project completion
3. **Re-inspection**: Required if initial inspection fails ($75 fee)

### Inspection Scheduling
- **Notice Period**: 24-hour advance notice required
- **Inspection Hours**: Monday-Friday 7:30 AM - 3:30 PM
- **Emergency Inspections**: Available for emergency repairs
- **Failed Inspection**: Re-inspection required within 30 days

### Inspector Access Requirements
- **Safe Access**: Ladder access to roof required
- **Site Preparation**: Clear access paths, organized work area
- **Documentation**: All permits and approvals on-site
- **Contractor Presence**: Licensed contractor must be present

## Contractor Licensing Requirements

### State and Local Requirements
- **Florida License**: Roofing contractor license required
- **Brevard Registration**: County contractor registration required
- **Insurance Requirements**:
  - General Liability: $300,000 minimum
  - Workers' Compensation: Required if employees
- **Bond Requirements**: $10,000 contractor bond

### Specialty Requirements
- **Storm Damage**: Public adjuster licensing for insurance work
- **Historic Properties**: Additional certification for historic work
- **Commercial Roofing**: Commercial contractor classification required

## Environmental and Safety Considerations

### Manatee Protection Zones
- **Coastal Areas**: Special noise restrictions during manatee season
- **Equipment Restrictions**: Quiet equipment required in sensitive areas
- **Timing Restrictions**: Limited work hours during nesting seasons

### Sea Turtle Protection
- **Lighting Restrictions**: Coastal lighting regulations during nesting season (May-October)
- **Beach Access**: Special permits for beach-adjacent properties
- **Construction Timing**: Restrictions during turtle nesting season

### Storm Water Management
- **Runoff Control**: Sediment and erosion control required
- **Disposal Requirements**: Proper disposal of roofing materials
- **Environmental Permits**: Required for projects near waterways

- **48-Hour Notice**: Secure all loose materials and equipment
- **Evacuation Zones**: Know evacuation requirements for coastal areas

### Post-Storm Procedures
- **Damage Assessment**: Systematic assessment of all active projects
- **Emergency Repairs**: Priority given to life safety issues
- **Insurance Coordination**: Immediate adjuster notification required
- **Permit Modifications**: Emergency permit procedures available

### Recovery Operations
- **Debris Management**: County debris removal coordination
- **Material Availability**: Supply chain coordination with suppliers
- **Workforce Management**: Additional crew scheduling and coordination
- **Customer Communication**: Enhanced communication during recovery
