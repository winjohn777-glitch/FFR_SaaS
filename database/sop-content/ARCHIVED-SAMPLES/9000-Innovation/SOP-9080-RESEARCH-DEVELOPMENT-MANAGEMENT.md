## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for research and development (R&D) management to ensure Florida First Roofing LLC maintains technological leadership, drives innovation, and creates competitive advantages through systematic research, development, and implementation of advanced roofing technologies, materials, and methodologies.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Research and development team members
- Chief Technology Officer and innovation leadership
- Department heads and technical specialists
- External research partners and consultants
- Product and technology vendors
- Academic and industry research collaborators

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Research and Development (R&D)**: Systematic investigation and experimentation to advance knowledge and create innovation
**Innovation Pipeline**: Structured process for managing research projects from concept to commercialization
**Technology Transfer**: Process of applying research findings to practical business applications
**Intellectual Property**: Legally protected innovations, inventions, and proprietary technologies
**Proof of Concept**: Preliminary demonstration of project feasibility and potential value

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Chief Technology Officer
- Oversee overall R&D strategy and program management
- Allocate resources and approve research projects
- Coordinate with external research partners
- Ensure alignment between R&D and business objectives

### 4.2 R&D Manager
- Manage daily R&D operations and project coordination
- Lead research project planning and execution
- Coordinate with department heads on research needs
- Monitor research progress and performance

### 4.3 Research Scientists and Engineers
- Conduct research experiments and investigations
- Develop and test new technologies and materials
- Document research findings and recommendations
- Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders

### 4.4 Project Managers
- Coordinate research project logistics and timeline
- Manage research budgets and resource allocation
- Facilitate communication between research teams
- Monitor project milestones and deliverables
