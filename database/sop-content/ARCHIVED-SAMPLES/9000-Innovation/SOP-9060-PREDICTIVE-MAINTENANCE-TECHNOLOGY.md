## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for implementing and managing predictive maintenance technologies that utilize data analytics, machine learning, and IoT sensors to anticipate and prevent roofing system failures before they occur.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all predictive maintenance programs, technologies, data analysis systems, and proactive service offerings implemented across residential and commercial roofing projects by Florida First Roofing LLC.

## Definitions

- **Predictive Maintenance**: Maintenance strategy that uses data analysis to predict when equipment failure might occur
- **Condition Monitoring**: Continuous or periodic measurement and interpretation of system parameters to detect changes
- **Failure Mode Analysis**: Systematic examination of potential failure points and their causes in roofing systems
- **Remaining Useful Life (RUL)**: Estimated time before a component or system reaches end of useful life
- **Machine Learning Algorithm**: Computer program that learns patterns from data to make predictions without explicit programming

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC employs advanced predictive maintenance technologies to maximize roof system lifespan, minimize unexpected failures, reduce maintenance costs, and deliver superior customer value through proactive service delivery.
