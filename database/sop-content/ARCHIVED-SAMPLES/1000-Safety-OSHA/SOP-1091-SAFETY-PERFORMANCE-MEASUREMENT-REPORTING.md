# SOP-1091: Safety Performance Measurement and Reporting

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### Purpose
To establish comprehensive procedures for measuring, monitoring, and reporting safety performance within Florida First Roofing LLC operations, ensuring continuous improvement of safety programs, regulatory compliance, and effective communication of safety metrics to stakeholders while addressing Florida's unique operational challenges and regulatory environment.

### Scope
This SOP applies to all safety performance measurement activities including:
- Leading and lagging safety indicators
- Injury and illness tracking and analysis
- Near-miss and hazard reporting systems
- Safety audit and inspection metrics
- Training completion and effectiveness measures
- Regulatory compliance monitoring
- Cost-benefit analysis of safety programs
- Benchmarking and comparative analysis

## 2. DEFINITIONS

**Leading Indicators**: Proactive metrics that measure activities and conditions that influence future safety performance.

**Lagging Indicators**: Reactive metrics that measure the outcomes of past safety performance.

**Total Recordable Incident Rate (TRIR)**: The number of recordable incidents per 100 full-time equivalent workers per year.

**Days Away, Restricted, or Transferred (DART)**: Rate of cases involving days away from work, restricted work, or job transfer.

**Lost Time Incident Rate (LTIR)**: Rate of incidents resulting in lost workdays.

**Near-Miss Ratio**: The relationship between near-miss events and actual incidents.

**Safety Climate**: The perceived state of safety in the workplace at a specific time.
