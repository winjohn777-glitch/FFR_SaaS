# SOP-1099: Safety Legal Compliance and Regulatory Management

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### Purpose
To establish comprehensive procedures for managing legal compliance and regulatory requirements related to safety and health within Florida First Roofing LLC operations, ensuring adherence to federal, state, and local regulations while maintaining current knowledge of regulatory changes and implementing effective compliance strategies specific to Florida's regulatory environment.

### Scope
This SOP applies to all regulatory compliance activities including:
- Federal OSHA compliance management
- Florida state safety regulations
- Local building and safety codes
- Environmental regulations
- Workers' compensation requirements
- DOT transportation regulations
- Industry-specific standards and certifications
- Contract and customer safety requirements

## 2. DEFINITIONS

**Regulatory Compliance**: Adherence to laws, regulations, guidelines, and specifications relevant to business operations.

**Compliance Management System**: A structured framework for managing regulatory requirements and ensuring ongoing compliance.

**Regulatory Register**: A documented list of applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

**Compliance Audit**: A systematic examination of compliance with regulatory requirements.

**Legal Obligation**: A requirement imposed by law or regulation that must be met.

**Regulatory Change Management**: The process of identifying, evaluating, and implementing changes in regulatory requirements.
