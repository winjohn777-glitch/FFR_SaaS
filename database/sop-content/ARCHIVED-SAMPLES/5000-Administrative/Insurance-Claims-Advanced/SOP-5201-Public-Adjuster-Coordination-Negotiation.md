# SOP-5201: Public Adjuster Coordination and Negotiation

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive public adjuster coordination and negotiation procedures for Florida First Roofing LLC to optimize collaboration with public adjusters, maximize claim settlements, ensure professional cooperation, and maintain competitive positioning while delivering superior customer outcomes.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all interactions with public adjusters representing Florida First Roofing LLC customers, including initial coordination, joint damage assessments, estimate negotiations, claim advocacy, and settlement optimization for insurance claims involving public adjuster representation.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Public Adjuster Relations Manager**: Oversee all public adjuster relationships and coordination activities
- **Senior Negotiation Specialist**: Lead negotiations and settlement discussions with public adjusters
- **Technical Documentation Coordinator**: Provide technical support and documentation for adjuster collaboration
- **Customer Advocacy Representative**: Ensure customer interests are protected during adjuster negotiations
- **Legal Compliance Monitor**: Ensure all interactions comply with Florida public adjuster regulations
- **Quality Assurance Coordinator**: Monitor collaboration effectiveness and relationship quality

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Initial Coordination and Relationship Establishment (Day 1-2)
1. **Public Adjuster Verification and Assessment**:
   - Verify public adjuster licensing with Florida Department of Financial Services
   - Review adjuster reputation, track record, and professional references
   - Assess adjuster specialization and experience with similar claim types
   - Evaluate adjuster's relationship with relevant insurance carriers
   - Establish initial communication protocols and professional boundaries

2. **Collaborative Framework Development**:
   - **Customer Interests Alignment**: Ensure all parties prioritize customer's best interests and maximum recovery
   - **Communication Protocols**: Establish regular communication schedules and information sharing procedures
   - **Documentation Standards**: Agree on documentation requirements and quality standards
   - **Timeline Coordination**: Develop coordinated schedules for assessments, estimates, and negotiations
   - **Conflict Resolution**: Establish procedures for resolving disagreements and maintaining collaboration

### Phase 2: Joint Damage Assessment and Technical Coordination (Day 2-5)
1. **Collaborative Property Assessment**:
   - Conduct joint property inspection with coordinated documentation procedures
   - Share technical expertise and damage assessment methodologies
   - Coordinate with insurance adjusters for comprehensive damage evaluation
   - Develop consensus on damage scope and repair requirements
   - Document areas of agreement and identify any technical disagreements

2. **Technical Documentation Coordination**:
   - **Damage Quantification**: Collaborate on precise damage measurement and quantification
   - **Causation Analysis**: Coordinate technical analysis of damage causation and coverage determination
   - **Repair Methodology**: Develop agreed-upon repair specifications and quality standards
   - **Code Compliance**: Coordinate code upgrade requirements and compliance analysis
   - **Market Pricing**: Share market data and pricing information for accurate cost estimation

### Phase 3: Estimate Development and Negotiation Strategy (Day 3-7)
1. **Collaborative Estimate Development**:
   - Share detailed cost estimates and pricing methodologies with public adjuster
   - Coordinate on material specifications and quality standards for repair estimates
   - Develop alternative repair scenarios and cost-benefit analysis for optimization
   - Ensure estimate accuracy and completeness for insurance carrier presentation
   - Establish pricing transparency and professional credibility for negotiations

2. **Negotiation Strategy Coordination**:
   - Develop coordinated negotiation strategy maximizing customer recovery
   - Identify potential coverage disputes and develop resolution strategies
   - Coordinate timing and presentation of estimates to insurance carriers
   - Establish fallback positions and alternative negotiation approaches
   - Plan for potential supplemental claims and additional damage discovery

### Phase 4: Insurance Carrier Negotiations and Advocacy (Day 5-14)
1. **Coordinated Carrier Presentations**:
   - Present unified front with consistent technical analysis and cost estimates
   - Coordinate technical testimony and expert witness support as needed
   - Support public adjuster presentations with contractor expertise and credibility
   - Provide real-time clarification and technical support during negotiations
   - Maintain professional relationships with insurance adjusters throughout process

2. **Settlement Optimization and Advocacy**:
   - Coordinate settlement negotiations to maximize customer recovery
   - Provide technical support for coverage interpretation and policy application
   - Negotiate payment schedules and milestone releases beneficial to customer
   - Coordinate supplemental claims for additional damage discovered during repairs
   - Ensure settlement terms are favorable for customer and project completion

### Phase 5: Project Execution and Relationship Management (Day 10-30)
1. **Project Coordination and Quality Assurance**:
   - Coordinate repair project execution with agreed-upon specifications and standards
   - Provide regular progress updates to public adjuster and customer
   - Ensure quality control measures meet or exceed negotiated standards
   - Coordinate any necessary supplemental claims during project execution
   - Maintain professional relationship and collaboration throughout project completion

2. **Performance Evaluation and Relationship Development**:
   - Evaluate collaboration effectiveness and identify improvement opportunities
   - Document lessons learned and best practices for future collaborations
   - Maintain ongoing professional relationships for future claim coordination
   - Provide feedback to public adjuster on process efficiency and customer satisfaction
   - Develop referral relationships and mutual business development opportunities

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Estimate discrepancies**: Implement detailed cost breakdown analysis and market data comparison
- **Timeline conflicts**: Develop flexible scheduling and priority coordination procedures
- **Communication breakdowns**: Establish regular check-ins and alternative communication methods
- **Settlement disagreements**: Engage neutral third-party mediation and expert consultation
- **Quality standard disputes**: Implement detailed specifications and photographic documentation

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Address conflicts immediately through direct communication and problem-solving
2. Engage senior management for relationship mediation and conflict resolution
3. Utilize neutral third-party experts for technical disagreement resolution
4. Document all issues and implement process improvements for future collaborations
5. Maintain customer focus and professional standards throughout dispute resolution
