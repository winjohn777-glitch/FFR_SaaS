# SOP-5202: Specialty Insurance Products Management (Wind, Flood, Commercial)

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive specialty insurance products management procedures for Florida First Roofing LLC to effectively handle complex insurance scenarios including wind-only policies, flood insurance claims, commercial insurance products, and specialized coverage situations requiring advanced expertise and coordination.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all specialty insurance product claims including wind-only policies, National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP) claims, private flood insurance, commercial property insurance, umbrella policies, excess coverage, specialty building codes coverage, and multi-peril insurance coordination scenarios.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Specialty Insurance Manager**: Oversee all specialty insurance product claims and coordination
- **Wind Insurance Specialist**: Handle wind-only policies and windstorm coverage coordination
- **Flood Insurance Coordinator**: Manage NFIP and private flood insurance claims
- **Commercial Insurance Analyst**: Handle large commercial and institutional insurance claims
- **Multi-Peril Coordinator**: Coordinate claims involving multiple insurance products and carriers
- **Regulatory Compliance Specialist**: Ensure compliance with specialty insurance regulations

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Specialized Assessment Technology:
- Wind damage assessment tools and anemometer readings for causation analysis
- Flood damage documentation equipment and moisture detection systems
- Commercial building assessment tools for large-scale damage evaluation
- Multi-spectral imaging for flood vs. wind damage differentiation
- Structural engineering software for commercial building analysis

### Documentation and Coordination Systems:
- Specialty insurance carrier portal access and submission systems
- NFIP claim processing software and FEMA coordination systems
- Commercial insurance coordination platforms for multi-carrier claims

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Implement enhanced safety protocols for flood-damaged properties with contamination risks
- Coordinate with environmental specialists for hazardous material assessment in flood zones
- Ensure proper PPE for mold and contamination exposure in flood-damaged structures
- Maintain structural safety assessment for wind-damaged commercial buildings
- Coordinate with emergency services for commercial building access and safety clearance
- Implement fall protection protocols for large commercial roof systems

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Insurance Product Identification and Analysis (Day 1-2)
1. **Comprehensive Policy Review and Analysis**:
   - Identify all applicable insurance products and coverage types
   - Analyze policy language and coverage limitations for specialty products
   - Evaluate deductible structures and policy limits for each product type
   - Assess coordination requirements between multiple insurance products
   - Review exclusions and limitations specific to specialty insurance products

2. **Damage Causation Assessment and Allocation**:
   - **Wind vs. Flood Determination**: Analyze damage patterns to determine causation for proper allocation
   - **Commercial vs. Residential Assessment**: Evaluate coverage differences and documentation requirements
   - **Peril-Specific Analysis**: Determine primary and secondary causes for multi-peril situations
   - **Temporal Analysis**: Document damage progression for proper causation determination
   - **Expert Consultation**: Coordinate with specialists for complex causation scenarios

### Phase 2: Wind Insurance Claims Management (Day 2-5)
1. **Wind-Only Policy Coordination**:
   - Coordinate with Citizens Property Insurance Corporation for wind-only claims
   - Document wind damage specifically and differentiate from other perils
   - Analyze wind speed data and correlation with damage patterns
   - Coordinate with meteorological experts for wind speed verification
   - Ensure proper documentation for wind-specific coverage requirements

2. **Windstorm Coverage Optimization**:
   - Evaluate additional coverage options including code upgrade benefits
   - Coordinate with insurance carriers on temporary housing benefits for wind damage
   - Assess business interruption coverage for commercial wind damage claims
   - Negotiate wind damage settlements with specialized carrier requirements
   - Coordinate supplemental claims for additional wind damage discovered during repairs

### Phase 3: Flood Insurance Claims Coordination (Day 2-7)
1. **NFIP Claims Processing**:
   - Coordinate with FEMA and National Flood Insurance Program requirements
   - Complete specialized NFIP documentation and proof of loss forms
   - Coordinate with NFIP adjusters and provide technical support for assessments
   - Ensure compliance with NFIP repair standards and elevation requirements
   - Navigate NFIP appeals process for disputed coverage determinations

2. **Private Flood Insurance Coordination**:
   - Coordinate with private flood insurance carriers and specialized requirements
   - Analyze coverage differences between NFIP and private flood policies
   - Optimize coverage benefits through private flood insurance advocacy
   - Coordinate with private flood adjusters and provide technical expertise
   - Ensure proper documentation for private flood insurance claim requirements

### Phase 4: Commercial Insurance Claims Management (Day 3-10)
1. **Large Commercial Property Claims**:
   - Coordinate with commercial insurance carriers and specialized adjusters
   - Manage large-scale damage assessment and documentation requirements
   - Coordinate with structural engineers for commercial building analysis
   - Evaluate business interruption and additional expense coverage
   - Manage complex repair coordination for large commercial properties

2. **Multi-Carrier Commercial Coordination**:
   - Coordinate between primary, excess, and umbrella insurance policies
   - Manage complex policy limit coordination and layered coverage scenarios
   - Negotiate with multiple carriers for coordinated claim resolution
   - Coordinate with risk managers and commercial insurance brokers
   - Ensure proper documentation for complex commercial insurance requirements

### Phase 5: Multi-Peril and Complex Coverage Coordination (Day 5-14)
1. **Multi-Carrier Coordination and Optimization**:
   - Coordinate simultaneous claims across multiple insurance products
   - Optimize coverage benefits through strategic claim presentation and timing
   - Negotiate with carriers to avoid coverage gaps and disputes
   - Coordinate payment schedules across multiple insurance products
   - Ensure customer receives maximum available benefits from all applicable coverage

2. **Specialty Coverage Maximization**:
   - Evaluate and pursue specialty coverage including ordinance and law benefits
   - Coordinate green building and sustainable repair coverage when available
   - Assess additional living expense and business interruption benefits
   - Negotiate debris removal and additional coverage benefits
   - Coordinate with experts for specialty coverage interpretation and advocacy

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Causation disputes between wind and flood**: Engage meteorological and engineering experts for analysis
- **NFIP documentation requirements**: Maintain specialized NFIP expertise and FEMA coordination
- **Commercial carrier coordination**: Implement systematic multi-carrier communication protocols
- **Policy interpretation disputes**: Engage legal counsel and insurance coverage experts
- **Coverage allocation conflicts**: Utilize mediation and alternative dispute resolution procedures

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Engage specialized experts immediately for complex causation and coverage issues
2. Coordinate with legal counsel for policy interpretation and coverage disputes
3. Escalate to Specialty Insurance Manager for complex multi-carrier coordination
4. Implement alternative dispute resolution procedures for coverage conflicts
5. Document all challenges and develop specialized procedures for future similar situations
