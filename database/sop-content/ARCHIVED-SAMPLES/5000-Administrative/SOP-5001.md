# SOP-5001: Employee Onboarding and Orientation Program

## 1. Purpose
Establish a comprehensive employee onboarding and orientation program to ensure smooth integration of new hires into Florida First Roofing LLC operations, accelerate productivity, reduce turnover, and promote long-term employee engagement and retention.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all new employees joining Florida First Roofing LLC, including full-time, part-time, temporary, and seasonal workers across all departments and positions, as well as all personnel involved in the onboarding process.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Human Resources Manager**: Oversee complete onboarding program and track completion milestones
- **Direct Supervisors**: Conduct department-specific orientation and job-specific training
- **Safety Coordinator**: Provide comprehensive safety training and certification verification
- **IT Administrator**: Setup technology access and digital tool training
- **Administrative Staff**: Coordinate scheduling, documentation, and facility access
- **Mentors/Buddies**: Provide ongoing support and guidance during initial employment period

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Documentation Systems:
- Employee handbook and policy manuals
- Benefits enrollment packages and insurance information
- Safety training materials and certification tracking systems
- Technology setup guides and access credential documentation
- Department-specific training materials and job aids

### Technology Infrastructure:
- Computer workstations and mobile device configurations
- Software access credentials and training platforms
- Communication systems and collaboration tools
- Safety monitoring and reporting applications
- Project management and scheduling platforms

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Complete safety orientation before any field work activities
- Verify personal protective equipment fitting and training
- Ensure understanding of emergency procedures and evacuation plans
- Document safety training completion and certification status
- Establish safety mentor assignments for high-risk positions
- Review Florida-specific safety regulations and heat illness prevention

- [ ] Verify all pre-employment requirements are completed
- [ ] Prepare workstation and technology access credentials

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: First Day Welcome and Initial Setup (Hours 1-4)
1. **Welcome and Reception**:
   - Greet new employee with positive welcome and introduction
   - Provide company overview and brief history of Florida First Roofing LLC
   - Present employee handbook and key policy information
   - Complete initial paperwork verification and documentation
   - Issue employee identification and security access credentials

2. **Facility Tour and Orientation**:
   - Conduct comprehensive facility tour including office areas, warehouse, and work zones
   - Introduce key personnel and department contacts
   - Review emergency procedures, evacuation routes, and safety equipment locations
   - Explain parking, break areas, and facility amenities
   - Demonstrate time keeping systems and attendance procedures

3. **Technology Setup and Training**:
   - Provide computer access, email setup, and software installation
   - Train on company communication platforms and collaboration tools
   - Introduce project management systems and scheduling applications
   - Setup mobile device access and field technology tools
   - Review IT policies, security requirements, and support procedures

### Phase 2: Company Culture and Benefits Orientation (Hours 5-8)
1. **Company Culture and Values**:
   - **Mission and Vision**: Detailed explanation of company purpose and long-term goals
   - **Core Values**: Customer service excellence, safety commitment, and quality craftsmanship
   - **Company History**: Florida First Roofing heritage and community involvement
   - **Team Dynamics**: Collaboration expectations and communication standards
   - **Performance Expectations**: Quality standards and professional behavior requirements

2. **Benefits and Compensation Overview**:
   - Health insurance options and enrollment procedures
   - Retirement plan participation and company matching programs
   - Paid time off policies and vacation accrual systems
   - Professional development opportunities and tuition assistance
   - Performance incentive programs and advancement pathways

3. **Policies and Procedures Review**:
   - Employee conduct standards and disciplinary procedures
   - Anti-harassment and workplace respect policies
   - Confidentiality and intellectual property protection
   - Social media and external communication guidelines
   - Grievance procedures and open door policies

### Phase 3: Department-Specific Training (Week 1)
1. **Job Role and Responsibilities**:
   - Detailed job description review and performance expectations
   - Department organizational structure and reporting relationships
   - Key projects and current priorities explanation
   - Individual goal setting and performance metric establishment
   - Career development pathway and advancement opportunities

2. **Department Procedures and Systems**:
   - Department-specific software and tool training
   - Quality control procedures and documentation requirements
   - Customer interaction protocols and service standards
   - Safety procedures specific to job function and work environment
   - Communication protocols and reporting procedures

3. **Hands-On Training and Mentoring**:
   - Job shadowing with experienced team members
   - Supervised practice of key job functions and responsibilities
   - Mentor assignment for ongoing support and guidance
   - Initial project assignments with appropriate supervision
   - Feedback sessions and learning progress assessment

### Phase 4: Safety and Compliance Training (Week 1-2)
1. **Comprehensive Safety Training**:
   - **OSHA Standards**: Florida construction safety regulations and federal requirements
   - **Personal Protective Equipment**: Proper selection, use, and maintenance procedures
   - **Fall Protection**: Harness systems, guardrails, and roof safety protocols
   - **Ladder Safety**: Inspection, setup, and safe usage procedures
   - **Heat Illness Prevention**: Florida-specific heat stress recognition and prevention

2. **Emergency Response Training**:
   - Emergency contact procedures and notification systems
   - First aid and CPR training for designated personnel
   - Fire safety and evacuation procedures
   - Severe weather response and hurricane protocols
   - Incident reporting and investigation procedures

3. **Compliance and Legal Requirements**:
   - Florida employment law and worker rights information
   - Workers' compensation procedures and reporting requirements
   - Environmental protection and waste disposal procedures
   - Quality control standards and inspection requirements
   - Customer privacy and confidentiality obligations

### Phase 5: Performance Monitoring and Integration (Month 1-3)
1. **30-Day Check-In and Assessment**:
   - Performance review and goal adjustment session
   - Training effectiveness evaluation and additional needs assessment
   - Workplace integration and team relationship evaluation
   - Benefits enrollment completion and questions resolution
   - Career development planning and next steps discussion

2. **60-Day Evaluation and Feedback**:
   - Comprehensive performance evaluation and feedback session
   - Skills assessment and additional training identification
   - Professional development planning and resource allocation
   - Performance improvement planning if needed
   - Compensation review and adjustment consideration

3. **90-Day Probationary Review**:
   - Final probationary period evaluation and employment confirmation
   - Long-term goal setting and career pathway planning
   - Performance recognition and achievement acknowledgment
   - Ongoing development planning and resource commitment
   - Employee feedback on onboarding process and improvement suggestions

## 8. Quality Control Standards
- Onboarding completion rate: 100% within established timeline
- New employee satisfaction: 4.5/5 rating for onboarding experience
- Training completion: 95% within 30 days of hire date
- 90-day retention rate: 90% of new hires successfully complete probationary period
- Safety training compliance: 100% completion before field work assignment

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Information overload**: Break training into smaller segments with reinforcement sessions
- **Technology difficulties**: Provide additional IT support and simplified training materials
- **Department integration challenges**: Enhance mentor programs and team building activities
- **Scheduling conflicts**: Develop flexible training schedules and alternative delivery methods
- **Cultural adjustment issues**: Increase supervisor check-ins and peer support programs

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Identify specific onboarding challenges through regular feedback collection
2. Adjust training pace and content based on individual learning needs
3. Provide additional mentoring and support resources when needed
4. Escalate persistent issues to HR manager for intervention and resolution
5. Document lessons learned and incorporate improvements into onboarding process
