# SOP-7000: Training Program Development and Management

## 1. Purpose and Scope

### Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for developing, implementing, and managing training programs for Florida First Roofing LLC, ensuring all employees receive appropriate safety, technical, and professional development training in compliance with Florida regulations and industry best practices.

### Scope
- Training program design and curriculum development
- Training delivery methods and scheduling coordination
- Employee competency assessment and certification management
- Training effectiveness measurement and continuous improvement
- Regulatory compliance training requirements and documentation
- Professional development and career advancement planning

## 2. Regulatory Framework

### OSHA Training Requirements
- 29 CFR 1926.95: Personal protective equipment training
- 29 CFR 1926.501: Fall protection training and certification
- 29 CFR 1926.1060: Stairways and ladders training requirements
- OSHA 10-Hour Construction Safety training compliance

### Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation
- Roofing contractor continuing education requirements
- Employee training and competency documentation
- Safety training compliance and verification
- Professional development and certification maintenance

### Florida Building Code Training
- Code compliance training and certification
- Update training for code revisions and amendments
- Inspection and quality control training requirements
- Permit and regulatory process training

### Industry Certification Requirements
- National Roofing Contractors Association (NRCA) certifications
- Manufacturer-specific product training and certification
- Safety certification and renewal requirements
- Professional development and continuing education

## 5. Detailed Procedures

### 5.1 Training Needs Assessment
```
Assessment Process:
1. Regulatory requirement analysis and compliance verification
2. Job role analysis and competency requirement identification
3. Performance gap analysis and improvement opportunity identification
4. Employee feedback collection and training preference assessment
5. Industry best practice research and benchmark comparison

Documentation Requirements:
□ Current competency level assessment
□ Regulatory compliance gap analysis
□ Performance improvement opportunity identification
□ Employee development goal establishment
□ Training priority ranking and resource allocation
```

### 5.2 Curriculum Development
```
Design Framework:
- Learning objectives establishment and outcome definition
- Content development and material creation
- Training method selection and delivery planning
- Assessment criteria development and measurement tools
- Resource requirement identification and allocation

Quality Standards:
- Regulatory compliance verification and integration
- Industry best practice incorporation and alignment
- Adult learning principle application and engagement
- Practical application emphasis and skill development
- Continuous improvement and update mechanisms
```

### 5.3 Training Delivery Methods
```
Classroom Training:
- Safety procedures and regulatory compliance
- Technical knowledge and theory instruction
- Equipment operation and maintenance training
- Customer service and communication skills

Hands-On Training:
- Installation technique practice and skill development
- Safety procedure implementation and verification
- Equipment operation and troubleshooting
- Quality control and inspection techniques

On-the-Job Training:
- Mentorship and experienced worker guidance
- Real-world application and skill reinforcement
- Problem-solving and decision-making development
- Team coordination and communication practice
```

### 5.4 Competency Assessment
```
Assessment Methods:
- Written examinations and knowledge verification
- Practical demonstrations and skill evaluation
- On-the-job performance observation and assessment
- Peer review and team feedback collection
- Customer feedback and service quality evaluation

Certification Requirements:
- Minimum competency score achievement (80%)
- Practical skill demonstration and verification
- Safety protocol compliance and implementation
- Ongoing performance maintenance and improvement
- Recertification and continuing education completion
```
