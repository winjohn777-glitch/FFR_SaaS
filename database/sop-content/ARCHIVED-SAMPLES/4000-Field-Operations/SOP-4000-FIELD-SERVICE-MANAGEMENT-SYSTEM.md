## BLOCK 1: PURPOSE

To establish comprehensive field service management system procedures ensuring efficient mobile workforce coordination, real-time project tracking, customer communication, and service delivery optimization for all Florida First Roofing field operations and service calls.

---

## BLOCK 2: SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Field supervisors and crew leaders
- Mobile workforce and field technicians
- Dispatch and scheduling coordinators
- Customer service representatives
- Operations management and oversight personnel

Service Types Covered:
- Roofing installation and repair services
- Emergency response and storm damage
- Routine maintenance and inspections
- Warranty service and customer support
- Quality control and follow-up services

---

## BLOCK 3: DEFINITIONS

**Field Service Management System:** The integrated mobile platform enabling real-time coordination, tracking, documentation, and communication for field operations including work order management, GPS tracking, customer interaction, and service delivery optimization.

**Mobile Work Order:** The digital assignment containing all necessary information for field personnel to complete customer service including location, requirements, materials, safety protocols, and customer communication details.

**Real-Time Tracking:** The continuous monitoring and reporting of field personnel location, activity status, and progress updates enabling immediate coordination and customer communication.

## BLOCK 5: PROCEDURE

### 5.1 Work Order Creation and Assignment

**Work Order Generation:**
1. Create work order from customer request or project requirement
2. Include detailed scope, specifications, and customer information
3. Assign priority level and estimated completion time
4. Attach relevant documents, photos, and reference materials
5. Configure customer communication and notification preferences

**Resource Assignment:**
1. Evaluate skill requirements and crew availability
2. Assign qualified personnel based on expertise and location
3. Allocate necessary equipment and materials
4. Schedule work order based on priority and customer preferences
5. Send automatic notifications to assigned personnel

### 5.2 Mobile Field Operations

**Work Order Access and Preparation:**
1. Access assigned work orders through mobile application
2. Review scope, requirements, and customer information
3. Verify material availability and equipment readiness
4. Navigate to customer location using GPS integration
5. Conduct pre-work safety assessment and preparation

**Service Execution and Documentation:**
1. Check in at customer location and begin work order
2. Communicate with customer regarding work scope and timeline
3. Execute work according to specifications and quality standards
4. Document progress with photos and detailed notes
5. Complete safety inspections and quality control checks

### 5.3 Customer Communication and Interaction

**Arrival and Introduction:**
1. Contact customer upon arrival at service location
2. Introduce team members and review work scope
3. Address customer questions and concerns
4. Establish communication preferences and contact methods
5. Review safety protocols and site access requirements

**Progress Updates and Completion:**
1. Provide regular progress updates to customer
2. Document any changes or additional requirements
3. Complete final quality inspection with customer
4. Obtain customer approval and satisfaction confirmation
5. Process completion documentation and next steps

### 5.4 System Integration and Reporting

**Real-Time Data Synchronization:**
1. Sync field data with central systems in real-time
2. Update inventory and material usage automatically
3. Process time tracking and labor cost allocation
4. Generate automatic customer notifications and updates
5. Trigger billing and invoicing processes upon completion

**Performance Reporting and Analytics:**
1. Generate field productivity and efficiency reports
2. Analyze customer satisfaction and service quality metrics
3. Monitor safety compliance and incident reporting
4. Track material usage and waste reduction
5. Measure profitability and cost optimization opportunities
