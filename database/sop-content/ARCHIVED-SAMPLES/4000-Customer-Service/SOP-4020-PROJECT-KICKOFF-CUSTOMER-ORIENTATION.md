# SOP-4020: Project Kick-Off and Customer Orientation

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for project kick-off meetings, customer orientation, and initial project communication to ensure clear expectations, smooth project execution, and exceptional customer service from project initiation through completion.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all project kick-off activities, customer orientation procedures, and initial project communication for residential and commercial roofing projects throughout Florida First Roofing operations.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Project Manager**: Lead kick-off meetings and coordinate project initiation
- **Customer Service Representative**: Facilitate customer communication and orientation
- **Sales Representative**: Provide project background and customer relationship continuity
- **Site Supervisor**: Coordinate field operations and logistics planning
- **Administrative Coordinator**: Manage documentation and scheduling support

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Presentation Materials:
- Project presentation templates and visual aids
- Sample materials and product demonstrations
- Project timeline and milestone documentation
- Quality standards and process explanation materials
- Safety information and site protocols

### Documentation Tools:
- Customer orientation packets and informational materials
- Project contracts and specification documents
- Communication tracking and scheduling systems
- Digital presentation equipment and mobile devices
- Customer feedback and satisfaction tracking tools

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Communicate safety protocols and site access requirements to customers
- Establish emergency contact procedures and communication protocols
- Review site safety requirements and customer responsibility areas
- Plan for safe customer access during project visits and inspections
- Coordinate safety information with ongoing construction activities
- Implement visitor safety protocols and personal protective equipment requirements

## 6. Pre-Operation Checklist
- [ ] Review project contract and specifications with all team members
- [ ] Prepare customer orientation materials and presentation
- [ ] Schedule kick-off meeting at convenient time for customer
- [ ] Coordinate project team member availability and participation
- [ ] Verify project timeline and milestone scheduling
- [ ] Prepare project communication systems and contact protocols
- [ ] Review customer service standards and satisfaction expectations

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Pre-Kick-Off Preparation and Team Coordination
1. **Project Team Assembly and Preparation**:
   - **Project Manager**: Overall project coordination and customer relationship
   - **Site Supervisor**: Field operations coordination and crew management
   - **Customer Service Rep**: Customer communication and service coordination
   - **Quality Inspector**: Quality standards and inspection coordination
   - **Administrative Support**: Documentation and scheduling coordination

2. **Customer Information Review and Analysis**:
   - Review customer background, preferences, and special requirements
   - Analyze project scope, timeline, and potential challenges
   - Identify customer communication preferences and availability
   - Review previous customer interactions and service history
   - Prepare customized orientation materials and presentations

3. **Project Documentation and Material Preparation**:
   - Compile project contracts, specifications, and permit documentation
   - Prepare material samples and product demonstration materials
   - Create project timeline with key milestones and decision points
   - Develop communication schedule and progress reporting plan
   - Prepare customer orientation packet with all relevant information

### Phase 2: Kick-Off Meeting Execution and Customer Orientation
1. **Welcome and Team Introduction**:
   - **Professional Introductions**: Present project team members and their roles
   - **Company Overview**: Florida First Roofing values, mission, and service commitment
   - **Project Background**: Review project scope, objectives, and customer goals
   - **Timeline Overview**: Present project schedule and key milestones
   - **Communication Establishment**: Set communication preferences and protocols

2. **Detailed Project Review and Orientation**:
   - **Scope Verification**: Review all project specifications and deliverables
   - **Material Selection**: Confirm material choices and demonstrate quality features
   - **Installation Process**: Explain installation procedures and quality standards
   - **Timeline Details**: Review detailed schedule and customer coordination points
   - **Quality Assurance**: Explain quality control procedures and inspection processes

3. **Customer Service and Communication Framework**:
   - **Communication Schedule**: Establish regular update and progress reporting
   - **Contact Information**: Provide primary and emergency contact details
   - **Customer Portal Access**: Set up online project tracking and communication tools
   - **Feedback Mechanisms**: Establish customer feedback and satisfaction monitoring
   - **Issue Resolution**: Explain problem resolution procedures and escalation process

### Phase 3: Logistics Coordination and Site Preparation
1. **Site Access and Logistics Planning**:
   - **Property Access**: Coordinate site access, keys, and security requirements
   - **Parking and Storage**: Plan crew parking and material storage locations
   - **Utility Coordination**: Identify and coordinate utility location and protection
   - **Neighbor Communication**: Discuss neighbor notification and courtesy procedures
   - **Delivery Scheduling**: Plan material delivery timing and coordination

2. **Safety and Protection Protocols**:
   - **Customer Safety**: Explain safety requirements and restricted access areas
   - **Property Protection**: Review landscape, equipment, and structure protection
   - **Daily Operations**: Explain typical work hours and daily procedures
   - **Emergency Procedures**: Provide emergency contact and response protocols
   - **Insurance Information**: Review insurance coverage and liability protection

3. **Customer Participation and Decision Points**:
   - **Inspection Participation**: Schedule customer participation in quality inspections
   - **Change Order Process**: Explain procedures for project modifications
   - **Progress Reviews**: Schedule regular progress meetings and updates
   - **Final Acceptance**: Explain final inspection and acceptance procedures
   - **Warranty Activation**: Review warranty registration and activation process

### Phase 4: Documentation and Follow-Up Coordination
1. **Customer Orientation Documentation**:
   - **Meeting Minutes**: Document kick-off meeting discussions and agreements
   - **Customer Acknowledgment**: Obtain customer signature on orientation completion
   - **Contact Verification**: Confirm all contact information and preferences
   - **Schedule Confirmation**: Verify project timeline and milestone acceptance
   - **Communication Preferences**: Document preferred communication methods and frequency

2. **Project Initiation and Team Communication**:
   - **Team Briefing**: Communicate customer preferences and special requirements to project team
   - **Schedule Distribution**: Distribute project timeline to all team members
   - **Communication Setup**: Activate customer communication and tracking systems
   - **Quality Standards**: Reinforce quality expectations and customer service standards
   - **Documentation Systems**: Set up project tracking and progress reporting

3. **Customer Follow-Up and Satisfaction Monitoring**:
   - **Initial Follow-Up**: Contact customer within 24 hours of kick-off meeting
   - **Satisfaction Survey**: Conduct kick-off satisfaction survey and feedback collection
   - **Issue Resolution**: Address any immediate concerns or questions
   - **Expectation Alignment**: Verify customer understanding and satisfaction with orientation
   - **Relationship Building**: Continue relationship development and trust building

## 8. Quality Control Standards
- Customer satisfaction: 95% customer satisfaction rating for kick-off process
- Communication clarity: 100% customer understanding of project scope and timeline
- Documentation completeness: 100% complete orientation packet delivery
- Team coordination: 100% project team preparation and participation
- Follow-up timeliness: 100% follow-up contact within 24 hours of kick-off

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Kick-Off Issues:
- **Schedule conflicts**: Coordinate alternative meeting times and formats
- **Communication problems**: Clarify preferences and establish backup protocols
- **Scope confusion**: Review contracts and provide detailed explanations
- **Timeline concerns**: Address scheduling issues and adjust as necessary
- **Team availability**: Ensure key team member participation or representation

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Document any issues or concerns identified during kick-off process
2. Implement immediate solutions to address customer concerns
3. Follow up with customer to verify satisfaction with issue resolution
4. Update kick-off procedures to prevent similar issues in future projects
5. Maintain customer satisfaction focus throughout resolution process
