## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes comprehensive protocols for advocating on behalf of customers during insurance claim processes, ensuring fair settlements, proper coverage utilization, and maximum customer satisfaction throughout the claims journey.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all customer service representatives, insurance specialists, adjusters, project managers, and legal support staff involved in insurance claim advocacy and customer representation.

## Definitions

- **Customer Advocacy**: Active representation of customer interests in insurance claim processes
- **Claim Advocacy**: Professional support services provided to customers during insurance claim proceedings
- **Coverage Analysis**: Detailed review of insurance policy terms and applicable coverage
- **Settlement Negotiation**: Professional representation in claim settlement discussions
- **Documentation Support**: Comprehensive evidence gathering and presentation for claim substantiation

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC serves as a dedicated advocate for customers throughout the entire insurance claim process, ensuring customers receive fair treatment, appropriate coverage, and maximum entitled benefits while maintaining ethical and professional standards.

## Procedure

### 4.1 Initial Claim Assessment and Customer Advocacy Setup

#### 4.1.1 Customer Consultation and Intake
1. **Damage Assessment and Documentation**
   - Comprehensive property damage evaluation
   - Professional photo and video documentation
   - Detailed written damage assessment report
   - Emergency damage mitigation recommendations
   - Initial repair scope and cost estimation

2. **Insurance Policy Analysis**
   - Complete policy review and interpretation
   - Coverage limits and deductible identification
   - Exclusion analysis and applicability assessment
   - Previous claim history review
   - Special provisions and endorsement evaluation

3. **Customer Education and Expectation Setting**
   - Insurance claim process explanation
   - Customer rights and responsibilities overview
   - Timeline and milestone communication
   - Advocacy service scope definition
   - Fee structure and payment terms clarification

#### 4.1.2 Advocacy Team Assignment
1. **Specialist Allocation**
   - Primary customer advocate assignment
   - Insurance specialist pairing
   - Technical expert designation
   - Legal counsel availability confirmation
   - Project manager coordination setup

2. **Customer Communication Plan**
   - Preferred communication method establishment
   - Update frequency and format agreement
   - Emergency contact protocol setup
   - Document sharing system configuration
   - Progress tracking dashboard access provision
