## Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes protocols for conducting virtual consultations and providing remote services to customers, ensuring comprehensive service delivery when in-person meetings are not feasible or preferred.

## Scope

This SOP applies to all customer service representatives, sales personnel, project managers, and technical staff involved in virtual customer interactions and remote service delivery.

## Definitions

- **Virtual Consultation**: Remote meeting using video conferencing technology for customer service delivery
- **Remote Assessment**: Property evaluation using digital tools and customer-provided information
- **Digital Documentation**: Electronic capture and management of consultation materials
- **Virtual Reality (VR) Tours**: Immersive digital property walkthroughs
- **Augmented Reality (AR) Visualization**: Digital overlay of roofing solutions on real property images

## Policy

Florida First Roofing LLC provides comprehensive virtual consultation services to accommodate customer preferences, safety requirements, and logistical constraints while maintaining service quality standards.

## Procedure

### 4.1 Virtual Consultation Preparation

#### 4.1.1 Technology Setup
1. **Platform Selection**
   - Primary: Zoom Professional
   - Secondary: Microsoft Teams
   - Mobile backup: FaceTime or WhatsApp Video
   - Test all systems 30 minutes before consultation

2. **Equipment Verification**
   - High-definition camera (minimum 1080p)
   - Quality microphone with noise cancellation
   - Stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps upload)
   - Backup power source (UPS)
   - Secondary device for screen sharing

3. **Environmental Setup**
   - Professional background or virtual backdrop
   - Optimal lighting (ring light recommended)
   - Quiet, interruption-free environment
   - Company branding visible
   - Reference materials readily accessible

#### 4.1.2 Pre-Consultation Planning
1. **Customer Information Review**
   - Previous interaction history
   - Property details and location
   - Specific service requirements
   - Customer technology capabilities
   - Preferred communication style

2. **Material Preparation**
   - Digital presentation materials
   - Sample portfolios and case studies
   - Pricing worksheets and calculators
   - Virtual product catalogs
   - Interactive design tools

### 4.2 Virtual Consultation Execution

#### 4.2.1 Initial Connection and Setup
1. **Meeting Initiation**
   - Join meeting 5 minutes early
   - Test audio and video quality
   - Verify screen sharing capabilities
   - Welcome customer professionally
   - Confirm meeting agenda and duration

2. **Technology Assistance**
   - Guide customer through platform features
   - Troubleshoot technical issues patiently
   - Provide alternative connection methods if needed
   - Ensure customer comfort with technology
   - Record meeting if consented and beneficial

#### 4.2.2 Consultation Process
1. **Introduction and Rapport Building**
   - Professional introduction with credentials
   - Company background and expertise overview
   - Service area and specialization explanation
   - Customer needs assessment questions
   - Expectation setting for virtual process

2. **Property Assessment Methodology**
   - **Customer-Guided Tour**: Direct customer to show property areas
   - **Photo/Video Review**: Analyze customer-provided imagery
   - **Satellite Imagery**: Use Google Earth Pro for aerial views
   - **Drone Footage**: Review if previously captured
   - **AR Measurement Tools**: Guide customer through mobile measurement apps

3. **Service Presentation**
   - Customized solution proposals
   - Material options with visual aids
   - Interactive design demonstrations
   - Cost breakdown and financing options
   - Timeline and process explanation

### 4.3 Remote Assessment Techniques

#### 4.3.1 Digital Property Evaluation
1. **Image Analysis Requirements**
   - Minimum 20 high-resolution photos per roof section
   - Multiple angles including close-ups and overviews
   - Interior shots of ceilings and attic spaces
   - Documentation of existing damage or wear
   - Comparison photos with reference objects for scale

2. **Video Walkthrough Protocol**
   - Structured 15-20 minute property tour
   - Narrated explanation of observed conditions
   - Focus on problem areas and access points
   - Documentation of surrounding property features
   - Safety hazard identification

3. **Measurement and Documentation**
   - Customer-guided measurements using mobile apps
   - Verification through satellite imagery analysis
   - Cross-reference with county assessor records
   - Photo documentation of measurement process
   - Digital sketch creation for complex geometries

#### 4.3.2 Advanced Remote Tools
1. **Drone Integration**
   - Coordinate customer-operated drone flights
   - Provide flight path guidance for optimal imagery
   - Real-time video consultation during flights
   - Post-flight image analysis and markup
   - Integration with assessment documentation

2. **Virtual Reality Applications**
   - VR headset loan program for qualified customers
   - Immersive property walkthroughs
   - Design visualization in virtual environment
   - Material selection in simulated conditions
   - Customer experience enhancement

## Performance Standards

### 4.7 Service Level Agreements

#### 4.7.1 Response Time Metrics
- Virtual consultation scheduling: Within 24 hours
- Technical setup assistance: Within 15 minutes
- Emergency remote support: Within 2 hours
- Follow-up documentation: Within 4 hours
- Digital proposal delivery: Within 24 hours

#### 4.7.2 Quality Metrics
- Customer satisfaction rating: Minimum 4.5/5.0
- Technical issue resolution: 95% success rate
- Consultation completion rate: 90% minimum
- Conversion rate: Maintain equivalent to in-person
- Customer retention: 85% minimum for virtual-only customers
