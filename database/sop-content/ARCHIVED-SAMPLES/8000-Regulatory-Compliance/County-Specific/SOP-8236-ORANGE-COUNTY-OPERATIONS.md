# SOP-8236: Orange County Roofing Operations

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for conducting roofing operations in Orange County, Florida, ensuring compliance with theme park industry requirements, international airport coordination, tourism infrastructure, rapid growth management, and protocols for Florida First Roofing LLC's certified roofing services in Central Florida's hub.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel conducting roofing operations, inspections, repairs, and installations within Orange County boundaries, including Orlando, Winter Park, Apopka, Ocoee, Winter Garden, Windermere, Bay Lake (Disney), Lake Buena Vista, and areas around major theme parks and tourist attractions.

## 3. Definitions
- **Orange County Building Services**: Local authority for building permits and inspections
- **Theme Park Districts**: Special districts for Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, SeaWorld
- **Orlando International Airport (MCO)**: Major international aviation hub
- **Tourist Corridor**: I-4 corridor with major attractions and hotels
- **Reedy Creek Improvement District**: Special governmental district (now Central Florida Tourism Oversight District)

## 4. Responsibilities
- **County Operations Manager**: Overall compliance with Orange County regulations
- **Theme Park Operations Coordinator**: Disney, Universal, SeaWorld coordination
- **Airport Operations Specialist**: Orlando International Airport area coordination
- **Tourism Infrastructure Manager**: Hotel, resort, and attraction facility coordination
- **Rapid Growth Coordinator**: Management of high-volume construction coordination

## 5. Prerequisites
- Active contractor license with Orange County
- Theme park vendor certifications and security clearances
- Airport area security clearances and certifications
- Current liability insurance meeting tourism industry requirements ($3M minimum)
- Theme park and entertainment industry safety certifications
