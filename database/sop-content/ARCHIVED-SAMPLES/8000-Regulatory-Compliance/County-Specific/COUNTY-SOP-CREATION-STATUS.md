## Standard SOP Template Elements
Each county SOP includes these 14 sections:
1. Purpose
2. Scope
3. Definitions
4. Responsibilities
5. Prerequisites
6. Materials and Equipment
7. Procedure (9 subsections covering unique county requirements)
8. Quality Standards
9. Safety Requirements
10. Environmental Considerations
11. Documentation
12. Training Requirements
13. Related SOPs
14. Revision History

## County-Specific Focus Areas
- **Building Code Requirements**: County amendments and local standards
- **Permit Procedures**: Local application and inspection processes
- **Hurricane Zone Classifications**: Wind loads and special requirements
- **Local Licensing**: Contractor registration and requirements
- **Environmental Regulations**: County-specific environmental protection
- **Historic Districts**: Preservation requirements where applicable
- **HOA Coordination**: Community association procedures
- **Emergency Response**: County-specific emergency protocols
- **Utility Coordination**: Local utility companies and requirements
- **Safety Regulations**: Beyond state requirements
