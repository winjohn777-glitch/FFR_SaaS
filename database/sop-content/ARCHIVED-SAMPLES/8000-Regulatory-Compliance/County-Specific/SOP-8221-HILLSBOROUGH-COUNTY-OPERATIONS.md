# SOP-8221: Hillsborough County Roofing Operations

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for conducting roofing operations in Hillsborough County, Florida, ensuring compliance with major metropolitan requirements, port operations coordination, sports venue management, university coordination, and protocols for Florida First Roofing LLC's certified roofing services in the Tampa Bay area.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel conducting roofing operations, inspections, repairs, and installations within Hillsborough County boundaries, including Tampa, Plant City, Temple Terrace, and unincorporated areas around major facilities including Tampa International Airport, Port Tampa Bay, Raymond James Stadium, and University of South Florida.

## 3. Definitions
- **Hillsborough County Building Review Services**: Local authority for unincorporated areas
- **Tampa International Airport (TPA)**: Major aviation hub requiring coordination
- **Port Tampa Bay**: Major cargo and cruise port operations
- **Sports and Entertainment Venues**: Professional sports and major event facilities
- **University of South Florida (USF)**: Major research university coordination

## 4. Responsibilities
- **County Operations Manager**: Overall compliance with Hillsborough County regulations
- **Airport Operations Coordinator**: Tampa International Airport area coordination
- **Port Operations Manager**: Port Tampa Bay and marine industry coordination
- **Sports Venue Specialist**: Stadium and arena coordination
- **University Liaison**: USF and educational facility coordination

## 5. Prerequisites
- Active contractor license with Hillsborough County and City of Tampa
- Airport area security clearances and certifications
- Port facility security clearances (TWIC card)
- Sports venue contractor certifications
- University vendor registration and clearances

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Tampa International Airport Coordination
1. **TPA Area Operations**
   - Obtain required airport security clearances (SIDA badges)
   - Follow strict FAA height restriction requirements
   - Coordinate with Hillsborough County Aviation Authority
   - Implement noise control during airport operations
   - Document airport area coordination and compliance

2. **Aviation Safety and Security**
   - Follow federal aviation security protocols
   - Coordinate crane operations with air traffic control
   - Implement construction lighting requirements
   - Follow airport construction notification procedures
   - Document aviation safety compliance measures

### 7.2 Port Tampa Bay Operations
1. **Port Facility Coordination**
   - Obtain Transportation Worker Identification Credential (TWIC)
   - Follow maritime security requirements
   - Coordinate with port authority for facility access
   - Implement port facility safety protocols
   - Document port coordination activities

2. **Marine and Industrial Requirements**
   - Follow marine transportation security protocols
   - Coordinate with cruise and cargo operations
   - Implement industrial facility safety standards
   - Follow hazardous material handling requirements
   - Document marine facility compliance

### 7.3 Sports and Entertainment Venue Coordination
1. **Raymond James Stadium Operations**
   - Coordinate with Tampa Bay Buccaneers operations
   - Follow NFL facility security and safety requirements
   - Plan work around game schedules and events
   - Implement crowd safety and emergency protocols
   - Document sports venue coordination

2. **Amalie Arena and Other Venues**
   - Coordinate with Tampa Bay Lightning operations
   - Follow professional sports facility requirements
   - Plan around entertainment and sporting events
   - Implement enhanced fire safety for assembly occupancies
   - Document entertainment venue compliance

### 7.4 University of South Florida Coordination
1. **USF Campus Operations**
   - Complete university vendor registration
   - Follow campus security and access procedures
   - Coordinate with academic schedules and activities
   - Implement campus safety and emergency protocols
   - Document university coordination activities

2. **Research Facility Requirements**
   - Follow research facility security protocols
   - Coordinate with laboratory and specialized facility operations
   - Implement contamination control measures
   - Follow institutional facility safety standards
   - Document research facility compliance

### 7.5 Metropolitan Area Requirements
1. **High-Density Urban Coordination**
   - Coordinate complex urban traffic management
   - Implement pedestrian safety in dense areas
   - Follow downtown Tampa development requirements
   - Coordinate with public transportation systems
   - Document metropolitan area coordination

2. **Multi-Jurisdictional Management**
   - Navigate multiple municipal building departments
   - Coordinate with City of Tampa building requirements
   - Follow varying municipal codes and procedures
   - Manage different permit fees and processes
   - Document multi-jurisdictional compliance

### 7.6 County Building Code Requirements
1. **Hillsborough County Code Implementation**
   - Follow Florida Building Code with county amendments
   - Apply 120 mph wind load requirements for inland areas
   - Implement metropolitan area density requirements
   - Follow institutional and commercial facility standards
   - Document county code compliance

2. **Permit Application Procedures**
   - Submit applications through county online portal
   - Include airport, port, and facility-specific documentation
   - Pay permit fees ($95 base + $0.13 per square foot)
   - Schedule inspections around facility operations
   - Coordinate with expedited permitting for priority projects

### 7.7 Transportation and Infrastructure
1. **Major Highway and Bridge Coordination**
   - Coordinate with interstate highway construction
   - Follow bridge and elevated roadway restrictions
   - Plan around major transportation projects
   - Coordinate with FDOT and transportation authorities
   - Document transportation coordination

2. **Public Transportation Integration**
   - Coordinate with HART (Hillsborough Area Regional Transit)
   - Protect streetcar and bus route infrastructure
   - Follow transit authority safety requirements

   - Document public transportation coordination

### 7.8 Environmental and Water Management
1. **Tampa Bay Water Quality Protection**
   - Implement stormwater management for bay protection
   - Follow Tampa Bay Estuary Program requirements
   - Protect marine ecosystems and water quality
   - Prevent construction debris in waterways
   - Document water quality protection measures

2. **Urban Environmental Management**
   - Follow urban air quality requirements
   - Implement noise control in dense residential areas
   - Coordinate with environmental protection agencies
   - Monitor construction environmental impact
   - Document environmental management compliance

### 7.9 Emergency Response and Public Safety
1. **Metropolitan Emergency Coordination**
   - Coordinate with Tampa Fire Rescue

   - Follow multi-agency emergency response protocols
   - Coordinate with federal facility emergency procedures

2. **Major Event and Facility Security**

   - Document security coordination activities
