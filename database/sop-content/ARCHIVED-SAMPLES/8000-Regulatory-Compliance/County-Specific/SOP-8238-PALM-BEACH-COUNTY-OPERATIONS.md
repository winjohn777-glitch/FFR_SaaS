# SOP-8238: Palm Beach County Roofing Operations

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for conducting roofing operations in Palm Beach County, Florida, ensuring compliance with luxury residential requirements, international business coordination, agricultural area operations, and protocols for Florida First Roofing LLC's certified roofing services in South Florida's affluent coastal county.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel conducting roofing operations, inspections, repairs, and installations within Palm Beach County boundaries, including West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, Jupiter, Wellington, Royal Palm Beach, Greenacres, Lake Worth, Palm Beach Gardens, and agricultural areas.

## 3. Definitions
- **Palm Beach County Building Division**: Local authority for building permits and inspections
- **Luxury Residential Standards**: Enhanced requirements for high-end properties
- **Agricultural Reserve**: Protected farming area with special requirements
- **Coastal High Hazard Area**: Hurricane storm surge zone designation
- **International Business District**: Global commerce and finance areas

## 4. Responsibilities
- **County Operations Manager**: Overall compliance with Palm Beach County regulations
- **Luxury Residential Coordinator**: High-end property standards and client relations
- **Agricultural Operations Manager**: Farming area coordination and compliance
- **Coastal Operations Specialist**: Hurricane zone and coastal area management
- **International Business Liaison**: Corporate and international facility coordination

## 5. Prerequisites
- Active contractor license with Palm Beach County
- Luxury residential work experience and certifications
- Agricultural area operation permits and training
- Current liability insurance meeting high-value property requirements ($5M minimum)
- International business facility security clearances
