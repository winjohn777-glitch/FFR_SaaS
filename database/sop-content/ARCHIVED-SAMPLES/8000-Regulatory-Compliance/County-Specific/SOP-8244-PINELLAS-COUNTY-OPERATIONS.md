# SOP-8244: Pinellas County Roofing Operations

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for conducting roofing operations in Pinellas County, Florida, ensuring compliance with coastal building requirements, high-density urban development, historic preservation, beach tourism coordination, and protocols for Florida First Roofing LLC's certified roofing services in one of Florida's most densely populated counties.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel conducting roofing operations, inspections, repairs, and installations within Pinellas County boundaries, including St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Largo, Pinellas Park, Dunedin, Safety Harbor, Belleair, Indian Shores, Madeira Beach, Treasure Island, St. Pete Beach, and other coastal communities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Pinellas County Construction Licensing Board**: Local authority for contractor licensing
- **Coastal High Hazard Area**: Areas subject to hurricane storm surge
- **Historic Districts**: Multiple preserved areas throughout county
- **Beach Communities**: Barrier island municipalities with special requirements
- **Tampa Bay Area**: Regional coordination requirements

## 4. Responsibilities
- **County Operations Manager**: Overall compliance with Pinellas County regulations
- **Coastal Operations Specialist**: Beach and barrier island coordination
- **Historic Preservation Coordinator**: Historic district compliance management
- **Urban Density Manager**: High-density development coordination
- **Tourism Area Coordinator**: Beach tourism and hospitality coordination

## 5. Prerequisites
- Active contractor license with Pinellas County Construction Licensing Board
- Beach community municipal licenses as required
- Current liability insurance meeting coastal requirements ($2M minimum)
- Historic preservation work certifications
- Coastal construction experience and certifications

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### County-Approved Materials:
- Coastal environment corrosion-resistant materials

- Historic preservation-compatible materials
- Beach community aesthetic-compliant materials
- High-density urban construction materials

### Equipment Requirements:
- Coastal environment corrosion-resistant equipment

- Historic preservation-appropriate equipment and techniques

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Coastal and Beach Community Requirements
1. **Barrier Island Operations**
   - Coordinate access to barrier island communities
   - Follow beach and dune protection requirements
   - Implement saltwater corrosion protection measures
   - Plan for limited access and egress routes
   - Document barrier island coordination activities

2. **Storm Surge and Coastal Protection**
   - Apply enhanced wind load requirements for coastal exposure
   - Implement storm surge resistance measures
   - Follow coastal construction control line requirements
   - Coordinate with coastal engineering projects
   - Document coastal protection compliance

### 7.2 Historic Preservation Requirements
1. **Multiple Historic Districts**
   - Kenwood Historic District coordination
   - Historic Old Northeast preservation requirements
   - Dunedin Historic District protocols
   - Safety Harbor historic area coordination
   - Belleair historic preservation standards

2. **Historic Preservation Compliance**
   - Submit Certificate of Appropriateness applications
   - Use period-appropriate materials and techniques
   - Work with local historic preservation boards
   - Maintain architectural integrity and character
   - Document historic preservation compliance

### 7.3 High-Density Urban Development
1. **Urban Infill and Redevelopment**
   - Coordinate with downtown St. Petersburg development
   - Follow urban density and height restrictions
   - Implement traffic and pedestrian management
   - Coordinate with public transportation systems
   - Document urban development coordination

2. **Mixed-Use and Transit-Oriented Development**
   - Work with transit-oriented development projects
   - Follow mixed-use facility requirements
   - Coordinate with PSTA (Pinellas Suncoast Transit Authority)
   - Implement pedestrian and bicycle facility protection
   - Document transit-oriented development compliance

### 7.4 Beach Tourism Industry Coordination
1. **Tourist Season Planning**
   - Plan major projects during off-peak periods
   - Coordinate with beach tourism calendar
   - Minimize disruption during spring break and summer
   - Coordinate with hotel and resort operations
   - Document tourism impact mitigation

2. **Beach Access and Recreation Protection**
   - Maintain beach access during construction
   - Protect recreational facilities and amenities
   - Coordinate with beach events and festivals
   - Follow noise restrictions in tourist areas
   - Document recreation protection measures

### 7.5 County Building Code and Permit Requirements
1. **Pinellas County Code Implementation**
   - Follow Florida Building Code with coastal amendments
   - Apply appropriate wind load requirements (115-130 mph)
   - Implement flood zone construction requirements
   - Follow county-specific energy efficiency standards
   - Document county code compliance

2. **Permit Application Procedures**
   - Submit applications through Pinellas County online system
   - Include coastal and historic compliance documentation
   - Pay permit fees ($85 base + $0.11 per square foot)
   - Schedule inspections around tourism and historic requirements
   - Coordinate with multiple municipal jurisdictions

### 7.6 Environmental and Water Protection
1. **Tampa Bay Water Quality Protection**
   - Implement stormwater management for bay protection
   - Follow Tampa Bay Estuary Program requirements
   - Protect marine ecosystems and seagrass beds
   - Prevent construction debris in waterways
   - Document water quality protection measures

2. **Coastal Ecosystem Protection**
   - Protect sea turtle nesting areas (May 1 - October 31)
   - Follow shorebird protection protocols
   - Implement marine pollution prevention
   - Coordinate with Florida Fish and Wildlife
   - Document coastal ecosystem protection

### 7.7 Municipal Coordination
1. **Multiple Municipal Jurisdictions**
   - Navigate 24 different municipal building departments
   - Apply municipality-specific codes and requirements
   - Coordinate with individual city permit processes
   - Follow varying municipal fee structures
   - Document multi-municipal compliance

2. **Beach Community Special Requirements**
   - St. Pete Beach specific coastal requirements
   - Treasure Island barrier island protocols
   - Indian Shores environmental protection
   - Madeira Beach tourism coordination
   - Belleair historic preservation standards

### 7.8 Transportation and Infrastructure
1. **Bridge and Causeway Coordination**
   - Coordinate material transport across bridges
   - Follow weight and size restrictions for causeways
   - Plan for bridge openings and marine traffic
   - Coordinate with Pinellas County Public Works
   - Document transportation coordination

2. **Public Transportation Integration**
   - Coordinate with PSTA bus route operations
   - Protect public transportation infrastructure
   - Follow transit authority safety requirements
   - Plan around transit operational schedules
   - Document public transportation coordination

### 7.9 Emergency Preparedness and Response
1. **Hurricane and Storm Surge Preparedness**
   - Maintain rapid deployment for coastal storms
   - Coordinate with Pinellas County Emergency Management
   - Pre-position materials for barrier island response
   - Train crews in coastal evacuation procedures
   - Participate in county emergency exercises

2. **Barrier Island Emergency Coordination**
   - Understand limited evacuation routes
   - Coordinate with emergency management for island access
   - Plan for post-storm barrier island response
   - Maintain emergency communication systems
   - Document barrier island emergency preparedness
