## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for maintaining effective customer communication during crisis situations to ensure customer safety, preserve relationships, minimize service disruptions, and maintain Florida First Roofing LLC's commitment to exceptional customer service throughout emergency events.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All customer service representatives
- Project managers and field supervisors
- Sales and estimation teams
- Emergency response coordinators
- Management personnel
- Subcontractors and vendors interacting with customers

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Customer Crisis Communication**: Systematic approach to informing and updating customers during emergency situations
**Service Disruption**: Interruption of normal business operations affecting customer projects
**Emergency Contact Protocol**: Prioritized method for reaching customers during crisis events
**Customer Care Package**: Comprehensive support and information bundle for affected customers
**Recovery Communication**: Post-crisis customer relationship restoration activities

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Customer Service Manager
- Oversee all customer communication activities
- Coordinate with emergency response team
- Authorize service adjustments and compensation
- Monitor customer satisfaction during crisis

### 4.2 Customer Service Representatives
- Execute direct customer communications
- Document all customer interactions
- Escalate urgent customer issues
- Provide emotional support and reassurance

### 4.3 Project Managers
- Communicate project-specific impacts
- Coordinate field team customer interactions
- Assess and report project timeline changes
- Manage customer expectations realistically

### 4.4 Emergency Response Coordinator
- Provide situation updates to customer service team
- Authorize emergency communication protocols
- Coordinate with operations for service restoration
- Monitor overall customer communication effectiveness

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Crisis Customer Communication Activation

#### 5.1.1 Immediate Customer Safety Assessment (0-30 minutes)
1. **Active Project Customer Identification**
   - Review all active project locations
   - Identify customers in affected geographic areas
   - Assess project status and vulnerability
   - Prioritize customers based on safety risk

2. **Emergency Contact Initiation**
   - Use emergency contact information
   - Attempt multiple communication methods
   - Document contact attempts and responses
   - Escalate unreachable customers immediately

3. **Safety Information Delivery**
   - Provide immediate safety instructions
   - Share evacuation recommendations if applicable
   - Communicate emergency contact information
   - Verify customer safety and location

#### 5.1.2 Communication Infrastructure Setup (30-60 minutes)
1. **Customer Service Center Activation**
   - Activate emergency communication protocols
   - Increase staffing levels appropriately
   - Set up dedicated crisis hotline
   - Prepare customer information systems

2. **Communication Channel Verification**
   - Test all customer communication systems
   - Verify backup communication methods
   - Confirm customer contact information accuracy
   - Establish alternative contact procedures

### 5.2 Customer Communication Strategy Implementation

#### 5.2.1 Initial Customer Notifications
1. **Safety-First Messaging**
   - Prioritize customer and family safety
   - Provide relevant safety information
   - Share emergency resources and contacts
   - Express genuine concern for customer welfare

2. **Service Impact Communication**
   - Explain current service disruptions honestly
   - Provide preliminary timeline estimates
   - Describe company response efforts
   - Outline next communication schedule

#### 5.2.2 Ongoing Customer Updates
1. **Regular Status Updates**
   - Provide scheduled communication updates
   - Share progress on service restoration
   - Communicate any timeline changes promptly
   - Maintain consistent messaging across all channels

2. **Proactive Problem Resolution**
   - Identify potential customer concerns early
   - Develop solutions before customers request them
   - Communicate resolution options clearly
   - Implement customer-friendly policies

### 5.3 Florida-Specific Customer Communication Protocols

#### 5.3.1 Hurricane Season Customer Support
1. **Pre-Storm Customer Preparation**
   - Send 72-hour advance warning notifications
   - Provide roof securing recommendations
   - Share emergency preparedness checklists
   - Offer expedited project completion if possible

2. **Storm Event Customer Safety**
   - Check on customer safety and needs
   - Provide emergency temporary repairs if safe
   - Share storm tracking and safety information
   - Coordinate with local emergency services

3. **Post-Storm Customer Recovery**
   - Conduct immediate damage assessments
   - Prioritize emergency repairs
   - Expedite insurance claim support
   - Provide recovery timeline estimates

#### 5.3.2 Insurance Coordination Support
1. **Insurance Claim Assistance**
   - Provide detailed damage documentation
   - Coordinate with insurance adjusters
   - Offer claim process guidance
   - Support customer advocacy needs

2. **Financial Impact Mitigation**
   - Offer flexible payment arrangements
   - Provide emergency repair financing
   - Coordinate with insurance payment schedules
   - Minimize customer financial burden

### 5.4 Customer Communication Channels and Methods

#### 5.4.1 Primary Communication Channels
1. **Direct Personal Contact**
   - Phone calls to primary and emergency numbers
   - Text messaging for urgent updates
   - Email for detailed information sharing
   - In-person visits when safe and appropriate

2. **Digital Communication Platforms**
   - Customer portal updates
   - Mobile app notifications
   - Social media direct messaging
   - Video calls for complex discussions

#### 5.4.2 Mass Communication Methods
1. **Automated Notification Systems**
   - Robocall emergency notifications
   - Mass text messaging alerts
   - Email blast communications
   - Website banner announcements

2. **Community Communication**
   - Local media coordination
   - Neighborhood association updates
   - Community bulletin board postings
   - Door-to-door notification if necessary

### 5.5 Customer Care and Support Enhancement

#### 5.5.1 Emotional Support Protocols
1. **Empathetic Communication**
   - Acknowledge customer stress and concerns
   - Provide emotional support and reassurance
   - Listen actively to customer needs
   - Express genuine care and understanding

2. **Resource Assistance**
   - Share community support resources
   - Provide disaster relief information
   - Connect customers with assistance programs
   - Offer practical help and guidance

#### 5.5.2 Service Recovery Actions
1. **Expedited Service Restoration**
   - Prioritize customer projects appropriately
   - Accelerate repair and completion timelines
   - Provide additional resources when needed
   - Offer temporary solutions during delays

2. **Customer Compensation and Adjustments**
   - Waive or reduce fees where appropriate
   - Offer service upgrades at no charge
   - Provide complimentary services
   - Extend warranties and guarantees
