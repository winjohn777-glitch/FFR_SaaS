## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for emergency strategic planning and response to ensure Florida First Roofing LLC maintains strategic direction, adapts effectively to crisis situations, and emerges stronger while protecting long-term business interests and stakeholder value.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Chief Executive Officer and senior leadership
- Board of Directors and advisors
- Department heads and senior management
- Strategic planning team members
- Emergency response coordinators
- Key stakeholders and partners

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Emergency Strategic Planning**: Crisis-focused strategic decision-making and planning processes
**Strategic Response Team**: Senior leadership group responsible for emergency strategic decisions
**Crisis Strategy**: Modified strategic approach designed for emergency situations
**Strategic Recovery**: Long-term strategic planning for post-crisis business restoration
**Adaptive Leadership**: Leadership approach that adjusts to changing crisis conditions

## 4. RESPONSIBILITIES

### 4.1 Chief Executive Officer
- Lead emergency strategic planning and response
- Make critical strategic decisions during crisis
- Coordinate with board and senior leadership
- Oversee strategic communication and implementation

### 4.2 Senior Leadership Team
- Participate in strategic planning sessions
- Implement strategic decisions within departments
- Provide situational intelligence and feedback
- Support strategic communication efforts

### 4.3 Strategic Planning Coordinator
- Facilitate strategic planning processes
- Coordinate information gathering and analysis
- Document strategic decisions and plans
- Monitor strategic plan implementation

### 4.4 Department Heads
- Provide departmental strategic input
- Implement strategic initiatives within departments
- Monitor strategic performance metrics
- Report strategic challenges and opportunities
