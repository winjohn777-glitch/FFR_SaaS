## 1. PURPOSE

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for emergency financial management to ensure Florida First Roofing LLC maintains financial stability, cash flow continuity, and operational funding during crisis situations while protecting assets and minimizing financial impact.

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- Wanetta Johnson, CFO and accounting staff
- Senior management team
- Department heads with budget authority
- Emergency response coordinators
- Banking and insurance contacts
- External financial advisors and consultants

## 3. DEFINITIONS

**Emergency Financial Management**: Crisis-focused financial planning and resource allocation
**Cash Flow Continuity**: Maintaining adequate liquid funds during emergencies
**Emergency Fund**: Reserved financial resources designated for crisis situations
**Financial Recovery Plan**: Strategy for restoring normal financial operations post-crisis
**Crisis Budget**: Modified budget allocation for emergency operations

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Emergency Financial Planning and Preparation

#### 5.1.1 Emergency Fund Management
1. **Emergency Reserve Establishment**
   - Maintain 6-month operating expense reserve
   - Diversify emergency fund investments
   - Ensure rapid access to emergency funds
   - Regular reserve adequacy assessment
   - Board-approved emergency fund policies

2. **Liquidity Management**
   - Multiple bank account relationships
   - Credit line establishment and maintenance
   - Investment portfolio liquidity planning
   - Cash flow forecasting and monitoring
   - Foreign currency considerations if applicable

#### 5.1.2 Financial Risk Assessment

2. **Expense Risk Planning**
