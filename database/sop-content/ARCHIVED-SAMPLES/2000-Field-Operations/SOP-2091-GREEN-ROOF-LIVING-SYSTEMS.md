# SOP-2091: Green Roof and Living System Installation

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

### 1.1 Purpose
This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) establishes comprehensive protocols for installing green roof and living system technologies that provide environmental benefits, energy efficiency, and sustainable building solutions. This procedure ensures proper installation techniques for extensive and intensive green roof systems.

### 1.2 Scope
This SOP applies to all Florida First Roofing LLC green roof projects including:
- Extensive green roof systems (lightweight, low-maintenance)
- Intensive green roof systems (accessible, diverse plantings)
- Modular green roof systems
- Blue-green roof combinations for stormwater management
- Living wall and vertical garden integration
- Rooftop agriculture and food production systems

## 2. DEFINITIONS

- **Green Roof**: Roof system with vegetation layer over a waterproof membrane
- **Extensive Green Roof**: Lightweight system with drought-tolerant plants and minimal maintenance
- **Intensive Green Roof**: Deeper growing medium supporting diverse plants and accessibility
- **Growing Medium**: Engineered soil substitute designed for roof applications
- **Root Barrier**: Membrane preventing plant roots from penetrating waterproofing
- **Drainage Layer**: System managing excess water and preventing root zone saturation
- **Blue Roof**: Roof system designed to temporarily retain stormwater

## 5. PROCEDURES

### 5.1 Pre-Installation Assessment

#### 5.1.1 Structural Analysis
1. **Load Capacity Evaluation**
   - Calculate saturated weight of green roof system
   - Assess live load requirements for accessibility
   - Evaluate wind uplift resistance with vegetation
   - Determine need for structural reinforcement

2. **Roof Structure Inspection**
   - Verify adequate structural framing capacity
   - Check roof deck condition and deflection limits
   - Assess drainage system adequacy
   - Evaluate access for maintenance equipment

3. **Climate and Microclimate Analysis**
   - Assess local climate conditions and plant zones
   - Evaluate roof exposure and wind patterns
   - Consider building height and urban heat effects
   - Plan for seasonal weather variations

#### 5.1.2 Design Coordination
1. **System Selection**
   - Choose between extensive and intensive systems
   - Select appropriate growing medium depth
   - Plan drainage and irrigation requirements
   - Design plant selection and layout

2. **Waterproofing Coordination**
   - Verify waterproofing system compatibility
   - Plan protection during green roof installation
   - Coordinate penetrations for irrigation and drainage
   - Ensure adequate warranty coverage

### 5.2 Green Roof System Installation

#### 5.2.1 Root Barrier Installation
1. **Material Selection**
   - Use root-resistant membrane materials
   - Select chemically compatible barriers
   - Ensure UV resistance for exposed applications
   - Verify puncture and tear resistance

2. **Installation Procedures**
   - Install over completed waterproofing system
   - Maintain proper overlap at seams (minimum 6 inches)
   - Seal all seams with compatible materials
   - Protect during subsequent installation activities

3. **Quality Control**
   - Inspect for tears, punctures, or gaps
   - Test seam integrity before proceeding
   - Verify complete coverage of planted areas
   - Document installation with photographs

#### 5.2.2 Drainage Layer Installation
1. **Drainage Material Placement**
   - Install granular drainage layer or modular systems
   - Maintain proper slope to roof drains (minimum 1/4" per foot)
   - Install overflow drains at required elevations
   - Ensure clear drainage pathways

2. **Filter Fabric Installation**
   - Install geotextile filter fabric over drainage layer
   - Overlap seams minimum 6 inches
   - Avoid punctures or tears in fabric
   - Secure fabric to prevent displacement

#### 5.2.3 Growing Medium Installation
1. **Material Quality Control**
   - Verify growing medium meets specifications
   - Test pH, nutrients, and organic content
   - Ensure proper particle size distribution
   - Check for weed seeds and contaminants

2. **Placement Procedures**
   - Install growing medium to specified depth
   - Avoid compaction during placement
   - Maintain consistent depth across roof area
   - Grade surface for proper drainage

3. **Quality Assurance**
   - Test depth measurements throughout installation
   - Verify proper grading and drainage
   - Check for settlement or displacement
   - Document installation depths and coverage

### 5.3 Plant Installation and Establishment

#### 5.3.1 Plant Selection and Procurement
1. **Climate-Appropriate Species**
   - Select native and adapted plant species
   - Choose drought-tolerant varieties for extensive systems
   - Plan for seasonal interest and biodiversity
   - Consider maintenance requirements and accessibility

2. **Plant Quality Standards**
   - Inspect plants for health and vigor
   - Verify proper root development
   - Check for pest and disease issues
   - Ensure plants are properly acclimated

#### 5.3.2 Planting Procedures
1. **Installation Timing**
   - Plant during optimal weather conditions
   - Avoid extreme heat or cold periods
   - Consider seasonal growth patterns
   - Plan for establishment period requirements

2. **Planting Techniques**
   - Plant at proper depth and spacing
   - Backfill with compatible growing medium
   - Water immediately after planting
   - Apply mulch where appropriate

3. **Establishment Care**
   - Provide adequate irrigation during establishment
   - Monitor plant health and growth
   - Replace failed plants as needed
   - Apply fertilizer according to specifications

### 5.4 Irrigation System Installation

#### 5.4.1 System Design and Layout
1. **Water Source and Pressure**
   - Verify adequate water pressure and volume
   - Install pressure regulation as needed
   - Plan for seasonal water demand variations
   - Consider rainwater harvesting integration

2. **Distribution System**
   - Install efficient irrigation delivery methods
   - Use drip irrigation for water conservation
   - Zone irrigation based on plant water needs
   - Install moisture sensors for automated control

#### 5.4.2 Control System Installation
1. **Automated Controls**
   - Install programmable irrigation controllers
   - Connect moisture and weather sensors
   - Program seasonal watering schedules
   - Provide manual override capabilities

2. **Monitoring and Maintenance**
   - Install flow meters and pressure gauges
   - Provide system diagnostics and alarms
   - Plan access for maintenance and repairs
   - Train facility staff on system operation

### 5.5 Specialized Green Roof Systems

#### 5.5.1 Modular Green Roof Systems
1. **Module Selection and Layout**
   - Choose appropriate module size and configuration
   - Plan installation sequence and crane access
   - Verify structural capacity for concentrated loads
   - Design connections between modules

2. **Installation Procedures**
   - Handle modules carefully to prevent damage
   - Install on properly prepared substrate
   - Connect modules according to manufacturer specifications
   - Ensure proper drainage between modules

#### 5.5.2 Blue-Green Roof Systems
1. **Stormwater Management Design**
   - Size retention areas for design storm events
   - Install controlled drainage outlets
   - Plan for overflow and emergency drainage
   - Integrate with building stormwater management

2. **Installation Coordination**
   - Install retention areas before green roof system
   - Connect controlled drainage to building systems
   - Test water retention and release rates
   - Verify integration with monitoring systems
