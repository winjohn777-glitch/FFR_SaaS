# SOP-0042: Training Content Development and Upload Procedures

**FLORIDA FIRST ROOFING LLC**
**Standard Operating Procedure**

---

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

**Purpose:** This SOP establishes standardized procedures for developing, creating, reviewing, approving, and uploading training content to the Learning Management System (LMS) to ensure consistent, high-quality, and compliant training materials for Florida First Roofing LLC.

**Scope:** This procedure applies to all training content development activities including safety training, technical skills, compliance education, company policies, equipment operation, and professional development across all departments and contractor levels.

---

## 2. REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

- OSHA 29 CFR 1926 Construction Industry Training Standards
- Florida Building Code Training Requirements
- NRCA Safety and Training Standards
- EPA RRP Certification Training Requirements
- Workers' Compensation Safety Training Mandates
- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) Accessibility Standards
- Copyright and Intellectual Property Laws
- Quality Assurance Standards for Educational Content

---

## 3. DEFINITIONS

- **SCORM Package:** Standardized e-learning content format for LMS compatibility
- **Learning Objective:** Specific, measurable outcome learners should achieve
- **Multimedia Content:** Training materials including video, audio, interactive elements
- **Content Repository:** Centralized storage system for training materials
- **Version Control:** System for tracking content changes and updates
- **Accessibility Compliance:** Content designed for learners with disabilities
- **Subject Matter Expert (SME):** Individual with specialized knowledge in content area

---

## 4. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

**Training Manager:**

**Subject Matter Experts (SMEs):**
- Provide technical expertise and content accuracy
- Review and validate content for industry standards

- Verify compliance with regulatory requirements
- Update content based on industry changes

**Content Developer/Producer:**
- Create multimedia content and interactive elements
- Produce videos, animations, and graphic materials
- Develop SCORM packages and LMS-compatible formats
- Ensure technical quality and functionality
- Manage content version control and documentation

**Quality Assurance Reviewer:**
- Review content for accuracy and compliance
- Test functionality and user experience
- Verify accessibility and technical standards
- Validate learning objectives and assessments
- Document review findings and recommendations

## 6. PROCEDURES

### A. Content Planning and Design

1. **Needs Assessment:**
   - Identify training gaps and requirements
   - Analyze target audience and learning objectives
   - Review regulatory and compliance mandates
   - Assess current content inventory and gaps
   - Establish content priorities and timeline

2. **Learning Design:**
   - Define specific learning objectives and outcomes
   - Design curriculum structure and learning paths
   - Select appropriate delivery methods and media
   - Plan assessments and evaluation criteria
   - Create content specifications and requirements

3. **Content Planning Documentation:**
   - Complete Content Development Request (FORM-0041-01)
   - Create detailed content outline and storyboard
   - Identify required resources and SME involvement
   - Establish project timeline and milestones
   - Define quality criteria and approval process

### B. Content Creation and Development

1. **Multimedia Content Production:**
   - Record video training sessions and demonstrations
   - Create graphic elements and visual aids
   - Develop interactive simulations and scenarios
   - Produce audio narration and background elements
   - Design user interface and navigation elements

2. **Text and Document Content:**
   - Write instructional content and procedures
   - Create reference materials and job aids
   - Develop assessment questions and scenarios
   - Produce handouts and supplementary materials
   - Write instructor guides and facilitator notes

3. **Interactive Elements:**
   - Design knowledge checks and practice exercises
   - Create scenario-based learning activities
   - Develop virtual simulations and demonstrations
   - Build interactive decision-making exercises
   - Design collaborative learning components

### C. Content Review and Quality Assurance

1. **SME Review Process:**
   - Distribute content to assigned subject matter experts
   - Collect feedback on technical accuracy and relevance
   - Verify compliance with industry standards and regulations
   - Validate practical application and real-world scenarios
   - Document review comments and required changes

2. **Quality Assurance Testing:**
   - Test content functionality across devices and browsers
   - Verify SCORM package compatibility and tracking
   - Validate accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
   - Check multimedia quality and performance
   - Test assessment scoring and feedback mechanisms

3. **Content Validation:**
   - Pilot test with representative user groups
   - Collect learner feedback and usability data
   - Measure learning effectiveness and objective achievement
   - Identify areas for improvement and refinement
   - Document validation results and recommendations

### D. Content Approval and Upload Process

1. **Approval Workflow:**
   - Submit content package for Training Manager review
   - Obtain SME sign-off on technical accuracy
   - Secure compliance approval for regulatory content
   - Get final authorization from Training Manager
   - Document approval chain and version control

2. **LMS Upload Preparation:**
   - Package content in SCORM or LMS-compatible format
   - Prepare metadata and tagging information
   - Create course descriptions and learning objectives
   - Set up assessment criteria and completion requirements
   - Prepare user enrollment and access parameters

3. **Upload and Configuration:**
   - Upload content packages to LMS platform
   - Configure course settings and parameters
   - Set up user groups and access permissions
   - Test content delivery and tracking functionality
   - Validate integration with existing learning paths

### E. Content Maintenance and Updates

1. **Version Control Management:**
   - Maintain master content files and backups
   - Track version history and change documentation
   - Archive superseded content versions
   - Update content metadata and tracking information
   - Manage content lifecycle and retirement schedules

2. **Content Updates and Revisions:**
   - Monitor regulatory changes requiring content updates
   - Collect user feedback and improvement suggestions
   - Update content based on industry best practices
   - Revise content for technology and tool changes
   - Refresh content to maintain engagement and relevance

3. **Content Performance Monitoring:**
   - Track completion rates and learner engagement
   - Monitor assessment scores and learning outcomes
   - Analyze user feedback and satisfaction ratings
   - Identify content requiring updates or replacement
   - Generate reports on content effectiveness
