# SOP-0043: User Enrollment and Course Assignment Management

**FLORIDA FIRST ROOFING LLC**
**Standard Operating Procedure**

---

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

**Purpose:** This SOP establishes standardized procedures for managing user enrollment, course assignments, learning path allocation, and training requirements tracking within the Learning Management System (LMS) for Florida First Roofing LLC employees, subcontractors, and stakeholders.

**Scope:** This procedure applies to all user enrollment activities including new hire onboarding, role-based training assignments, compliance requirement management, continuing education tracking, and specialized certification programs across all organizational levels.

---

## 2. REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

- OSHA 29 CFR 1926 Construction Industry Training Requirements

- EPA RRP Certification and Renewal Mandates
- Workers' Compensation Safety Training Requirements
- Florida Roofing Contractor License Training Standards
- NRCA Professional Development Standards
- Equal Employment Opportunity Training Requirements
- Privacy and Data Protection Regulations (FERPA, GDPR)

## 3. DEFINITIONS

- **Learning Path:** Structured sequence of courses designed for specific roles or career progression
- **Enrollment Status:** Current state of user's course participation (enrolled, in-progress, completed, expired)
- **Prerequisite:** Required training that must be completed before accessing advanced courses
- **Competency-Based Assignment:** Course allocation based on skill gaps and job requirements
- **Automatic Enrollment:** System-triggered course assignment based on predefined rules
- **Bulk Enrollment:** Process of enrolling multiple users simultaneously
- **Training Matrix:** Grid showing required training by role, department, and compliance needs

## 6. PROCEDURES

### A. User Account Setup and Management

1. **New User Registration:**
   - Verify employee information in HRIS system
   - Create LMS user account with appropriate permissions
   - Configure user profile with role and department information
   - Set up supervisor and reporting relationships
   - Assign initial user groups and access levels

2. **Profile Management:**
   - Maintain current contact and demographic information
   - Update job roles and department assignments
   - Track certification statuses and expiration dates
   - Manage learning preferences and accessibility needs
   - Document special training requirements or accommodations

3. **Access Control and Permissions:**
   - Configure role-based access to courses and content
   - Set up approval workflows for course enrollment
   - Manage guest and contractor access permissions
   - Establish supervisor visibility and reporting access
   - Configure mobile and remote access capabilities

### B. Training Needs Assessment and Assignment

1. **Role-Based Training Matrix:**
   - Maintain comprehensive training requirements by position
   - Include mandatory compliance and safety training
   - Specify technical skills and competency requirements
   - Document continuing education and renewal needs
   - Update matrix based on regulatory and industry changes

2. **Individual Training Assessment:**
   - Evaluate current skills and competencies
   - Identify training gaps and development needs
   - Consider career progression and advancement goals
   - Review previous training history and certifications
   - Document special requirements or accommodations

3. **Course Assignment Strategy:**
   - Prioritize mandatory and compliance training
   - Sequence courses based on prerequisites and dependencies
   - Schedule training to minimize operational disruption
   - Consider learning capacity and time constraints
   - Plan for seasonal workload and project demands

### C. Enrollment Processes and Workflows

1. **Automatic Enrollment:**
   - Configure rules for new hire automatic assignments
   - Set up role-change triggered enrollments
   - Establish deadline-based automatic renewals
   - Create compliance-driven enrollment triggers
   - Implement bulk enrollment for company-wide initiatives

2. **Manual Enrollment Process:**
   - Review and approve individual enrollment requests
   - Process supervisor-initiated team enrollments
   - Handle special accommodation and exception requests
   - Manage waitlist and capacity-constrained courses
   - Document enrollment decisions and justifications

3. **Enrollment Verification:**
   - Confirm course availability and capacity
   - Verify prerequisite completion and eligibility
   - Check for scheduling conflicts and resource availability
   - Validate supervisor approval and authorization
   - Document enrollment confirmation and notifications

### D. Learning Path Management

1. **Learning Path Design:**
   - Develop role-specific learning progressions
   - Sequence courses from basic to advanced levels
   - Include assessment and competency milestones
   - Integrate certification and compliance requirements
   - Create flexible paths for different experience levels

2. **Path Assignment and Tracking:**
   - Assign appropriate learning paths based on roles
   - Monitor progress through path milestones
   - Track completion rates and time-to-completion
   - Identify path bottlenecks and improvement opportunities
   - Adjust paths based on performance and feedback

3. **Path Optimization:**
   - Analyze completion data and learner feedback
   - Update paths based on industry and regulatory changes
   - Optimize sequence and timing of course delivery
   - Remove outdated or ineffective content
   - Add new courses and learning opportunities

### E. Monitoring and Compliance Tracking

1. **Progress Monitoring:**
   - Track individual and team training progress
   - Monitor completion rates and deadlines
   - Identify at-risk learners and intervention needs
   - Generate alerts for overdue or incomplete training
   - Report on training metrics and performance indicators

2. **Compliance Management:**
   - Track mandatory training completion and deadlines
   - Monitor certification renewals and expiration dates
   - Generate compliance reports for regulatory audits
   - Manage exception requests and documentation
   - Coordinate with legal and compliance teams

3. **Performance Analytics:**
   - Analyze enrollment and completion patterns
   - Measure training effectiveness and outcomes
   - Identify high-performing and struggling learners
   - Evaluate course demand and resource utilization
   - Generate ROI and cost-benefit analysis
