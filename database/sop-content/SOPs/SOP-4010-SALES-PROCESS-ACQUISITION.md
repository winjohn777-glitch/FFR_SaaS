# SOP-4010: Sales Process and Customer Acquisition

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for managing the sales process from initial consultation through contract execution to ensure consistent customer acquisition, optimal profit margins, and sustainable business growth while maintaining high customer satisfaction levels.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in sales activities, including sales representatives, estimators, project managers, and support staff engaged in customer acquisition and contract development for residential and commercial roofing projects throughout Florida.

## 3. Responsibilities
- **Sales Manager**: Oversee sales process effectiveness and team performance
- **Sales Representatives**: Execute sales presentations and customer relationship development
- **Estimators**: Provide accurate pricing and technical specifications
- **Project Managers**: Support sales process with technical expertise and project planning
- **Office Manager**: Coordinate sales support and contract administration

## 4. Required Materials and Equipment
### Sales Presentation Tools:
- Digital presentation materials and project portfolio
- Mobile devices with sales applications and pricing tools
- Sample materials and product demonstration kits
- Customer reference lists and testimonial documentation
- Contract templates and proposal generation software

### Assessment Equipment:
- Professional measuring tools and laser measurement devices
- Digital cameras for documentation and proposal development
- Drone equipment for aerial assessments (when certified)
- Safety equipment for property assessment activities

## 5. Safety Considerations
- Follow all safety protocols during property assessments and presentations
- Ensure safe access to customer properties and rooftop areas
- Maintain current liability insurance for sales activities
- Use proper safety equipment during any climbing or assessment activities
- Implement emergency procedures for safety incidents during sales visits

## 7. Step-by-Step Procedure

### Phase 1: Pre-Sales Preparation and Customer Research
1. **Customer Background Research**:
   - Review CRM system for previous interactions and service history
   - Research property details through public records and online resources
   - Identify customer decision-making authority and influence factors
   - Analyze neighborhood characteristics and demographic information
   - Check for any insurance claims history or previous roofing work

2. **Competitive Analysis and Positioning**:
   - Research local competition and current market pricing
   - Identify unique value propositions and competitive advantages
   - Prepare responses to common objections and competitive challenges
   - Review recent project examples and customer testimonials
   - Update knowledge of current industry trends and innovations

3. **Presentation Customization**:
   - Tailor presentation materials to customer needs and preferences
   - Select relevant project examples and case studies
   - Prepare product samples and material options
   - Customize pricing strategies based on project scope and customer profile
   - Plan presentation flow and timing based on customer availability

### Phase 2: Customer Consultation and Needs Assessment
1. **Initial Consultation Structure**:
   - Professional introduction and company credibility establishment
   - Review of customer's current situation and immediate concerns
   - Explanation of assessment process and what to expect
   - Discussion of timeline expectations and project considerations
   - Information gathering on budget parameters and decision-making process

2. **Property Assessment and Documentation**:
   - Comprehensive roof inspection using systematic approach
   - Photographic documentation of current conditions and issues
   - Measurement and calculation of project scope and requirements
   - Identification of code compliance and permit requirements
   - Assessment of accessibility and project complexity factors

3. **Customer Education and Consultation**:
   - Explanation of findings and recommended solutions
   - Education on roofing materials and system options
   - Discussion of warranty options and long-term value considerations
   - Review of financing alternatives and payment options
   - Addressing customer questions and concerns thoroughly

### Phase 3: Proposal Development and Presentation
1. **Technical Specification Development**:
   - Detailed scope of work with specific material specifications
   - Compliance with applicable building codes and manufacturer requirements
   - Project timeline with major milestones and completion dates
   - Warranty coverage and manufacturer certifications
   - Permit requirements and inspection coordination

2. **Pricing Strategy and Proposal Structure**:
   - Detailed line-item pricing with transparent cost breakdowns
   - Alternative options and upgrade possibilities
   - Financing terms and payment schedule options
   - Discount opportunities and promotional considerations
   - Comparison with competitor pricing and value propositions

3. **Proposal Presentation and Discussion**:
   - Clear explanation of proposal components and value delivered
   - Visual aids and project examples to support recommendations
   - Discussion of timeline and project coordination requirements
   - Review of contract terms and warranty provisions
   - Addressing questions and concerns with solutions-focused approach

### Phase 4: Closing and Contract Execution
1. **Objection Handling and Resolution**:
   - Active listening to understand customer concerns and priorities
   - Providing evidence-based responses to pricing and value questions
   - Offering alternative solutions and flexible terms when appropriate
   - Using customer testimonials and references to build confidence
   - Creating urgency through limited-time offers or scheduling considerations

2. **Contract Negotiation and Terms Agreement**:
   - Flexible negotiation while protecting company profit margins
   - Clear explanation of all contract terms and conditions
   - Discussion of change order procedures and additional work policies
   - Review of payment terms and financing arrangements
   - Confirmation of project timeline and coordination requirements

3. **Contract Execution and Project Initiation**:
   - Electronic signature collection using secure platforms
   - Initial payment processing and payment plan setup
   - Project scheduling and coordination with operations team
   - Customer onboarding and communication protocol establishment
   - Permit application initiation and regulatory coordination

## 8. Quality Control Standards
- Proposal accuracy: 95% accuracy in initial proposals with minimal change orders
- Conversion rate: 30% minimum conversion from qualified leads to signed contracts
- Average sale value: Maintain target margins while remaining competitive
- Customer satisfaction: 4.7/5 rating for sales process experience
- Contract quality: Zero contract disputes or legal issues

## 9. Troubleshooting Guide
### Common Issues:
- **Price objections**: Emphasize value proposition and financing options
- **Competitor comparisons**: Highlight unique advantages and superior service
- **Decision delays**: Create appropriate urgency and follow-up schedules
- **Technical questions**: Engage technical specialists and provide detailed documentation
- **Financing concerns**: Offer multiple payment options and financing alternatives

### Resolution Procedures:
1. Document specific objections and customer concerns
2. Research appropriate responses and solution alternatives
3. Consult with management on pricing flexibility and options
4. Engage technical specialists when expertise is required
5. Follow up with customers to address concerns and close sales
