## 1. INTRODUCTION AND OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Manual Purpose
This Emergency Response Manual provides comprehensive guidance for Florida First Roofing LLC's emergency preparedness, response, and recovery operations. It integrates all emergency-related Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) into a cohesive framework for effective crisis management.

### 1.2 Manual Scope
This manual covers all aspects of emergency response including:
- Crisis communication and public relations
- Business continuity and operational recovery
- Employee safety and support services
- Financial emergency management
- Equipment and supply management
- Strategic planning and decision-making

### 1.3 Emergency Response Philosophy
Florida First Roofing LLC's emergency response philosophy prioritizes:
1. **Life Safety First**: Employee and customer safety above all considerations
2. **Stakeholder Communication**: Transparent, timely, and accurate information sharing
3. **Business Continuity**: Maintaining essential operations and customer service
4. **Community Support**: Contributing to community resilience and recovery
5. **Continuous Improvement**: Learning from each event to enhance preparedness

### 1.4 Manual Integration with SOPs
This manual integrates the following Emergency Response SOPs:
- SOP-6020: Crisis Communication Plan Implementation
- SOP-6021: Media Relations and Public Communication
- SOP-6022: Customer Communication During Crisis
- SOP-6023: Employee Communication and Support
- SOP-6030: Business Continuity Planning and Implementation
- SOP-6040: Emergency Equipment and Supply Management
- SOP-6050: Emergency Financial Management
- SOP-6150: Emergency Strategic Planning and Response
