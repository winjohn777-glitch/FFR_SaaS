## 1. QUALITY POLICY AND OBJECTIVES {#quality-policy}

### Quality Policy Statement
Florida First Roofing LLC is committed to delivering exceptional roofing services through a systematic approach to quality management. We strive to exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety, compliance, and professional excellence.

### Quality Objectives
1. **Customer Satisfaction**: Maintain customer satisfaction rating above 4.8/5.0
2. **First-Pass Quality**: Achieve 99% first-pass quality rate on all projects
3. **Continuous Improvement**: Implement quarterly improvement initiatives
4. **Compliance**: Maintain 100% regulatory and code compliance
5. **Employee Competence**: Ensure all personnel are trained and competent

### Quality Commitment
- Every employee is responsible for quality
- Quality is built into every process
- Prevention is preferred over correction
- Customer requirements drive our processes
- Continuous improvement is our standard

**Related SOPs**: SOP-0711, SOP-0712, SOP-0002
