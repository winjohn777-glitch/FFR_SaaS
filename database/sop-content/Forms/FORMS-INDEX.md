## FORM USAGE GUIDELINES

### Digital Integration Requirements
- All forms must be uploaded to the CRM system within specified timeframes
- Forms should be completed electronically when possible
- Digital signatures are acceptable for most non-legal documents
- Backup paper copies should be maintained for critical safety and legal forms

### Completion Standards
- All required fields must be completed before form submission
- Illegible handwriting or incomplete forms will be returned for correction
- Supervisory approval is required for forms marked as requiring authorization
- Original forms should be maintained in appropriate departmental files

### Training Requirements
- All employees must be trained on forms relevant to their job functions
- Annual refresher training is required for safety-critical forms
- New form releases require mandatory training within 30 days
- Form completion errors require additional training and supervision

---

## FORM MAINTENANCE AND UPDATES

### Review Schedule
- **Safety Forms**: Reviewed quarterly or after any safety incident
- **Quality Control Forms**: Reviewed semi-annually
- **Customer Service Forms**: Reviewed annually or based on customer feedback
- **Administrative Forms**: Reviewed annually or when procedures change
- **Emergency Response Forms**: Reviewed annually or after emergency activation

### Revision Process
1. Form deficiencies identified through use or audit
2. Proposed changes submitted to form owner
3. Review and approval by department manager
4. Legal and compliance review (if applicable)
5. Training material updates developed
6. Implementation with staff training
7. Old form versions removed from circulation

### Version Control
- All forms include version numbers and effective dates
- Superseded forms must be removed from use immediately
- Form archives maintained for historical reference
- Change logs documented for audit purposes
