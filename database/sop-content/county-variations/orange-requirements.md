# Orange County Roofing Requirements and Variations

## County-Specific Information
- **County Seat**: Orlando
- **Building Department**: Orange County Building Safety Division
- **Primary Contact**: (407) 836-5550
- **Online Portal**: www.orangecountyfl.net/BuildingSafety
- **Hurricane Risk Level**: Moderate (Inland Central Florida)

## Permit Requirements

### Residential Roofing Permits
- **New Construction**: Required for all new roof installations
- **Re-roofing**: Required for replacement over 100 sq ft (lower threshold than state)
- **Repair Permits**: Required for repairs exceeding $500 or structural modifications
- **Emergency Permits**: Available for storm damage (48-hour processing)

### Permit Application Process
- **Online Submission**: Mandatory through county portal system
- **Required Documents**:
  - Detailed site plan with roof dimensions
  - Product specification sheets
  - Structural calculations (if applicable)
  - Contractor licensing and insurance verification
- **Processing Time**: 2-3 business days for standard permits
- **Fees**: $1.25 per $100 of project value (minimum $50)

### Special Permit Categories
- **Tourist Corridor Properties**: Enhanced aesthetic requirements
- **Airport Overlay Districts**: Height and material restrictions
- **Lake Protection Areas**: Environmental impact considerations
- **Urban Redevelopment Areas**: Expedited permit processing

## Building Code Requirements

### Florida Building Code Implementation
- **Current Code**: 2020 Florida Building Code with Orange County Amendments
- **Local Modifications**: Stricter requirements in some categories
- **Wind Load Requirements**: 120-130 mph design wind speeds (lower than coastal)
- **Energy Efficiency**: Enhanced energy code requirements

### Material Standards
- **Shingles**: Florida Product Approval required, enhanced algae resistance
- **Metal Roofing**: Energy Star certification preferred
- **Tile Systems**: Impact resistance and load calculations required
- **Cool Roof Requirements**: Reflectivity standards for commercial properties

### Installation Standards
- **Fastener Requirements**: Standard Florida requirements with local enhancements
- **Ventilation**: 1 sq ft per 150 sq ft with balanced intake/exhaust
- **Drainage**: Enhanced stormwater management requirements
- **Fire Resistance**: Class A fire rating required in wildfire interface areas

## Inspection Requirements

### Inspection Schedule
1. **Foundation/Structural**: If structural modifications are made
2. **Rough Inspection**: After decking before underlayment installation
3. **Final Inspection**: Complete installation with all accessories
4. **Insulation Inspection**: Energy code compliance verification

### Inspection Procedures
- **Scheduling**: Online scheduling with 24-hour notice
- **Inspection Hours**: Monday-Friday 7:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- **Saturday Inspections**: Available by special request ($100 surcharge)
- **Failed Inspection**: Correction notice issued, re-inspection required

### Quality Standards
- **Workmanship Requirements**: Orange County workmanship standards
- **Material Verification**: Product approval documentation on-site
- **Safety Compliance**: OSHA and county safety requirements
- **Clean-up Standards**: Site restoration requirements

## Contractor Licensing and Registration

### Licensing Requirements
- **State License**: Florida roofing contractor license mandatory
- **Orange County Registration**: County business tax receipt required
- **Insurance Requirements**:
  - General Liability: $500,000 minimum
  - Workers' Compensation: State-required coverage
  - Vehicle Insurance: Commercial vehicle coverage
- **Bond Requirements**: $15,000 contractor bond

### Specialty Certifications
- **Green Building**: LEED AP certification for green building projects
- **Historic Preservation**: Certification for historic district work
- **Solar Installation**: Additional licensing for solar roofing systems
