# SOP-3075: Installation Quality Verification Standards

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive standards and procedures for verifying installation quality to ensure all roofing systems meet design specifications, performance requirements, and customer expectations while maintaining consistency across all projects and compliance with Florida building codes.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all quality control inspectors, field supervisors, and project managers involved in verifying installation quality for all types of roofing systems throughout the installation process from start to completion.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Verification**: Systematic confirmation that installation meets specified requirements
- **Hold Point**: Mandatory inspection point before proceeding with next phase
- **Quality Control Point**: Designated checkpoint for quality verification during installation
- **Acceptance Criteria**: Specific standards that must be met for work acceptance
- **Installation Tolerance**: Allowable deviation from specified installation requirements

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Control Manager**: Standards development, verification oversight, compliance monitoring
- **QC Inspector**: Field quality verification, testing, documentation, deficiency reporting
- **Field Supervisor**: Installation oversight, quality compliance, corrective action implementation
- **Project Manager**: Quality planning, resource allocation, customer quality communication
- **Installation Crew Leader**: Work quality execution, self-inspection, quality compliance

## 5. Prerequisites
- Installation specifications and quality standards defined
- Quality verification procedures and checklists prepared
- Testing equipment calibrated and available
- Quality control points and hold points established
- Documentation systems and forms ready

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Verification Tools:
- Measuring devices (rulers, calipers, gauges)
- Testing equipment (water testing, adhesion testing)
- Documentation equipment (cameras, tablets, forms)
- Environmental monitoring devices
- Quality reference standards and specifications

### Testing Equipment:
- Water testing equipment and procedures
- Adhesion testing devices
- Thickness measurement tools
- Level and alignment verification tools
- Performance testing equipment

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Pre-Installation Quality Verification
1. **Substrate Preparation Verification**
   - Surface cleanliness and preparation adequacy
   - Substrate moisture content within specifications
   - Surface irregularities and defect correction
   - Primer application verification when required
   - Environmental conditions suitable for installation

2. **Material Quality Verification**
   - Material condition and integrity upon delivery
   - Proper material storage and handling verification
   - Material specifications and lot number verification
   - Material preparation and conditioning adequacy
   - Tool and equipment readiness and calibration

### 7.2 Installation Process Quality Control Points
1. **Initial Installation Verification**
   - **Hold Point 1**: Substrate preparation complete and verified
   - Proper material placement and positioning
   - Correct installation sequence and procedures
   - Adequate overlap and coverage verification
   - Initial adhesion and attachment verification

2. **Progressive Installation Checkpoints**
   - **QC Point 1**: 25% completion - alignment and quality verification
   - **QC Point 2**: 50% completion - intermediate quality assessment
   - **QC Point 3**: 75% completion - system integrity verification
   - **Hold Point 2**: Final installation complete before testing

### 7.3 Membrane Installation Quality Standards
1. **Membrane Placement and Alignment**
   - Proper membrane orientation and direction
   - Adequate overlap at all seams (minimum 6 inches)
   - Elimination of wrinkles, bubbles, and bridging
   - Proper tension and positioning without stretching
   - Correct treatment of corners and transitions

2. **Seam Quality Verification**
   - Seam preparation and cleaning adequacy
   - Proper seaming technique and equipment settings
   - Continuous seam formation without gaps or voids
   - Proper seam overlap and edge treatment
   - Seam testing and quality verification

### 7.4 Fastener and Attachment Verification
1. **Fastener Installation Standards**
   - Proper fastener type and size for application
   - Correct fastener spacing and pattern
   - Adequate penetration depth into substrate
   - Proper sealing around fastener heads
   - No over-driving or under-driving of fasteners

2. **Mechanical Attachment Quality**
   - Proper attachment plate installation and spacing
   - Adequate fastener penetration and holding power
   - Correct overlap and edge treatment
   - Proper integration with drainage and details
   - No punctures or damage from attachment process

### 7.5 Flashing and Detail Work Verification
1. **Flashing Installation Standards**
   - Proper flashing material and thickness
   - Adequate overlap and water shedding design
   - Secure attachment without damage to membrane
   - Proper integration with membrane system
   - Sealant application quality and coverage

2. **Penetration Detail Quality**
   - Proper cutting and fitting around penetrations
   - Adequate sealing and waterproofing
   - Correct installation sequence and integration
   - No membrane damage during installation
   - Proper accommodation for thermal movement

### 7.6 Florida Climate-Specific Verification
1. **Hurricane Resistance Verification**
   - Wind uplift resistance testing and verification
   - Impact resistance verification where required
   - Fastener pullout testing and compliance
   - Edge securement and attachment adequacy
   - System performance under design wind loads

2. **Weather Resistance Quality Control**
   - UV resistance and protection adequacy
   - Thermal expansion accommodation verification
   - Moisture resistance and vapor barrier integrity
   - Drainage system performance verification
   - Long-term durability assessment

### 7.7 Performance Testing and Verification
1. **Water Testing Procedures**
   - Spray testing for water penetration
   - Flood testing for membrane integrity
   - Electronic leak detection where applicable
   - Drainage system performance testing
   - Ponding water evaluation and correction

2. **Adhesion and Bond Testing**
   - Peel adhesion testing of membrane bonds
   - Lap shear testing of seamed connections
   - Fastener pullout testing for mechanical attachment
   - Sealant adhesion testing at critical details
   - Long-term bond performance verification

### 7.8 Final Installation Quality Verification
1. **Complete System Inspection**
   - **Hold Point 3**: Complete installation before final inspection
   - Comprehensive visual inspection of entire system
   - Verification of all quality control checkpoints
   - Testing of all performance requirements
   - Documentation of final system condition

2. **Performance Verification Testing**
   - Water testing of complete system
   - Drainage performance verification
   - Thermal performance testing where applicable
   - Wind uplift resistance verification
   - Final quality compliance documentation

### 7.9 Quality Documentation and Reporting
1. **Quality Verification Documentation**
   - Complete inspection checklists and forms
   - Photographic documentation of key quality points
   - Test result documentation and certificates
   - Non-conformance reports and corrective actions
   - Final quality compliance certification

2. **Quality Report Preparation**
   - Comprehensive quality verification summary
   - Performance testing results and analysis
   - Quality compliance verification and certification
   - Recommendations for maintenance and care
   - Warranty documentation and requirements

### 7.10 Non-Conformance Management
1. **Deficiency Identification and Documentation**
   - Immediate identification and marking of defects
   - Detailed documentation with photos and measurements
   - Root cause analysis and corrective action planning
   - Work stoppage at hold points until correction
   - Re-inspection and verification after correction

2. **Corrective Action Implementation**
   - Immediate correction of safety-related defects
   - Systematic correction of quality deficiencies
   - Re-testing and verification after corrections
   - Documentation of corrective actions taken
   - Prevention of similar defects in future work

## 8. Quality Standards
- Installation accuracy: 100% compliance with specifications and tolerances
- Hold point compliance: No work progression without hold point clearance
- Testing performance: All performance tests must meet specification requirements
- Documentation completeness: Complete quality verification documentation
- Customer satisfaction: Final installation meets all customer requirements
