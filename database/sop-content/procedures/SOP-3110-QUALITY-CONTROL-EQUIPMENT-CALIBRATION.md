# SOP-3110: Quality Control Equipment Calibration

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for calibration, maintenance, and verification of quality control equipment and measuring instruments to ensure accurate, reliable, and traceable measurements for Florida roofing quality control activities.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in equipment calibration, maintenance, verification, and quality measurements using calibrated instruments across all Florida First Roofing quality control and testing operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Calibration**: Comparison of measurements against known standards to verify accuracy
- **Traceability**: Documented chain of calibrations to national or international standards
- **Verification**: Confirmation that equipment meets specified requirements
- **Measurement Uncertainty**: Estimate of doubt associated with measurement results
- **Calibration Certificate**: Document providing evidence of calibration performance
- **Measurement Standard**: Reference used for calibration with known accuracy
- **Drift**: Change in equipment accuracy over time

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Calibration program oversight, standard maintenance, compliance monitoring
- **Calibration Technician**: Equipment calibration, maintenance, record keeping
- **Quality Technician**: Equipment operation, basic maintenance, calibration scheduling
- **Equipment Manager**: Equipment inventory, procurement, lifecycle management
- **Laboratory Manager**: Standard maintenance, calibration procedure development

## 5. Prerequisites
- Equipment inventory and calibration requirements identified
- Calibration procedures and schedules established
- Calibration standards and references acquired
- Calibration records and tracking systems operational
- Personnel training on calibration procedures completed

- Calibration compliance: 100% of equipment calibrated per schedule
- Traceability: 100% traceable calibration to national standards
- Accuracy: Equipment meets or exceeds specified accuracy requirements
- Documentation: 100% complete calibration records and certificates
- Non-conformance response: 24-hour response to critical equipment failures
