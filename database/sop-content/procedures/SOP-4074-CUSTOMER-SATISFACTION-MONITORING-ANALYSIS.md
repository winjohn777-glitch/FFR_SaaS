# SOP-4074: Customer Satisfaction Monitoring and Analysis

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for monitoring, measuring, analyzing, and improving customer satisfaction to ensure exceptional service delivery, customer loyalty, and business growth in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in customer service delivery, satisfaction measurement, feedback collection, and improvement implementation across all Florida First Roofing customer interaction points.

## 3. Definitions
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Degree to which customer expectations are met or exceeded
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: Metric measuring customer loyalty and advocacy
- **Customer Satisfaction Score (CSAT)**: Direct measurement of satisfaction with specific interactions
- **Customer Effort Score (CES)**: Measurement of ease of customer experience
- **Voice of Customer (VOC)**: Systematic capture and analysis of customer feedback
- **Customer Journey**: Complete experience across all customer touchpoints
- **Satisfaction Driver**: Factors that most influence customer satisfaction levels

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Customer Service Manager**: Satisfaction program oversight, analysis, improvement planning
- **Quality Manager**: Satisfaction measurement, analysis, improvement integration
- **Survey Coordinator**: Survey design, distribution, data collection, reporting
- **Data Analyst**: Data analysis, trend identification, insight generation
- **All Customer-Facing Staff**: Feedback collection, satisfaction delivery, improvement implementation

## 5. Prerequisites
- Customer satisfaction measurement framework established
- Survey tools and platforms implemented
- Data collection and analysis systems operational
- Customer feedback channels identified and operational
- Staff training on satisfaction delivery completed

## 8. Quality Standards
- Response rate: 25% minimum customer survey response rate
- NPS score: Target NPS of 70+ (industry leading)
- CSAT score: 90% customer satisfaction score target
- Response time: Customer issue response within 24 hours
- Improvement implementation: 80% of planned improvements completed on time
