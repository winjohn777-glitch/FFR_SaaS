# SOP-3072: Quality Control Documentation and Records

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for quality control documentation and record management to ensure systematic tracking of quality activities, compliance verification, performance analysis, and continuous improvement while maintaining regulatory compliance and customer satisfaction.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all quality control personnel, project managers, and administrative staff involved in creating, maintaining, and managing quality control documentation and records for all roofing projects and operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Quality Record**: Document providing objective evidence of quality activities and results
- **Control Document**: Document requiring formal control and revision management
- **Retention Schedule**: Specified timeframes for maintaining different types of records
- **Traceability**: Ability to track quality activities through complete documentation
- **Audit Trail**: Complete documented history of quality control activities

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Overall QC documentation system oversight, policy development, compliance verification
- **Quality Coordinator**: Daily documentation management, record compilation, system maintenance
- **Project QC Inspector**: Field documentation creation, real-time record keeping, quality verification
- **Records Administrator**: Document storage, retrieval, retention management, archive coordination
- **Compliance Officer**: Regulatory compliance verification, audit support, legal record requirements

## 5. Prerequisites
- Quality management system fully implemented
- Documentation templates and forms standardized
- Record storage and retrieval systems established
- Staff training on documentation requirements completed
- Technology systems for documentation management operational

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Documentation Materials:
- Standardized quality control forms and checklists
- Digital documentation devices (tablets, cameras)
- Measurement and testing equipment with calibration records
- Sample collection and identification materials
- Document storage and filing systems

### Technology Systems:
- Quality management software systems
- Document management and version control systems
- Digital photography and video documentation equipment
- Data backup and archive systems
- Mobile documentation applications

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Documentation System Framework
1. **Document Classification System**
   - **Level 1**: Quality policy and manual documents
   - **Level 2**: Procedures and work instructions
   - **Level 3**: Forms, checklists, and templates
   - **Level 4**: Records and evidence documents
   - **Level 5**: Reference materials and external standards

2. **Document Control Requirements**
   - Version control and approval tracking
   - Distribution control and access management
   - Regular review and update schedules
   - Obsolete document management
   - Change control and notification procedures

### 7.2 Quality Planning Documentation
1. **Project Quality Plans**
   - Project-specific quality requirements
   - Inspection and testing schedules
   - Resource allocation and responsibility matrix
   - Risk assessment and mitigation plans
   - Customer-specific quality requirements

2. **Quality Control Checklists**
   - Material receiving and inspection checklists
   - Installation quality verification forms
   - System testing and performance checklists
   - Final inspection and completion forms
   - Customer satisfaction and handover documentation

### 7.3 Material Quality Documentation
1. **Material Receiving Records**
   - Delivery receipts and shipping documentation
   - Material inspection and acceptance forms

   - Supplier certificates and test reports
   - Material traceability and lot tracking

2. **Material Testing Documentation**
   - Test procedures and protocols
   - Test results and data sheets
   - Calibration records for testing equipment
   - Sample identification and chain of custody
   - Test report summaries and conclusions

### 7.4 Installation Quality Records
1. **Installation Documentation**
   - Daily installation quality reports
   - Progress photos and visual documentation
   - Measurement and verification records
   - Installation sequence and method verification
   - Environmental condition monitoring during installation

2. **Inspection and Testing Records**
   - Pre-installation substrate inspection reports
   - In-process quality verification checkpoints
   - Final system testing and performance verification
   - Water testing and leak detection records
   - Warranty and performance documentation

### 7.5 Florida-Specific Quality Documentation
1. **Hurricane Resistance Documentation**
   - Wind uplift testing results and certifications
   - Impact resistance verification records
   - Fastener pullout testing documentation
   - System attachment and securement verification
   - Hurricane simulation testing records

2. **Climate Performance Documentation**
   - UV resistance testing and certification
   - Thermal cycling test results
   - Moisture resistance verification
   - Heat aging and weathering documentation

3. **Environmental Compliance Records**

   - Stormwater management compliance documentation

   - Air quality monitoring during installation

### 7.6 Nonconformance Documentation

   - Root cause analysis and investigation records

2. **Corrective Action Documentation**

2. **Customer Communication Records**

1. **Quality Metrics and KPI Documentation**

   - Trend analysis and performance reporting

2. **Quality Cost Documentation**

   - Prevention cost documentation and analysis

   - Failure cost analysis and reduction efforts
   - Quality investment return on investment analysis

### 7.9 Training and Competency Documentation
1. **Quality Training Records**
   - Quality training program documentation
   - Individual training records and certifications
   - Competency assessment and verification
   - Training effectiveness evaluation

2. **Competency Management**

### 7.10 Record Management and Retention
1. **Storage and Retrieval Systems**
   - Physical and electronic storage standards
   - Document organization and filing systems
   - Access control and security measures
   - Backup and disaster recovery procedures
   - Search and retrieval capabilities

2. **Retention and Disposal Management**
   - Record retention schedule development and maintenance

### 7.11 Audit and Review Documentation
1. **Internal Audit Documentation**
   - Audit planning and scheduling documentation
   - Audit checklists and procedures

   - Corrective action plans and implementation

2. **Management Review Documentation**
   - Management review meeting agendas and minutes
   - Quality system performance reports

   - Resource allocation and budget decisions
   - Strategic quality planning and goal setting

## 8. Quality Standards
- Documentation completeness: 100% of required quality records maintained
- Record accuracy: Zero errors in critical quality documentation
- Retention compliance: 100% compliance with retention requirements
- Accessibility: Quality records available within 24 hours of request
- Audit readiness: Complete audit trail for all quality activities
