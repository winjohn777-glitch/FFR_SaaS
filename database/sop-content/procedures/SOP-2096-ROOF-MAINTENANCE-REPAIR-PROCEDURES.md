# SOP-2096: Roof Maintenance and Repair Procedures

## 1. Purpose
Establish standardized procedures for routine roof maintenance and non-emergency repairs, ensuring system longevity, preventing costly failures, and maintaining warranty compliance while optimizing maintenance schedules for Florida's climate conditions.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all maintenance technicians, service managers, and field crews involved in routine roof maintenance, preventive care, and non-emergency repair services for residential and commercial roofing systems.

## 3. Definitions
- **Preventive Maintenance**: Scheduled activities to prevent roof system deterioration
- **Corrective Maintenance**: Repairs to address identified deficiencies
- **Condition Assessment**: Systematic evaluation of roof system condition
- **Service Life Extension**: Maintenance activities that extend roof system lifespan
- **Warranty Maintenance**: Required activities to maintain manufacturer warranties

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Service Manager**: Maintenance scheduling, customer communication, quality oversight
- **Lead Technician**: Work planning, quality control, safety compliance
- **Maintenance Technician**: Hands-on maintenance and repair work
- **Quality Inspector**: Condition assessment, documentation, recommendations
- **Customer Service Representative**: Communication, scheduling, follow-up

## 5. Prerequisites
- Current roof system documentation and warranty information
- Weather conditions suitable for maintenance work
- Customer access arrangements and notifications
- Safety equipment and materials ready
- Maintenance history review completed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Materials:
- Sealants and adhesives compatible with existing systems
- Replacement materials matching original specifications
- Cleaning agents and surface preparation materials
- Protective coatings and renewal systems
- Emergency repair materials

### Equipment:
- Inspection tools and measuring devices
- Cleaning equipment (pressure washers, brushes)
- Basic repair tools and application equipment
- Safety equipment and fall protection
- Documentation equipment (cameras, tablets)

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Pre-Maintenance Assessment
1. **Documentation Review**
   - Review roof system specifications and warranty
   - Check maintenance history and previous issues
   - Identify manufacturer requirements
   - Plan for warranty compliance activities
   - Note any special considerations

2. **Visual Inspection**
   - Conduct comprehensive roof system inspection
   - Identify areas requiring immediate attention
   - Document existing conditions with photos
   - Plan maintenance priorities and sequence
   - Assess safety requirements for work

### 7.2 Routine Maintenance Activities
1. **Drainage System Maintenance**
   - Clean all drains, gutters, and downspouts
   - Remove debris from drainage paths
   - Check drain strainers and overflow systems
   - Verify proper water flow and drainage
   - Document drainage performance

2. **Surface Cleaning**
   - Remove debris, leaves, and organic growth
   - Clean membrane surfaces per manufacturer requirements
   - Remove algae, mildew, and biological growth
   - Clean around equipment and penetrations
   - Restore proper surface appearance

3. **Sealant and Flashing Maintenance**
   - Inspect all sealant joints and flashings
   - Remove deteriorated sealants
   - Clean and prepare surfaces for re-sealing
   - Apply new sealants per specifications
   - Verify proper adhesion and coverage

### 7.3 Minor Repair Procedures
1. **Membrane Repairs**
   - Identify and mark damaged areas
   - Clean and prepare repair surfaces
   - Apply patches using compatible materials
   - Test repair integrity and adhesion
   - Document repair locations and methods

2. **Flashing Repairs**
   - Remove damaged flashing sections
   - Prepare substrate for new flashing
   - Install replacement flashing properly
   - Seal all joints and transitions
   - Verify water-shedding performance

3. **Fastener and Hardware Maintenance**
   - Inspect all visible fasteners and hardware
   - Tighten loose fasteners to specification
   - Replace damaged or corroded hardware
   - Apply sealant to exposed fasteners
   - Document hardware condition

### 7.4 Florida Climate-Specific Maintenance
1. **Hurricane Season Preparation**
   - Inspect and secure all loose materials
   - Check and tighten equipment attachments
   - Clear all debris that could become projectiles
   - Verify emergency drainage capacity
   - Document storm readiness condition

2. **UV Damage Assessment and Treatment**
   - Inspect for UV-related deterioration
   - Apply UV-protective coatings where needed
   - Check for membrane chalking or cracking
   - Plan for UV protection renewal
   - Monitor UV exposure effects over time

3. **Moisture and Humidity Management**
   - Check for condensation issues
   - Inspect vapor barrier integrity
   - Verify ventilation system operation
   - Monitor for mold and mildew growth
   - Address humidity-related problems

### 7.5 Equipment and Penetration Maintenance
1. **HVAC Equipment Maintenance**
   - Inspect equipment mounting and supports
   - Check for equipment-related damage
   - Clean around equipment bases
   - Verify proper drainage around equipment
   - Coordinate with HVAC maintenance schedules

2. **Penetration Maintenance**
   - Inspect all roof penetrations
   - Check pipe boots and flashing
   - Verify seal integrity around penetrations
   - Clean and maintain penetration areas
   - Plan for penetration upgrades if needed

### 7.6 Coating and Restoration Systems
1. **Coating Assessment**
   - Evaluate existing coating condition
   - Test coating adhesion and integrity
   - Plan for coating renewal or repair
   - Prepare surfaces for coating application
   - Apply restoration coatings as needed

2. **Surface Preparation for Coatings**
   - Clean surfaces thoroughly
   - Remove loose or failing coating material
   - Repair substrate defects
   - Prime surfaces where required
   - Apply coatings per manufacturer specifications

### 7.7 Documentation and Reporting
1. **Condition Documentation**
   - Take before and after photos
   - Document all maintenance activities performed
   - Note any concerns or recommendations
   - Update maintenance history records
   - Prepare detailed condition reports

2. **Customer Communication**
   - Provide immediate feedback on urgent issues
   - Prepare comprehensive maintenance reports
   - Recommend future maintenance activities
   - Discuss warranty compliance status
   - Schedule next maintenance visit

### 7.8 Quality Assurance
1. **Work Verification**
   - Inspect all completed maintenance work
   - Test repairs for effectiveness
   - Verify proper material application
   - Check for compliance with specifications
   - Document quality verification

2. **Performance Testing**
   - Test drainage system performance
   - Verify repair water resistance
   - Check coating application quality
   - Test sealant adhesion and coverage
   - Document test results

## 8. Quality Standards
- Maintenance completion: 100% of scheduled activities completed
- Repair effectiveness: All repairs must pass water testing
- Documentation accuracy: Complete and accurate maintenance records
- Customer satisfaction: Minimum 4.5/5 rating
- Warranty compliance: 100% compliance with manufacturer requirements

## 9. Safety Requirements
- Fall protection for all roof work
- Proper handling of cleaning chemicals
- Equipment safety during maintenance operations
- Weather monitoring during work
- Emergency procedures for maintenance incidents

## 10. Environmental Considerations

- Energy efficiency considerations in maintenance
- Protection of building surroundings during work
- Sustainable maintenance practices
