# SOP-2093: Commercial Flat Roof Installation

## 1. Purpose
Establish standardized procedures for commercial flat roof installation, ensuring proper drainage, membrane integrity, and long-term performance while meeting Florida building codes, energy efficiency requirements, and hurricane resistance standards.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all field crews, project managers, and specialized contractors involved in installing flat roofing systems for commercial buildings including office buildings, retail centers, warehouses, and industrial facilities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Flat Roof**: Roof with slope of 2% (1/4 inch per foot) or less
- **Membrane System**: Primary waterproofing layer (TPO, EPDM, Modified Bitumen, etc.)
- **Tapered Insulation**: Sloped insulation system creating positive drainage
- **Parapet Wall**: Vertical extension of building wall above roof level
- **Roof Drain**: Device for removing water from roof surface
- **Scupper**: Opening through parapet wall for overflow drainage

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Project Manager**: Overall coordination, scheduling, compliance oversight
- **Membrane Specialist**: Installation techniques, seaming, quality control
- **Insulation Installer**: Substrate preparation, insulation placement, vapor barriers
- **Sheet Metal Worker**: Flashing installation, drain connections, parapet capping
- **Quality Inspector**: Installation verification, testing, documentation

## 5. Prerequisites
- Building permits and structural approvals obtained
- Weather window suitable for membrane installation
- Roof deck prepared and inspected
- All materials delivered and inspected
- Safety systems and equipment in place

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Materials:
- Roof membrane system (TPO, EPDM, or specified)
- Insulation materials (polyiso, EPS, or specified)
- Vapor barrier materials
- Fasteners and adhesives
- Flashing materials
- Drainage components

### Equipment:
- Hot air welding equipment (for TPO)
- Membrane cutting and seaming tools
- Insulation cutting equipment
- Material hoists and conveyors
- Testing equipment for seams and membrane

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Roof Deck Preparation
1. **Deck Inspection**
   - Verify structural adequacy for planned loads
   - Check for level and proper slope
   - Identify and repair any deck defects
   - Verify attachment points for equipment
   - Document deck conditions with photos

2. **Surface Preparation**
   - Clean deck of all debris and contaminants
   - Verify deck is dry (moisture content <18%)
   - Install temporary protection if needed
   - Mark equipment locations and penetrations
   - Plan material staging areas

### 7.2 Vapor Barrier Installation
1. **Vapor Barrier Placement**
   - Install vapor barrier per manufacturer specifications
   - Lap joints minimum 6 inches
   - Seal all penetrations and transitions
   - Verify continuous coverage
   - Coordinate with insulation installation

2. **Quality Control**
   - Inspect for tears, punctures, or gaps
   - Verify proper overlap and sealing
   - Test adhesion where applicable
   - Document installation with photos
   - Address any deficiencies immediately

### 7.3 Insulation System Installation
1. **Tapered Insulation Design**
   - Install tapered system for positive drainage
   - Verify minimum 1/4 inch per foot slope
   - Plan drainage paths to drains and scuppers
   - Eliminate low spots and ponding areas
   - Document slope verification

2. **Insulation Installation**
   - Install insulation in proper sequence
   - Stagger joints between layers
   - Secure with appropriate fasteners
   - Cut precise fits around penetrations
   - Maintain continuous thermal barrier

3. **Cover Board Installation**
   - Install cover board over insulation
   - Provide smooth, stable surface for membrane
   - Secure with compatible fasteners
   - Verify proper joint treatment
   - Check for surface irregularities

### 7.4 Membrane Installation
1. **Membrane Layout**
   - Plan membrane layout to minimize seams
   - Orient seams parallel to drainage flow
   - Minimize cross-slope seams
   - Plan for thermal movement
   - Mark seam locations

2. **Membrane Placement**
   - Roll out membrane according to plan
   - Allow for thermal relaxation
   - Position for optimal seam welding
   - Avoid stretching or distortion
   - Protect from damage during installation

3. **Seam Welding (TPO Systems)**
   - Clean seam areas thoroughly
   - Set welding equipment to proper temperature
   - Weld seams with consistent speed and pressure
   - Test seam strength continuously
   - Repair any defective seams immediately

4. **Adhesive Application (EPDM Systems)**
   - Apply adhesive per manufacturer specifications
   - Allow proper flash-off time
   - Press membrane into adhesive firmly
   - Verify complete adhesion
   - Clean excess adhesive immediately

### 7.5 Flashing and Detail Work
1. **Parapet Wall Flashing**
   - Install base flashing minimum 8 inches high
   - Secure mechanically to wall structure
   - Install counterflashing with proper overlap
   - Seal all penetrations and joints
   - Verify proper water shedding

2. **Penetration Flashing**
   - Flash all roof penetrations properly
   - Use compatible materials and methods
   - Ensure watertight seals
   - Plan for thermal movement
   - Install proper drainage around penetrations

3. **Drain Installation**
   - Install drains at low points
   - Verify proper connection to drain lines
   - Install drain strainers and overflow protection
   - Test drainage capacity
   - Document drain locations and elevations

### 7.6 Florida Hurricane Compliance
1. **Wind Uplift Resistance**
   - Design system for Florida wind loads
   - Use enhanced fastening patterns
   - Install perimeter securement systems
   - Test system uplift resistance
   - Document compliance with FM or UL ratings

2. **Impact Resistance**
   - Install impact-resistant membrane systems
   - Protect equipment and penetrations
   - Plan for debris impact loading
   - Use hurricane-rated components
   - Document impact resistance compliance

3. **Drainage for Extreme Events**
   - Design for 100-year storm events
   - Install adequate primary and overflow drainage
   - Plan for rapid water removal
   - Size drains for extreme rainfall rates
   - Monitor performance during storms

### 7.7 Quality Testing and Verification
1. **Water Testing**
   - Perform flood testing of completed areas
   - Test all penetrations and details
   - Check drainage system performance
   - Document test results with photos
   - Address any identified leaks

2. **Seam Testing**
   - Test all membrane seams per standards
   - Use destructive and non-destructive methods
   - Document test locations and results
   - Repair any failed seams
   - Re-test repaired areas

3. **Final Inspection**
   - Conduct comprehensive system inspection
   - Verify all details and flashings
   - Check drainage and ponding
   - Document final conditions
   - Obtain required approvals

### 7.8 Energy Efficiency Optimization
1. **Cool Roof Systems**
   - Install high-reflectance membrane
   - Verify Solar Reflectance Index (SRI) values
   - Document energy code compliance
   - Plan for long-term reflectance maintenance
   - Consider ENERGY STAR qualification

2. **Thermal Performance**
   - Install continuous insulation systems
   - Eliminate thermal bridging
   - Verify R-value compliance
   - Document thermal performance
   - Plan for energy monitoring

## 8. Quality Standards
- Water infiltration: Zero leaks under test conditions
- Drainage: No ponding water after 48 hours
- Seam integrity: 100% seam testing compliance
- Wind resistance: Meet specified uplift ratings
- Energy performance: Achieve specified R-values and reflectance

## 9. Safety Requirements
- Fall protection systems for all roof work
- Hot work permits for membrane welding
- Proper ventilation for adhesive applications
- Material handling safety for large membrane sheets
- Weather monitoring for installation conditions

## 10. Environmental Considerations

- Proper disposal of packaging materials
- Energy-efficient system design
- Stormwater management integration
- Long-term sustainability considerations
