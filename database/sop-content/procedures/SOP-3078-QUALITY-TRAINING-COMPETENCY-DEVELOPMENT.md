# SOP-3078: Quality Training and Competency Development

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for quality training and competency development to ensure all personnel have the knowledge, skills, and abilities necessary to maintain high quality standards and continuously improve performance in roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality-related activities including quality control staff, field crews, supervisors, and management, covering initial training, ongoing development, and competency assessment programs.

## 3. Definitions
- **Competency**: Demonstrated ability to apply knowledge, skills, and behaviors
- **Training Matrix**: Systematic identification of training requirements by role
- **Competency Assessment**: Evaluation of individual capabilities and performance
- **Training Effectiveness**: Measurement of training impact on job performance
- **Continuous Learning**: Ongoing development and skill enhancement

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Training Manager**: Overall training program development, coordination, effectiveness monitoring
- **Quality Manager**: Quality training requirements definition, competency standards, assessment
- **Department Managers**: Training needs identification, resource allocation, performance monitoring
- **Subject Matter Experts**: Training content development, delivery, competency assessment
- **HR Manager**: Training record management, certification tracking, compliance monitoring

## 5. Prerequisites
- Job descriptions and competency requirements defined
- Training materials and resources developed
- Assessment criteria and methods established
- Training tracking and management systems operational
- Qualified trainers and subject matter experts identified

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Training Materials:
- Training curricula and lesson plans

- Assessment tools and evaluation forms

### Training Resources:
- Classroom and training facilities

- Certification and documentation materials

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Training Needs Assessment
1. **Job Role Analysis**
   - Quality responsibilities identification for each position
   - Required knowledge, skills, and abilities determination
   - Performance standards and expectations definition
   - Regulatory and certification requirements identification
   - Customer requirement and expectation integration

2. **Individual Competency Assessment**
   - Current skill and knowledge level evaluation
   - Performance gap identification
   - Development needs prioritization
   - Career advancement planning
   - Individual development plan creation

### 7.2 Training Program Development
1. **Curriculum Design and Development**
   - Learning objectives and outcomes definition
   - Content organization and sequencing
   - Training methods and delivery approaches
   - Assessment and evaluation criteria
   - Resource requirements and allocation

2. **Training Material Creation**
   - Technical content development and verification
   - Visual aids and presentation materials
   - Hands-on exercises and practical applications
   - Assessment tools and evaluation instruments
   - Reference materials and job aids

## 8. Quality Standards
- Training completion: 100% completion of required training within timeframes
- Competency achievement: All personnel demonstrate required competencies
- Training effectiveness: Measurable improvement in job performance
- Record accuracy: Complete and accurate training documentation
- Continuous improvement: Regular training program updates and enhancements
