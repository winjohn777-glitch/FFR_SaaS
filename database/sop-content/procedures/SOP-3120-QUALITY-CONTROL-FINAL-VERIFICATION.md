# SOP-3120: Quality Control Final Verification

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for final quality verification and acceptance to ensure all quality requirements are met, customer expectations are fulfilled, and project deliverables meet specified standards before final handover in Florida roofing projects.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in final quality verification, project completion assessment, customer acceptance, and quality sign-off across all Florida First Roofing project completion and handover activities.

## 3. Definitions
- **Final Verification**: Comprehensive quality assessment before project completion
- **Quality Acceptance**: Formal approval that quality requirements have been met
- **Project Handover**: Transfer of completed project to customer with documentation
- **Quality Sign-off**: Formal authorization that quality standards are achieved
- **Completion Checklist**: Systematic verification of all quality requirements
- **Quality Certification**: Official documentation of quality compliance
- **Customer Acceptance**: Customer formal approval of completed work quality

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Final verification oversight, quality certification, sign-off authority
- **Project Manager**: Completion coordination, customer interface, handover management
- **Quality Inspector**: Final inspection execution, documentation, verification
- **Technical Supervisor**: Technical compliance verification, system testing
- **Customer Representative**: Acceptance review, approval, feedback provision

## 5. Prerequisites
- Project completion and quality control activities finished
- All quality control checkpoints passed successfully
- Documentation and records completed and organized
- Customer notification and scheduling completed
- Final verification procedures and checklists prepared

## 7. Procedure

   - Verification scope and criteria definition
   - Resource allocation and team assignment
   - Schedule development and customer coordination

   - Quality standard and requirement review

2. **Verification Process Structure**
   - **Pre-Verification Review**: Documentation and readiness assessment
   - **Technical Verification**: System and component performance verification
   - **Quality Verification**: Quality standard and requirement compliance
   - **Safety Verification**: Safety system and compliance verification
   - **Customer Verification**: Customer walkthrough and acceptance

## 8. Quality Standards
- Verification completion: 100% completion of all verification requirements
- Customer satisfaction: 95% minimum customer satisfaction score
- Issue resolution: 100% resolution of identified issues before handover
- Documentation: 100% complete and accurate documentation package
- On-time completion: 95% on-time final verification and handover
