# SOP-3077: Non-Conformance Management and Corrective Action

## 1. Purpose
Establish systematic procedures for identifying, documenting, investigating, and correcting non-conformances while implementing effective corrective and preventive actions to prevent recurrence and continuously improve quality performance.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in quality control, project management, and operations who may identify non-conformances, and all managers responsible for implementing corrective and preventive actions across all company operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Non-Conformance**: Failure to meet specified requirements or quality standards
- **Corrective Action**: Action taken to eliminate the cause of detected non-conformance
- **Preventive Action**: Action taken to eliminate the cause of potential non-conformance
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Systematic investigation to identify underlying causes
- **Effectiveness Verification**: Confirmation that corrective actions achieved intended results

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Non-conformance system oversight, corrective action approval, effectiveness verification
- **Non-Conformance Coordinator**: Documentation management, investigation coordination, tracking
- **Department Manager**: Root cause analysis, corrective action implementation, resource allocation
- **Quality Inspector**: Non-conformance identification, initial documentation, verification
- **Project Manager**: Project-specific non-conformance response, customer communication

## 5. Prerequisites
- Non-conformance identification and reporting procedures established
- Root cause analysis tools and techniques defined
- Corrective action procedures and approval processes ready
- Documentation and tracking systems operational
- Personnel training on non-conformance management completed

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Non-Conformance Identification and Reporting
1. **Immediate Identification Requirements**
   - Any deviation from specifications or requirements
   - Customer complaints or dissatisfaction
   - Internal audit findings
   - Supplier quality issues
   - Safety or environmental incidents

2. **Reporting Procedures**
   - Immediate verbal notification to supervisor
   - Written non-conformance report within 24 hours
   - Photo documentation of conditions
   - Containment actions implemented immediately
   - Stakeholder notification as required

### 7.2 Non-Conformance Documentation
1. **Initial Documentation Requirements**
   - Clear description of non-conformance condition
   - Location, time, and circumstances of occurrence
   - Personnel involved and witnesses
   - Immediate containment actions taken
   - Potential impact assessment

2. **Evidence Collection**
   - Photographic documentation
   - Physical samples when applicable
   - Witness statements and interviews
   - Related documentation and records
   - Environmental condition documentation

### 7.3 Immediate Containment Actions
1. **Safety and Environmental Protection**
   - Immediate safety hazard mitigation
   - Environmental impact prevention
   - Personnel protection and notification
   - Area isolation or restriction as needed
   - Emergency response activation if required

2. **Product and Service Containment**
   - Segregation of non-conforming materials
   - Work stoppage to prevent continuation
   - Customer notification if product delivered
   - Inventory identification and control
   - Service suspension until resolution

### 7.4 Risk Assessment and Prioritization
1. **Impact Assessment**
   - **High Priority**: Safety risk, customer impact, regulatory violation
   - **Medium Priority**: Quality impact, schedule delay, cost increase
   - **Low Priority**: Minor deviation, minimal impact, easily corrected
   - Customer satisfaction impact evaluation
   - Business risk and reputation impact

2. **Response Timeline Establishment**
   - High priority: Immediate response, 24-hour investigation
   - Medium priority: 48-hour response, 1-week investigation
   - Low priority: 1-week response, 2-week investigation
   - Resource allocation based on priority
   - Communication schedule establishment

### 7.5 Root Cause Analysis
1. **Investigation Team Formation**
   - Team leader assignment based on expertise
   - Cross-functional team member selection
   - Subject matter expert involvement
   - Customer representative inclusion when appropriate
   - External expert consultation if needed

2. **Root Cause Analysis Techniques**
   - **5-Why Analysis**: Progressive questioning to reach root cause
   - **Fishbone Diagram**: Systematic cause categorization
   - **Fault Tree Analysis**: Logical analysis of failure modes
   - **Statistical Analysis**: Data-driven cause identification
   - **Timeline Analysis**: Sequence of events investigation

### 7.6 Florida-Specific Non-Conformance Considerations
1. **Climate-Related Non-Conformances**
   - Hurricane damage and performance failures
   - UV degradation and weathering issues
   - Moisture intrusion and mold problems
   - Thermal expansion and contraction failures
   - Corrosion and salt air damage

2. **Regulatory Compliance Issues**
   - Florida Building Code violations
   - Environmental regulation non-compliance
   - Safety regulation violations
   - Permit and inspection failures
   - Local ordinance non-compliance

### 7.7 Corrective Action Development
1. **Solution Development Process**
   - Multiple solution option generation
   - Cost-benefit analysis of alternatives
   - Implementation feasibility assessment
   - Risk evaluation of proposed solutions
   - Customer and stakeholder input consideration

2. **Corrective Action Plan Components**
   - Specific actions to address root causes
   - Implementation timeline and milestones
   - Resource requirements and assignments
   - Success measurement criteria
   - Monitoring and verification procedures

### 7.8 Corrective Action Implementation
1. **Implementation Management**
   - Action plan approval and authorization
   - Resource allocation and coordination
   - Progress monitoring and reporting
   - Obstacle identification and resolution
   - Timeline adjustment and communication

2. **Change Management**
   - Procedure and process updates
   - Personnel training and communication
   - System and technology modifications
   - Supplier and partner notification
   - Customer communication and updates

### 7.9 Effectiveness Verification
1. **Implementation Verification**
   - Action completion confirmation
   - Quality and compliance verification
   - Performance measurement and monitoring
   - Customer satisfaction assessment
   - Stakeholder feedback collection

2. **Long-term Effectiveness Monitoring**
   - Performance trend monitoring
   - Recurrence prevention verification
   - System improvement measurement
   - Customer satisfaction tracking
   - Cost and benefit analysis

### 7.10 Preventive Action Implementation
1. **Preventive Action Identification**
   - Trend analysis and pattern recognition
   - Potential problem identification
   - System vulnerability assessment
   - Best practice implementation
   - Risk mitigation measure development

2. **System Improvement Integration**
   - Process improvement implementation
   - Training and competency enhancement
   - Technology and tool upgrades
   - Supplier and partner development
   - Customer communication improvement

### 7.11 Documentation and Communication
1. **Complete Documentation Requirements**
   - Non-conformance report and investigation
   - Root cause analysis documentation
   - Corrective action plan and implementation
   - Effectiveness verification results
   - Lesson learned and improvement recommendations

2. **Communication and Sharing**
   - Internal communication and training
   - Customer notification and updates
   - Supplier and partner communication
   - Industry best practice sharing
   - Regulatory reporting as required

### 7.12 Non-Conformance Trend Analysis
1. **Data Collection and Analysis**
   - Non-conformance frequency and pattern analysis
   - Root cause trend identification
   - Cost impact analysis and trending
   - Customer impact assessment
   - System performance improvement measurement

2. **System Improvement Planning**
   - Systematic improvement opportunity identification
   - Resource allocation for improvement initiatives
   - Priority setting for improvement projects
   - Success measurement and monitoring
   - Continuous improvement integration

## 8. Quality Standards
- Response time: Immediate containment, investigation within priority timelines
- Documentation completeness: 100% complete documentation for all non-conformances
- Root cause identification: Systematic root cause analysis for all significant issues
- Corrective action effectiveness: >95% effectiveness in preventing recurrence
- Customer satisfaction: Prompt resolution and communication
