# SOP-2098: Roof Inspection and Assessment

## 1. Purpose
Establish standardized procedures for comprehensive roof inspections and condition assessments, ensuring accurate evaluation of roof system performance, identification of deficiencies, and development of appropriate maintenance or replacement recommendations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all inspectors, project managers, and technical staff involved in roof system inspections for insurance claims, maintenance planning, pre-purchase evaluations, and warranty assessments for all types of roofing systems.

## 3. Definitions
- **Comprehensive Inspection**: Detailed evaluation of all roof system components
- **Condition Assessment**: Analysis of current system condition and remaining service life
- **Deficiency Documentation**: Recording of system defects, damage, or wear
- **Moisture Survey**: Testing for moisture intrusion and trapped moisture
- **Performance Evaluation**: Assessment of system performance relative to design intent

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Lead Inspector**: Overall inspection coordination, technical evaluation, report preparation
- **Inspector Technician**: Hands-on inspection work, measurement, documentation
- **Moisture Specialist**: Moisture detection and assessment, thermal imaging
- **Structural Assessor**: Structural evaluation, load assessment, safety evaluation
- **Report Coordinator**: Documentation compilation, customer communication, recommendations

## 5. Prerequisites
- Current weather conditions suitable for safe inspection
- Customer access arrangements and notifications completed
- Safety equipment and inspection tools ready
- Previous inspection reports and building documentation available
- Insurance or warranty documentation reviewed

## 6. Materials and Equipment
### Inspection Tools:
- Moisture detection equipment (infrared camera, electrical meters)
- Measuring tools (tape measures, thickness gauges)
- Core cutting tools for sampling
- Digital cameras and documentation equipment
- Safety equipment and fall protection

### Documentation Materials:
- Inspection forms and checklists
- Digital tablets or mobile documentation systems
- Sample collection containers
- Marking materials for damage identification
- Report preparation software

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Pre-Inspection Planning
1. **Document Review**
   - Review building plans and roof specifications
   - Check previous inspection reports and maintenance records
   - Verify roof system type and age
   - Identify known problem areas
   - Plan inspection approach and sequence

2. **Safety Planning**
   - Assess roof access safety
   - Plan fall protection requirements
   - Check weather conditions and forecast
   - Identify hazards and special safety needs
   - Establish emergency procedures

### 7.2 Exterior Visual Inspection
1. **Overall System Assessment**
   - Evaluate general roof condition from ground level
   - Identify obvious damage or deficiencies
   - Assess drainage patterns and performance
   - Check building envelope integration
   - Document overall system appearance

2. **Membrane and Surface Inspection**
   - Inspect membrane for splits, punctures, or deterioration
   - Check for blistering, ridging, or surface defects
   - Evaluate UV damage and weathering
   - Assess granule loss on shingle systems
   - Document surface condition with photos

3. **Flashing and Detail Inspection**
   - Inspect all flashings for damage or deterioration
   - Check sealant condition at all penetrations
   - Evaluate parapet walls and coping systems
   - Inspect edge details and perimeter conditions
   - Assess equipment integration and flashing

### 7.3 Drainage System Evaluation
1. **Drainage Performance**
   - Check all drains for proper function
   - Inspect gutters and downspouts
   - Evaluate slope and drainage patterns
   - Identify ponding or standing water areas
   - Test drainage capacity where possible

2. **Structural Drainage Assessment**
   - Check for structural deflection affecting drainage
   - Evaluate overflow and emergency drainage
   - Inspect drain connections and attachments
   - Assess potential for ice dam formation
   - Document drainage deficiencies

### 7.4 Moisture Detection and Assessment
1. **Moisture Survey Techniques**
   - Use infrared thermography for moisture detection
   - Employ electrical resistance moisture meters
   - Perform core sampling in suspected wet areas
   - Document moisture levels and extent
   - Identify sources of moisture intrusion

2. **Moisture Damage Evaluation**
   - Assess extent of moisture damage to building components
   - Evaluate insulation moisture content and condition
   - Check for mold or biological growth
   - Assess structural damage from moisture
   - Document moisture-related deficiencies

### 7.5 Structural Assessment
1. **Structural Integrity Evaluation**
   - Inspect roof deck condition and stability
   - Check for structural member damage or deterioration
   - Assess adequacy for current and planned loads
   - Evaluate connection integrity and fastening
   - Document structural concerns

2. **Load Assessment**
   - Verify structural capacity for equipment loads
   - Assess wind uplift resistance
   - Check for overloading conditions
   - Evaluate seismic resistance where applicable
   - Document load-related issues

### 7.6 Florida-Specific Inspection Considerations
1. **Hurricane Damage Assessment**
   - Inspect for wind damage and uplift
   - Check for impact damage from debris
   - Evaluate hurricane clip and fastener performance
   - Assess pressure differential damage
   - Document storm-related deficiencies

2. **Climate-Related Deterioration**
   - Evaluate UV damage and thermal cycling effects
   - Check for corrosion from salt air exposure
   - Assess thermal expansion and contraction damage
   - Inspect for biological growth and algae
   - Document climate-related wear patterns

3. **Code Compliance Verification**
   - Verify compliance with current Florida Building Code
   - Check hurricane resistance requirements
   - Assess fire rating compliance
   - Evaluate accessibility and safety requirements
   - Document code compliance issues

### 7.7 Interior Inspection
1. **Interior Condition Assessment**
   - Inspect interior spaces for water damage
   - Check ceilings for staining or deterioration
   - Evaluate HVAC system integration
   - Assess insulation condition and performance
   - Document interior moisture or damage issues

2. **Building Systems Integration**
   - Evaluate roof penetrations from interior
   - Check coordination with building systems
   - Assess impact on building performance
   - Verify proper sealing and integration
   - Document integration issues

### 7.8 Documentation and Reporting
1. **Photographic Documentation**
   - Take comprehensive photos of all conditions
   - Document deficiencies with close-up views
   - Include overall system photos for context
   - Mark photos with location and description
   - Organize photos by inspection area

2. **Condition Assessment Report**
   - Prepare detailed written assessment
   - Include condition ratings for all components
   - Provide repair and maintenance recommendations
   - Estimate remaining service life
   - Include cost estimates for recommended work

### 7.9 Recommendations and Follow-up
1. **Repair Prioritization**
   - Classify repairs by urgency and importance
   - Provide immediate, short-term, and long-term recommendations
   - Estimate costs for recommended repairs
   - Suggest maintenance schedules
   - Plan for future monitoring and assessment

2. **Customer Communication**
   - Review findings with customer immediately
   - Explain technical issues in understandable terms
   - Discuss options and alternatives
   - Provide written recommendations
   - Schedule follow-up as needed

## 8. Quality Standards
- Inspection completeness: 100% of accessible areas inspected
- Documentation accuracy: All deficiencies documented with photos
- Moisture detection: All moisture intrusion identified and located
- Report quality: Professional, comprehensive, and actionable reports
- Customer satisfaction: Clear communication and useful recommendations
