# SOP-4071: Customer Relationship Management System Operation

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for operating and managing the Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system to optimize customer interactions, track relationships, manage sales processes, and deliver exceptional customer service in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel involved in customer interactions, sales activities, service delivery, and relationship management using the CRM system across all Florida First Roofing customer-facing operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Customer Relationship Management (CRM)**: System and strategy for managing customer interactions
- **Lead Management**: Process of capturing, tracking, and nurturing potential customers
- **Contact Management**: Organization and tracking of customer contact information
- **Opportunity Management**: Tracking and management of sales opportunities
- **Customer Journey**: Complete experience of customer interactions with the company
- **Customer Lifecycle**: Stages of customer relationship from prospect to advocate
- **Customer Touchpoint**: Any interaction between customer and company

## 4. Responsibilities
- **CRM Administrator**: System configuration, user management, data integrity, reporting
- **Sales Manager**: Sales process management, opportunity tracking, performance monitoring
- **Customer Service Manager**: Service case management, customer satisfaction tracking
- **Marketing Manager**: Lead management, campaign tracking, customer segmentation
- **All Users**: Data entry, customer interaction recording, system compliance

## 5. Prerequisites
- CRM system installed and configured
- User accounts and permissions established
- Data migration and system integration completed
- User training and certification completed
- Standard operating procedures and workflows defined
