# SOP-3081: Quality Cost Management and Analysis

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for identifying, measuring, analyzing, and managing quality-related costs to optimize quality investments, reduce waste, and improve overall business profitability while maintaining superior quality standards.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all quality managers, financial analysts, project managers, and department heads involved in quality cost identification, measurement, analysis, and cost reduction initiatives across all Florida First Roofing operations.

## 3. Definitions
- **Cost of Quality (COQ)**: Total cost of achieving quality objectives and dealing with quality failures
- **Prevention Costs**: Costs incurred to prevent quality defects and failures
- **Appraisal Costs**: Costs of measuring, evaluating, and auditing quality performance
- **Internal Failure Costs**: Costs from defects found before delivery to customers
- **External Failure Costs**: Costs from defects found after delivery to customers
- **Quality Investment**: Strategic spending on quality improvement initiatives
- **Quality Return on Investment (QROI)**: Financial return from quality investments

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Quality Manager**: Cost data collection, analysis coordination, improvement planning
- **Financial Manager**: Cost accounting support, financial analysis, ROI calculation
- **Project Manager**: Project-specific cost tracking, cost control implementation
- **Operations Manager**: Process cost optimization, efficiency improvement
- **Senior Management**: Strategic cost decisions, investment approval, policy development

## 5. Prerequisites
- Quality cost accounting systems established
- Cost classification and coding structure defined
- Data collection and reporting systems operational
- Analysis tools and methodologies ready
- Cost reduction targets and goals established

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Quality Cost Framework Development
1. **Cost of Quality Model**
   - **Prevention Costs**: Training, planning, quality system development
   - **Appraisal Costs**: Inspection, testing, audit, measurement
   - **Internal Failure**: Rework, scrap, reinspection, downtime
   - **External Failure**: Warranty, returns, customer complaints, reputation
   - **Opportunity Costs**: Lost sales, market share, customer loyalty

2. **Cost Classification System**
   - Direct quality costs identification
   - Indirect quality costs allocation
   - Hidden quality costs estimation
   - Quality-related overhead distribution
   - Opportunity cost quantification

### 7.2 Florida Roofing Quality Cost Categories

## 8. Quality Standards
- Cost data accuracy: 95% minimum accuracy in cost allocation
- Reporting timeliness: Monthly reports within 5 days of month-end
- Analysis completion: Quarterly analysis within 10 days
- Improvement tracking: 100% tracking of cost reduction initiatives
- ROI achievement: Positive ROI on all quality investments
