# SOP-4072: Customer Data Management and Privacy Protection

## 1. Purpose
Establish comprehensive procedures for managing customer data, protecting privacy, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations, and maintaining customer trust through responsible data handling practices in Florida roofing operations.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all personnel who collect, process, store, or access customer data across all Florida First Roofing operations, systems, and customer interaction touchpoints.

## 3. Definitions
- **Customer Data**: Any information that identifies or relates to a customer or prospect
- **Personal Information (PI)**: Data that can identify a specific individual
- **Personally Identifiable Information (PII)**: Data that can identify, contact, or locate an individual
- **Data Privacy**: Protection of personal information from unauthorized access or use
- **Data Security**: Technical and administrative safeguards protecting data integrity
- **Data Retention**: Policies governing how long data is kept and when it's disposed
- **Consent Management**: Process of obtaining and managing customer data consent

## 4. Responsibilities
- **Data Protection Officer**: Privacy program oversight, compliance monitoring, policy development
- **IT Manager**: Technical security implementation, access controls, system protection
- **Customer Service Manager**: Data collection procedures, staff training, customer communication
- **Legal Counsel**: Regulatory compliance, policy review, breach response coordination
- **All Employees**: Data protection compliance, secure handling, incident reporting

## 5. Prerequisites
- Data protection policies and procedures established
- Privacy impact assessments completed
- Technical security controls implemented
- Staff training on data protection completed
- Incident response procedures developed

## 7. Procedure

### 7.1 Data Protection Framework
1. **Privacy Program Structure**
   - Data protection governance and oversight
   - Privacy policy development and maintenance
   - Compliance monitoring and assessment
   - Employee training and awareness programs
   - Customer communication and transparency

2. **Regulatory Compliance**
   - **Florida Personal Information Protection Act**: State privacy requirements
   - **Federal Trade Commission (FTC)**: Consumer protection regulations
   - **Industry Standards**: Roofing industry best practices
   - **Insurance Requirements**: Insurance and bonding privacy requirements
   - **Customer Contractual Requirements**: Customer-specific privacy obligations

### 7.2 Customer Data Classification and Inventory
1. **Data Type Classification**
   - **Basic Contact Information**: Names, addresses, phone numbers, emails
   - **Financial Information**: Payment data, credit information, billing details
   - **Property Information**: Property details, inspection data, service history
   - **Communication Records**: Email, call logs, meeting notes, preferences
   - **Sensitive Information**: Insurance claims, legal matters, emergency contacts

2. **Data Inventory and Mapping**
   - Data source identification and cataloging
   - Data flow mapping and system tracking
   - Data storage location and access documentation
   - Data sharing and third-party transfer tracking
   - Data retention schedule and disposal planning

### 7.3 Data Collection and Consent Management
1. **Lawful Data Collection**
   - Purpose limitation and necessity principles
   - Transparent collection notice and disclosure
   - Informed consent acquisition and documentation
   - Opt-in and opt-out mechanism provision
   - Special category data protection measures

2. **Consent Management Process**
   - Clear and specific consent request language
   - Granular consent option provision
   - Consent withdrawal mechanism and process
   - Consent record keeping and documentation
   - Consent renewal and refresh procedures

### 7.4 Data Access and Authorization
1. **Access Control Framework**
   - Role-based access control implementation
   - Principle of least privilege application
   - Regular access review and certification
   - Privileged account monitoring and control
   - Third-party access management and oversight

2. **Authentication and Authorization**
   - Multi-factor authentication requirements
   - Strong password policy and enforcement
   - User account lifecycle management
   - Session management and timeout controls
   - Administrative access logging and monitoring

### 7.5 Data Security and Protection
1. **Technical Safeguards**
   - Data encryption at rest and in transit
   - Network security and firewall protection
   - Intrusion detection and prevention systems
   - Vulnerability scanning and patch management
   - Secure backup and disaster recovery

2. **Administrative Safeguards**
   - Security policy and procedure development
   - Employee security training and awareness
   - Incident response and breach procedures
   - Vendor security assessment and management
   - Regular security audit and assessment

### 7.6 Data Quality and Accuracy
1. **Data Quality Management**
   - Data validation and verification procedures
   - Error detection and correction processes
   - Data cleansing and standardization
   - Duplicate detection and removal
   - Data quality monitoring and reporting

2. **Customer Data Rights**
   - Data access request processing
   - Data correction and update procedures
   - Data portability and export services
   - Data deletion and erasure procedures
   - Customer communication and notification

### 7.7 Data Sharing and Third-Party Management
1. **Third-Party Data Sharing**
   - Legitimate business purpose verification
   - Data sharing agreement and contract requirements
   - Third-party security assessment and validation
   - Data transfer monitoring and oversight
   - Customer notification and consent requirements

2. **Vendor Management**
   - Privacy and security requirement inclusion
   - Due diligence and risk assessment
   - Contract terms and compliance monitoring
   - Performance review and audit procedures
   - Incident coordination and response

### 7.8 Data Retention and Disposal
1. **Retention Policy and Procedures**
   - Legal and business requirement assessment
   - Retention schedule development and implementation
   - Automated retention and disposal systems
   - Legal hold and litigation support procedures
   - Customer request accommodation

2. **Secure Data Disposal**
   - Data destruction method and verification
   - Certificate of destruction documentation
   - Media sanitization and physical destruction
   - Electronic data wiping and verification
   - Disposal audit trail and documentation

### 7.9 Privacy Incident Response
1. **Incident Detection and Assessment**
   - Incident identification and categorization
   - Risk assessment and impact evaluation
   - Regulatory notification requirement determination
   - Customer notification requirement assessment
   - Containment and mitigation action planning

2. **Incident Response Process**
   - Immediate containment and stabilization
   - Forensic investigation and evidence collection
   - Regulatory notification and reporting
   - Customer notification and communication
   - Remediation and prevention planning

### 7.10 Customer Communication and Transparency
1. **Privacy Notice and Communication**
   - Clear and understandable privacy notice
   - Collection purpose and use explanation
   - Data sharing and transfer disclosure
   - Customer rights and choice explanation

2. **Ongoing Customer Engagement**
   - Regular privacy communication and updates
   - Customer education and awareness programs

   - New employee orientation and onboarding
   - Contractor and vendor training requirements

2. **Compliance Monitoring and Enforcement**

   - Policy violation investigation and response
   - Disciplinary action and corrective measures

### 7.12 Privacy Program Monitoring and Improvement
1. **Compliance Monitoring**

2. **Continuous Improvement**
