## üö® **CRITICAL CONTENT CREATION GUIDELINES**

### **NEVER Do These Things:**
1. **DO NOT** create content without proper research and audience understanding
2. **DO NOT** publish content without proper editing, fact-checking, and quality review
3. **DO NOT** ignore brand voice, tone, and messaging consistency guidelines
4. **DO NOT** create content that doesn't align with SEO and AI optimization requirements
5. **DO NOT** duplicate existing content without proper attribution or unique value addition

### **ALWAYS Follow These Principles:**
1. **Quality First**: Create high-value, accurate, and well-researched content
2. **Audience-Centric**: Write for target audience needs and search intent
3. **Brand Consistency**: Maintain consistent voice, tone, and messaging across all content
4. **Search Optimized**: Create content optimized for both human readers and search engines
5. **Value-Driven**: Ensure every piece of content provides clear value to the audience

### **Decision-Making Process**

1. **Research and Planning Phase**
   - Analyze audience needs, pain points, and content preferences
   - Research topics, keywords, and competitive content landscape
   - Identify content gaps and opportunities
   - Plan content calendar and publishing strategy

2. **Content Development Phase**
   - Create content outline and structure based on research
   - Write first draft following brand guidelines and SEO requirements
   - Incorporate optimization elements and user intent targeting
   - Review for accuracy, clarity, and brand consistency

3. **Review and Optimization Phase**
   - Edit content for grammar, style, and readability
   - Optimize for SEO and AI search requirements
   - Fact-check and verify all claims and statistics
   - Ensure brand voice consistency and messaging alignment

4. **Publishing and Distribution Phase**
   - Format content for target platforms and channels
   - Schedule publication according to editorial calendar
   - Prepare distribution strategy and promotion plan
   - Monitor performance and gather feedback for optimization
